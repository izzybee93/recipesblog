---
title: "Curried chickpea mini burgers"
categories: mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/curriedchickpeaminiburgers.jpeg
recipe:
  servings: serves 2
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 400g can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
    * 40g of Panko breadcrumbs
    * 40g cornflour
    * ½ onion, finely chopped
    * ½ bell pepper, finely chopped
    * 10g fresh coriander, chopped
    * ½ tsp salt
    * ½ tsp garlic powder
    * 1 tbsp curry powder
    * 80ml water
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Preheat the oven to 180C. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
    2. Mash the chickpeas in a large bowl with a fork.
    3. Add the breadcrumbs, cornstarch, onion, bell pepper, coriander, salt, garlic powder, and curry powder to the mashed chickpeas. Stir to combine. Add the water and mix until a dough comes together.
    4. Divide and shape into 6 small burgers. Brush with olive oil on both sides. Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway.
---
