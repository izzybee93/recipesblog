---
title: Crackers
categories: bread snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crackers.jpeg"
recipe:
  servings: makes ~50 crackers
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 185g plain flour
    * ¼ teaspoon baking powder
    * 30g nutritional yeast (optional)
    * 2 teaspoons Italian herbs
    * ¾ teaspoon salt
    * ½ teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
    * ¼ teaspoon black pepper (optional)
    * 115ml water
    * ½ tbsp olive oil
  directions_markdown: "1. Preheat the oven to 190°C. Line two large baking trays
    with parchment paper.\n2. In a medium bowl, add the flour, baking powder, nutritional
    yeast, herbs, salt, garlic powder and black pepper. Mix well to combine. Add the
    water and olive oil and mix until a dough forms. \n3. Transfer the dough to a
    lightly floured surface. Roll out as thin as possible (about 2mm thick). \n4.
    Using a pizza cutter, cut the dough into a grid pattern. Transfer each cracker
    to the baking trays, leaving space intebween the crackers. Use a fork to prick
    each cracker 3 times.\n5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the crackers are golden
    brown around the edges. Let cool completely in the tray. Store in an air-tight
    container for up to 1 week."
