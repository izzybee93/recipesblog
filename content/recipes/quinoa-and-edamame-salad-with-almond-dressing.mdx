---
title: "Quinoa and edamame salad with almond dressing"
date: "2026-05-07"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/quinoa-and-edamame-salad-with-almond-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 80g quinoa
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 100g red cabbage, shredded
  - 1 tsp lime juice
  - 'Pinch sea salt, to taste'
  - '150g edamame, cooked'
  - '1 tbsp almonds, roughly chopped'
  - 'Small handful coriander, chopped'
  - '½ avocado, chopped'
  - Almond dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa. Stir in the turmeric and ground black pepper.
  - In a large bowl, add the cabbage, lime juice and salt. Massage for 1 minute then set aside.
  - Make the almond dressing.
  - Add the quinoa, edamame, almonds and coriander to the cabbage and toss to combine. Add the almond dressing and mix again. Add the avocado and gently mix.
