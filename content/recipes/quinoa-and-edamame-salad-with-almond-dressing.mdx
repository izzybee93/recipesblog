---
title: "Quinoa and edamame salad with almond dressing"
date: "2026-05-07"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/quinoa-and-edamame-salad-with-almond-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 60g quinoa
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - '¼ small red cabbage, shredded'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - '150g edamame, cooked'
  - '1 tbsp almonds, roughly chopped'
  - 'Small handful coriander, chopped'
  - '½ avocado, sliced'
  - Almond dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa. Stir in the turmeric.
  - In a large bowl, add the red cabbage and lime juice. Massage briefly then set aside.
  - Add the quinoa, cucumber, edamame, almonds and coriander to the bowl and toss to combine.
  - Make the almond dressing. Pour over the salad and toss to combine. Add the avocado and gently mix.
