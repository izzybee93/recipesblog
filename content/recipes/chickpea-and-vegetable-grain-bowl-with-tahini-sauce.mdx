title: Chickpea and vegetable grain bowl with tahini sauce

featured_image: /images/recipes/chickpea-and-vegetable-grain-bowl-with-tahini-sauce.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '100g rice or quinoa, rinsed'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 garlic clove, crushed'
  - '## Any 2 veggies:'
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 2 medium carrots, chopped
  - 1 large courgette, chopped
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 200g mushrooms, sliced
  - 150g baby corn, chopped
  - ¼ broccoli, chopped into florets
  - ¼ cauliflower, chopped into florets
  - '## Sauce'
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - Tahini sauce (see recipe)
directions:
  - Cook the rice or quinoa.
  - >-
    In a frying pan, add the chickpeas and spices. Toss together and cook on a
    medium-high heat until slightly crispy.
  - >-
    In another frying pan, heat the sesame oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for
    1 minute. Add the veggies and cook for 10 minutes. Add the soy sauce and a splash of water as needed.
    Continue to cook for 5-10 minutes until softened.
  - >-
    Divide the rice or quinoa between 2 bowls. Add the veggies and chickpeas.
    Serve with a drizzle of tahini sauce.
