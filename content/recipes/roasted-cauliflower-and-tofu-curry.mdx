---
title: "Roasted cauliflower and tofu curry"
date: "2026-03-22"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-cauliflower-and-tofu-curry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - '¼ large cauliflower, chopped into florets'
  - 2½ tbsp olive oil
  - '200g firm tofu, cubed'
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, peeled and minced'
  - '15g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves separated'
  - 1 tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 250ml vegetable stock
  - 80g spinach
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 100g silken tofu cream (see recipe)
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the cauliflower, drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes until golden and slightly crisp.
  - Cook the rice.
  - In a large pan, heat ½ tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning to brown on all sides. Set aside.
  - In the same pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Stir in the garlic, ginger and finely chopped coriander stalks and cook for 2 minutes.
  - Stir in the turmeric, cumin, coriander, chilli flakes and salt. Cook for 1 minute, then add the tomato paste and cook for another minute. Pour in the vegetable stock and stir.
  - Add the roasted cauliflower and tofu. Simmer for 5 minutes to allow the flavours to combine.
  - Make the silken tofu cream.
  - Add the spinach and stir through until wilted. Remove from the heat and stir in the lime juice, tofu cream and coriander.
  - Serve with the rice.
