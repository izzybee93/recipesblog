---
title: "Chocolate pots"
date: "2026-03-31"
categories:
  - desserts
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-pots.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - 50g dark chocolate
  - '150g silken tofu, drained'
  - 1 tbsp maple syrup
  - Pinch sea salt
  - ½ tsp cacao nibs
  - Handful raspberries
directions:
  - Melt the chocolate in a glass bowl over a pan of boiling water. Set aside for 10 minutes to cool.
  - In a food processor, add the tofu, maple syrup and salt. Process until smooth. Add the melted chocolate and blend until well combined.
  - Divide the mousse into 3 small glasses and top with cacoa nibs and raspberries. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
