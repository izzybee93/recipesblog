---
title: "Creamy tofu sauce"
date: "2026-02-15"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-tofu-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 150g silken tofu
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - ½ tsp miso paste (optional)
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Add the tofu, olive oil, nutritional yeast, garlic, sea salt and miso paste to a blender and blend until smooth.
  - Heat a large pan over medium heat. Gently heat the sauce for 2 minutes - do not allow the sauce to boil or the tofu may split. 
  - Stir in the lemon and ground black pepper. Gently cook for 1-2 more minutes, adding a splash of water (or pasta water) as needed until the sauce reached the desired consistency.
---
