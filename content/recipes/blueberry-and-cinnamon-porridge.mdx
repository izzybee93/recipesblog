---
title: Blueberry and cinnamon porridge
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.263Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/blueberry-and-cinnamon-porridge.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 75g fresh or frozen blueberries
  - ½ tsp cinnamon
  - 1 tsp almond butter
directions:
  - >-
    Add the oats, milk, chia seeds and blueberries to the pan and cook for a few minutes on
    a medium heat, stirring frequently until the juices start to release. Stir
    in the cinnamon.
  - Serve topped with a swirl of almond butter and more blueberries.
