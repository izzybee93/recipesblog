---
title: "Chocolate and almond ganache"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-and-almond-ganache.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1 loaf cake"
ingredients:
  - '4 Medjool dates, chopped'
  - 60ml soya milk
  - 65g dark chocolate
  - 1½ tbsp almond butter
  - 1 tsp cocoa powder
  - ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Add the dates, soya milk, chocolate and almond butter to a small pan. Bring over medium-low heat and stir until the chocolate melts and forms a shiny chocolate mixture around the dates.
  - Remove from the heat, cover and set aside for 2 minutes to soften.
  - Transfer to a food processor and add the cocoa powder, vanilla extract and salt. Process for a few minutes, scraping down the sides as needed, until the mixture is shiny and smooth. Add a few more tbsp milk if needed, until smooth and spreadable.
  - Transfer to the fridge for 5-10 minutes before decorating your cake.
