---
title: "Mint chocolate cookies"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mint-chocolate-cookies.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12-16"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  - 3 tbsp water
  - 145g plain flour
  - 35g cocoa powder
  - ½ tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - 100g plant butter
  - 85g caster sugar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - ¼ tsp peppermint extract
  - '50g dark chocolate, chopped'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray with baking paper.
  - Whisk together the flaxseed and water in a small bowl and set aside for 15 minutes to thicken.
  - In a large bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt.
  - In a medium bowl, beat together the butter and sugar until light and creamy. Beat in the vanilla, peppermint extract and flax egg. Add the flour mixture and mix until just combined. Fold in the chocolate.
  - Scoop out a spoonful of dough and roll into a ball. Place on the tray, leaving space between each cookie, then lightly flatten. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Leave to cool slightly before transferring to a wire rack.
