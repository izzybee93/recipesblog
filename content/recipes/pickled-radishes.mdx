---
title: "Pickled radishes"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - salad
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pickled-radishes.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '50g radishes, finely sliced'
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - ½ tsp sugar
  - '1½ tbsp rice vinegar, apple cider vinegar or lemon juice'
directions:
  - 'In a small bowl, add the radishes and salt. Toss well and leave for 10 minutes.'
  - Squeeze out any excess liquid from the radishes and drain. Add the apple cider vinegar and sugar. Toss well and leave for at least 15 minutes before serving. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
