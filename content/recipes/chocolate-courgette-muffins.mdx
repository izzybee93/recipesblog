---
title: "Chocolate courgette muffins"
date: "2026-04-12"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-courgette-muffins.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - '250g courgette, grated (~1 large courgette)'
  - 120g soft brown sugar
  - 190ml soya milk
  - 125g vegetable oil
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 250g self-raising flour
  - 50g cocoa powder
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 100g dark chocolate chips
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a muffin tin with cases.
  - 'In a large bowl, add the courgette, sugar, milk, oil and vanilla extract, and mix well.'
  - 'In a medium bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.'
  - 'Divide the batter between muffin cases. Bake for 25 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. Leave to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.'
