---
title: "Strawberry power porridge"
date: "2026-07-19"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/strawberry-power-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water'
  - '75g strawberries, sliced'
  - ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
directions:
  - In a small pan, add the oats, chia seeds, plant milk and half the strawberries. Cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently. Stir in the cinnamon.
  - Serve topped with hemp seeds, the remaining strawberry slices and a drizzle of nut butter.
