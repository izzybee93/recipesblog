---
title: "Cherry and almond polenta cake"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - cakes
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/cherry-and-almond-polenta-cake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - 150g frozen cherries
  - 75ml vegetable oil
  - 200ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - Zest of 2 lemons
  - 150g fine polenta
  - 150g ground almonds
  - 75g plain flour
  - 100g caster sugar
  - ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - 1½ tsp baking powder
  - 25g smooth almond butter
  - 20g flaked almonds
directions:
  - Defrost the cherries and drain off any excess liquid.
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Line a 20cm cake tin. Place the cherries in the tin.
  - 'In a measuring jug, add the apple cider vinegar and soya milk. Set aside for 5 minutes.'
  - 'In a large bowl, combine the polenta, ground almonds, flour, sugar, bicarbonate of soda and baking powder.'
  - Add the oil and lemon zest to the soya milk mixture and whisk to combine. Pour into the dry ingredients and gently mix. Stir in the almond butter.
  - 'Pour the batter into the tin over the cherries. Bake for 1 hour, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. Leave to cool slightly before turning out onto a serving plate, so the cherries are on top. Top with a sprinkle of flaked almonds.'
