---
title: "Mushrooms on toast with paprika remoulade"
date: "2026-04-16"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mushrooms-on-toast-with-paprika-remoulade.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 300g mushrooms
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tsp dried thyme
  - '2 tbsp pumpkin seeds, toasted'
  - '1 large tomato, sliced'
  - '2 slices sourdough, toasted'
  - '## Sauce'
  - 2 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - '1 tbsp capers, finely chopped'
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - 'To make the sauce, add all the ingredients to a small bowl and mix until smooth. Season to taste, then chill in the fridge for 15 minutes before serving.'
  - 'In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms, smoked paprika, garlic powder and thyme and cook for 5 minutes, until lightly browned.'
  - 'Place the tomato slices on the sourdough, then add the mushrooms. Drizzle the sauce over the mushrooms and top with the toasted pumpkin seeds.'
