servings: serves 2-3
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can of butter beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 garlic cloves
  - 70g pine nuts
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 25g basil
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - 100g frozen peas
  - 3 large handfuls spinach
  - Â¼ tsp sea salt
directions:
  - >-
    In a food processor, add the garlic, pine nuts, lemon juice, basil, olive
    oil, salt and a few tbsp of water and blend until a chunky pesto forms.
  - >-
    In a pan, warm a drizzle of olive oil on medium heat. Add the butter beans
    and cook for 5 minutes until they crisp up around the edges.
  - >-
    Add the pesto, peas and spinach and cook for 5-10 minutes until everything
    has warmed through and the spinach has wilted. Serving with salads, on toast
    or with pasta.
