---
title: "Carrot cake doughnuts"
date: "2025-08-28"
categories: ["treats", "cakes"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/carrot-cake-doughnuts.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 240g self-raising flour
  - ½ tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 85g light brown sugar
  - 2 tsp cinnamon
  - ½ tsp nutmeg
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - 150g carrots, grated (2 medium carrots)
  - 200ml soya milk
  - 30ml vegetable oil
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  - '## Glaze'
  - 75g icing sugar
  - 1-2 tbsp soya milk
  - 30g pecans, chopped
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Grease a 12 ring donut pan.
  - In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, salt, spices and sugar.
  - In a measuring jug, whisk together the oil, soya milk, vanilla extract and apple cider vinegar. 
  - Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix together. Stir in the carrots. 
  - Spoon the batter into the donut pan. Bake for 20 minutes until risen and golden.
  - Use a knife to gently loosen the donuts around the edges, then tip them out onto a plate.
  - To make the glaze, sift the icing sugar into a small bowl. Add the soya milk a spoon at a time and mix well. Dip the donuts in to coat the top half. Sprinkle with pecans before the glaze has set.
