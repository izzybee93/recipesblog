title: "Roasted cauliflower, carrot and chickpea bowl with orange dressing"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-cauliflower-carrot-and-chickpea-bowl-with-orange-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - ¼ cauliflower, chopped into small florets
  - 2 medium carrots, chopped
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - 50g baby spinach
  - 1 avocado, sliced
  - Small handful mint, finely chopped
  - Small handful parsley, finely chopped
  - 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  - 10 roasted almonds, chopped
  - Zingy orange dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the cauliflower, carrots and chickpeas. Drizzle with the olive oil, then add the spices and mix until coated. Bake for 25 minutes.
  - Make the dressing.
  - In a large bowl, add the spinach, herbs, roasted vegetables, pomegranate seeds and almonds. Add the dressing and gently toss to combine. Add the avocado and gently mix. Serve with extra mint leaves for garnish.
