---
title: "Lemon mousse"
date: "2025-11-13"
categories: ["treats", "desserts"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-mousse.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 300g silken tofu
  - Zest of 1 lemon
  - 85ml lemon juice
  - 50g icing sugar
  - Pinch sea salt
  - Pinch turmeric
directions:
  - Add all ingredients to a food processor. Blend until smooth.
  - Spoon into 4 glasses and chill for at least 2 hours to set.
  - Top with fruit, whipped cream, or your favourite toppings. Chill until ready to serve.
---
