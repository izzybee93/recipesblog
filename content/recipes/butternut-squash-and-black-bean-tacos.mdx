---
title: "Butternut squash and black bean tacos"
date: "2026-02-27"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/butternut-squash-and-black-bean-tacos.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '250g butternut squash, peeled and cut into 2cm cubes'
  - '1 small onion, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, finely chopped'
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp ground coriander
  - '120g black beans
  - 150ml vegetable stock
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 4-6 small tortillas
  - '## Harissa salsa'
  - 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  - 20g pumpkin seeds
  - ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  - 2-3 tsp harissa paste (see recipe)
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - ½ tbsp maple syrup
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - 'In a large frying pan, heat the olive oil then add the butternut squash. Cook over medium heat for 8 minutes, until browned and lightly charred. Add the onion and cook for 3 minutes, then add the garlic, cumin and ground coriander and cook for 2 more minutes.'
  - 'Add the black beans and vegetable stock. Bring to a simmer, cover with a lid and cook for 10 minutes, until the squash has softened and the liquid has reduced. Stir in the fresh coriander.'
  - 'Toast the sesame and pumpkin seeds in a small pan over a medium heat for 1-2 minutes. Transfer to a food processor. Add the harissa paste, lime juice and maple syrup and sea salt. Process until almost smooth.'
  - 'Heat the tortillas in a pan over a medium-high heat. Assemble the tacos with the veggies and top with the salsa.'
