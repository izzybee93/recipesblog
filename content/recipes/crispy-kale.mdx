---
title: "Crispy kale"
date: "2026-02-14"
categories: ["snacks"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crispy-kale.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 125g kale, finely chopped
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - Toss the kale with the olive oil and nutritional yeast. Spread it out the tray and bake on the top rack for 8 minutes until crispy.
---
