---
title: "Black beans"
date: "2026-01-02"
categories: ["grains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/black-beans.jpeg"
servings: "serves 12"
ingredients:
  - 500g dried black beans
directions:
  - Add the beans to a large bowl and fill with water. Leave to soak overnight
    for 12 hours.
  - Drain and rinse the beans. Add to a pressure cooker. Add cold water until
    the beans are covered by 3cm of water.
  - Once the pressure is reached, cook on a low heat for 15 minutes. Store in
    the fridge for up to 5 days, or freeze in portions for up to 6 months.
---
