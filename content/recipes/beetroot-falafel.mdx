---
title: "Beetroot falafel"
date: "2026-02-28"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-falafel.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 1 large beetroot
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds
  - '½ onion, chopped'
  - 1 garlic clove
  - Large handful fresh coriander
  - Small handful fresh parsley
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Zest of ½ lemon
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - Pinch chilli flakes (optional)
  - ¼ tsp baking powder
  - 2 tbsp plain flour
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 20g sesame seeds
directions:
  - 'In a small bowl, add the chia seeds and 3 tbsp water. Set aside for 10 minutes.'
  - 'In a food processor, add all the ingredients except the olive oil and sesame seeds. Process until finely chopped. Transfer to the fridge for 30 minutes.'
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C and prepare a large baking tray with parchment paper.
  - 'Take 1 tbsp of falafel mixture at a time and form small patties. Brush with oil and coat with sesame seeds, then place on the tray. Bake for 25 minutes, flipping halfway.'
  - 'Serve with salad, hummus and pitta. Store in the fridge for up to 3 days or freeze for up to 3 months.'
---
