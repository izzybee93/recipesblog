---
title: "Silken tofu ice cream"
date: "2025-11-09"
categories: ["desserts", "treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/silken-tofu-ice-cream.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - 600g silken tofu, drained
  - 50g caster sugar
  - 2 tsp vanilla bean paste
directions:
  - In a blender, blend silken tofu and sugar until smooth. Add the vanilla extract and blend again. Transfer to the fridge for 1-2 hours. Remove the ice cream maker from the freezer for at least 30 minutes to prevent the tofu from freezing immediately when churning.
  - Pour into the ice cream maker and churn for 20 minutes. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 1 hour before serving.
