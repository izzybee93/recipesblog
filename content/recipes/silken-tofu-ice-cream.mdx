---
title: "Silken tofu ice cream"
date: "2025-11-09"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/silken-tofu-ice-cream.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 300g silken tofu
  - 30g caster sugar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
directions:
  - In a blender, blend silken tofu and sugar until smooth. Add the vanilla extract and blend again.
  - Chill the mixture in the fridge for at least 1 hour for a better churn.
  - Pour into your ice cream maker and churn for 20 minutes. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 1 hour before serving.
