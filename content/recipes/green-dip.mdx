---
title: "Green dip"
date: "2025-12-28"
categories: ["snacks"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/green-dip.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-3"
ingredients:
  - 100g peas
  - 100g edamame 
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 150g silken tofu, drained
  - 25g basil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Cook the peas and edamame for 2 minutes then transfer to a food processor.
  - Add the tofu, oil, basil, lemon juice, garlic, salt and black pepper. Process until the mixture is smooth, thick and creamy. Serve as a dip or spread on toasted sourdough.
---
