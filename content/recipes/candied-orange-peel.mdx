title: Candied orange peel
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.267Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/candied-orange-peel.png
servings: serves 1 cake decoration
ingredients:
  - 1 orange
  - 100g caster sugar
directions:
  - >-
    Peel the orange in strips and cut into desired shape. Remove as much white
    pith as possible.
  - >-
    Blanch the strips by covering them in cold water then bring to boil. Drain
    and rinse.
  - >-
    In a small pan, whisk together the sugar and 100ml water. Place the peels in
    the mixture. Bring it to a boil and then simmer until the peels become
    translucent.
  - >-
    Roll in white sugar if desired. Allow to dry out for a few hours or
    overnight before decorating.
