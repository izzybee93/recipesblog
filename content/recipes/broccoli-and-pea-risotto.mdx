---
title: "Broccoli and pea risotto"
date: "2026-03-17"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/broccoli-and-pea-risotto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 140g risotto rice
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 small onion, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - ~800ml vegetable stock
  - '½ large broccoli, chopped'
  - 100g frozen peas
  - 75ml oat or soya cream
  - '4 basil leaves, chopped'
  - 2 tsp lemon juice
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Heat the oil in a pan and cook the onions and garlic on a medium heat. Add the rice and cook for 1 minute.
  - Gradually add the stock, pouring a little at a time until the rice is almost cooked, for 10-15 minutes. 
  - Stir in the broccoli and peas. Continue to add stock until rice is cooked and stock is absorbed. Stir in the cream until heated through.
  - Remove from the heat, then stir in the basil and lemon juice. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
---
