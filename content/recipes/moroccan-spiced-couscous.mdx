---
title: "Moroccan spiced couscous"
date: "2025-12-01"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/moroccan-spiced-couscous.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - 150g couscous
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - ~300ml vegetable stock
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 1 tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp coriander
  - ½ tsp cinnamon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - Small handful fresh coriander or mint, chopped
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 30g raisins or cranberries 
  - 3 tbsp toasted almond flakes
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two baking trays. Add the aubergine to the tray, brush with olive oil and roast for 15 minutes. Add the peppers to the other tray and brush with olive oil. Add the chickpeas and roast for another 15 minutes.
  - Meanwhile, in a large bowl, add the couscous and cover with vegetable stock until the water is 0.5cm above the couscous. Set aside for 10 mins.
  - Run a fork through the couscous. Add the lemon juice and mix, followed by the herbs and spices.
  - Stir in the chickpeas, raisins, almonds and roasted vegetables. Add the olive oil and mix well. Top with ground black pepper. Serve warm or cold.
