title: Sweet potato chickpea bowl with tahini sauce
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.312Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/sweet-potato-chickpea-buddha-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '100g rice or quinoa, rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 large sweet potato, sliced'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - '1 avocado, sliced'
  - Tahini sauce (see recipe)
directions:
  - Cook the rice or quinoa.
  - >-
    In a large frying pan, heat the sesame oil, then add the sweet potato. Toss
    to coat in oil, then add ~100ml water. Cover with a lid and cook on a medium
    heat until soft, stirring occasionally. Add more water if needed. After 8-10
    minutes, remove the lid to allow the water to evaporate.
  - >-
    In a medium frying pan, add the chickpeas and spices. Toss together and cook
    on a medium-high heat until slightly crispy.
  - >-
    Divide the rice or quinoa between 2 bowls. Add the sweet potato, chickpeas
    and avocado. Serve with a drizzle of tahini sauce.
