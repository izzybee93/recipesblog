---
title: "Roasted red pepper and tomato soup"
date: "2025-08-30"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-red-pepper-and-tomato-soup.jpeg"
servings: "serves 6"
ingredients:
  - 6 medium tomatoes, quartered
  - 2 red bell peppers, quartered
  - 1 onion, quartered 
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 3 garlic cloves, peeled
  - 600ml vegetable stock
  - ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two large baking trays with baking paper.
  - Place the tomatoes on one tray, and peppers, garlic and onions on the other. Drizzle each tray with 1 tbsp olive oil and gently toss to coat. Bake for 35-40 minutes, placing the tomatoes on top in the oven.
  - Transfer the roasted vegetables to a large pan. Add the vegetable stock, spices and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes on a medium heat.
  - Remove from the heat and blend the soup. Season to taste with salt and ground black pepper. Reheat for 5-10 minutes and serve.
