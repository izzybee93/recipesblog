categories:

  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolate-banana-peanut-butter-pudding.jpeg
servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - 1 medium banana
  - ½ ripe avocado
  - 30g cocoa powder
  - 45g salted peanut butter
  - '40ml maple syrup or 6 dates, chopped'
  - '20ml non-dairy milk, or 50ml if using dates'
directions:
  - Blend all ingredients in a food processor until creamy and smooth.
  - >-
    Divide between small serving glasses, cover and chill for 1 hour before
    serving. Store in the fridge for up to 2 days.
