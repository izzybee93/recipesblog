---
title: "Super green dip"
date: "2025-12-28"
categories: ["snacks"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-dip.jpeg"
servings: "serves 6"
ingredients:
  - 100g frozen peas
  - 80g frozen edamame 
  - 150g silken tofu, drained
  - 50g baby spinach
  - 15g fresh basil
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Cook the peas and edamame for 2 minutes then transfer to a food processor.
  - Add the remaining ingredients and process until the mixture is smooth, thick and creamy. Transfer to the fridge for 30 minutes before serving. Serve as a dip or spread on toasted sourdough.
---
