---
title: "Creamy oats with hazelnut and strawberry compote"
date: "2026-07-24"
categories:
  - breakfast
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-oats-with-hazelnut-and-strawberry-compote.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 2 tsp hazelnut butter or almond butter
  - 2 tsp strawberry compote (see recipe)
  - 3 tbsp oats
  - 50ml soya milk
  - '2 tsp hazelnuts, chopped and toasted'
  - '1-2 fresh strawberries, sliced'
  - 1 tsp cacao nibs
directions:
  - In a small glass, add the nut butter. Layer 2 tsp of strawberry compote on top. Add the oats and soya milk.
  - Top with hazelnuts, strawberries and cacao nibs.
  - Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.
