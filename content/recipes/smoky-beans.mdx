---
title: Smoky beans
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.305Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/smoky-beans.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ red onion, finely diced
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 1 tsp sugar
  - 1 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
  - '1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 100g passata
  - 2 tbsp water
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the oil over a medium heat. Add the onion and cook
    for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - >-
    Stir in the tomato paste, smoked paprika, chilli, salt, sugar, soy sauce, mustard and
    apple cider vinegar. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  - >-
    Add the beans and passata. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened. Add water as needed.
  - >-
    Serve on toast with a side of avocado and roasted tomatoes, or with flatbread and a refreshing cucumber and herb salad.
