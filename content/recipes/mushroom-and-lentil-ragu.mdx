---
title: "Mushroom and lentil ragu"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mushroom-and-lentil-ragu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 20g dried porcini mushrooms
  - 50g walnuts, finely chopped
  - 1 medium white onion, finely chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  - 2 sticks celery, finely chopped
  - 400g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  - 1 400g can brown lentils, drained and rinsed
  - 1 red pepper, finely chopped
  - 1 tsp dried thyme
  - 1 tsp dried oregano
  - 1 tsp dried rosemary
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce 
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - Â½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Soak the porcini mushrooms in 250ml boiling water for 20 minutes. Drain and reserve the liquid. Finely chop the mushrooms.
  - Heat the olive oil in the pan. Add the onion, garlic and celery, and cook for 5 minutes, until softened.
  - Add the mushrooms, pepper, lentils, thyme, oregano, rosemary and sea salt. Cook for 5 minutes, until softened.
  - In a large pan, toast the walnuts over medium-high heat for 2 minutes, then set aside.
  - Stir in the tomato paste, soy sauce, porcini mushrooms and liquid, and the tomatoes. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occassionally, until thickened.
  - Stir in the walnuts and nutritional yeast and cook for 5 more minutes. Season to taste.
  - Serve with pasta or crusty bread.
