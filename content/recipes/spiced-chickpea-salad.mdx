featured_image: /images/recipes/spicedchickpeasalad.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 lettuce heart, chopped'
  - '2 large tomatoes, chopped'
  - '4 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped'
  - '¼ cucumber, chopper'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 'Black pepper, to taste'
  - '## Dressing'
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
directions:
  - >-
    Heat a medium pan and add the chickpeas. Cook on a medium-high heat for a
    few minutes. Mix in the spices and cook for another 5-10 minutes, until
    slightly crispy.
  - >-
    Meanwhile, in a large bowl, add the lettuce, tomatoes and cucumber and toss
    together. Sprinkle over the salt, balsamic vinegar and olive oil for the
    dressing, then toss until mixed well. Add the chickpeas and gently toss
    together.
