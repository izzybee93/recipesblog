---
title: "Green pesto"
date: "2025-12-29"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/green-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 70g edamame
  - 70g peas
  - 80g spinach
  - 15g fresh basil
  - 40g pine nuts
  - 1 ½ tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Cook the peas and edamame for 2 minutes. 
  - Place all the ingredients in a food processor and process until almost smooth.
  - Serving with pasta, or on toasted sourdough with avocado, tomatoes or your favourite veggies.
