---
title: "Sesame tofu and mushroom salad bowl"
date: "2026-04-14"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sesame-tofu-and-mushroom-salad-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '225g extra firm tofu, cubed'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '200g mushrooms, chopped'
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - 80g sugar snap peas
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - Sesame salad dressing (see recipe)
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - 'In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Cook the tofu and mushrooms on a medium-high heat for 10 minutes, turning occasionally to brown each side.'
  - Make the dressing.
  - 'Assemble the bowls, drizzle with the dressing, and sprinkle with sesame seeds.'
