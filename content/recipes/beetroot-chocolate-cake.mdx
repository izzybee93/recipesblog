title: "Beetroot chocolate cake"
date: "2026-04-13"
categories:
  - cakes
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-chocolate-cake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice flour
  - 100g ground almonds
  - 75g cocoa powder
  - 25g cornflour
  - 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - 150g caster sugar
  - '250g beetroot, cooked'
  - 200ml vegetable oil
  - 120ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - '## Glaze'
  - 120g icing sugar
  - 2-3 tbsp beetroot, blended
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Prepare a 20cm cake tin.
  - 'In a large bowl, mix the rice flour, cornflour, ground almonds, bicarbonate of soda, cocoa powder and sugar until evenly combined.'
  - Add the beetroot to a blender and blend until smooth. Set aside 3 tbsp for the glaze.
  - 'In a measuring jug, whisk together the oil, soya milk, vinegar, vanilla and remaining beetroot. Pour into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.'
  - 'Pour into the prepared tin and bake for 40-45 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. Leave to cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.'
  - Sift the icing sugar into a small bowl. Add the reserved beetroot 1 tbsp at a time until a thick glaze forms. Stir in the lemon juice. Drizzle over the cake.
