---
title: "Creamy avocado dressing"
date: "2026-03-17"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-avocado-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 3 tbsp fresh lemon or lime juice
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 60ml water
  - '## Optional herbs'
  - 10g fresh coriander
  - 5g fresh dill
  - 10g fresh chives
  -'1 tsp mixed herbs, such as chimichurri'
directions:
  - 'Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth, scraping down sides as needed. Add the water, 1 tbsp at a time, until the dressing reaches desired consistency.'
---
