---
title: "Creamy avocado dressing"
date: "2026-03-17"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-avocado-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - ½ ripe avocado
  - Juice of ½ lemon or lime
  - 1 ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder (optional)
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 4-6 tbsp water
  - '## Optional herbs'
  - 10g fresh coriander
  - 5g fresh dill
  - 10g fresh chives
  - '2 tsp mixed herbs, such as chimichurri'
directions:
  - 'Combine all ingredients and 4 tbsp of the water in a blender and blend until smooth, scraping down sides as needed. Add more water as needed, 1 tbsp at a time, until it reaches desired consistency (thicker sauce or thinner dressing).'
  - Adjust the lemon or lime and salt to taste. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days.
---
