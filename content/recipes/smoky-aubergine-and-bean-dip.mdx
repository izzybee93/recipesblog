---
title: "Smoky aubergine and bean dip"
date: "2026-07-24"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/smoky-aubergine-and-bean-dip.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4–6"
ingredients:
  - 1 medium aubergine
  - '2 garlic cloves, unpeeled'
  - '1 400g can kidney beans or cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1–2 tbsp water
  - 'Small handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - Prick the aubergine a few times with a fork and place on the tray with the garlic. Roast for 25–30 minutes, turning halfway, until the aubergine is very soft.
  - Scoop out the flesh from the aubergine and add to a food processor. Squeeze the garlic from its skins and add it to the food processor with the miso paste, lemon juice, olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin and salt. Blend for 30 seconds. Add the kidney beans and blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
  - Add the water, 1 tbsp at a time, until creamy. Stir through the parsley.
  - Serve with warm pitta, crackers or crunchy vegetables.
