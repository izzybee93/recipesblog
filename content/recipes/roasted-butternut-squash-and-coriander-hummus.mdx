---
title: "Roasted butternut squash and coriander hummus"
date: "2026-04-13"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-butternut-squash-and-coriander-hummus.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - '1 butternut squash, halved'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 garlic cloves
  - '400g chickpeas, plus ½ their liquid'
  - 3 tbsp tahini
  - '1 lemon, juiced'
  - '25g coriander, roughly chopped'
  - Pitta bread or crudités
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Chop one half of the butternut squash into cubes.
  - 'Place the whole half of the squash on the tray and coat with 1 tbsp oil. Turn the squash face down and roast for 20 minutes. Add the chopped squash and garlic cloves to tray. Coat with the remaining oil and roast for another 20 minutes, until softened.'
  - Combine the tahini and lemon juice in a food processor and mix for 30 seconds. Scrape the sides of the bowl then process for a few more seconds.
  - 'Scoop the flesh from the roasted half and transfer to a food processor. Add the garlic, chickpeas and coriander, reserving a few leaves for garnish. Process until smooth, adding a few tbsp water if needed.'
  - Transfer to a large serving bowl and scatter over the roasted squash and a few extra coriander leaves.
