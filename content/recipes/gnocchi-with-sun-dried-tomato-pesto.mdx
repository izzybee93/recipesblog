---
title: "Gnocchi with sun-dried tomato pesto"
date: "2026-06-25"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/gnocchi-with-sun-dried-tomato-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 300g gnocchi
  - 120g broccolini
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 200g frozen peas
  - ½ sun-dried tomato pesto (see recipe)
  - Juice of ¼ lemon
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the broccolini, brush with the olive oil and roast for 10 minutes, until lightly charred.
  - Heat a large pan and add the peas and a few tbsp water. Cook for 2 minutes, then stir in pesto.
  - Cook the gnocchi. Add to the pan along with a few splashes of leftover water. Stir until heated through. Remove from the heat and stir in the lemon juice.
  - Serve topped with the roasted broccolini and ground black pepper.
