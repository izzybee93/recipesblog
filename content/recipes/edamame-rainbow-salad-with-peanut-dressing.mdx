title: "Edamame rainbow salad with peanut dressing"
date: "2026-05-12"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/edamame-rainbow-salad-with-peanut-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '¼ small red cabbage, chopped'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - '1 large carrot, grated'
  - '¼ cucumber, sliced'
  - '½ red pepper, finely sliced'
  - '200g edamame, cooked'
  - 'Large handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - Peanut dressing (see recipe)
  - '## Garnish'
  - '2 tbsp peanuts, chopped and roasted'
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the red cabbage and lime juice. Massage briefly then set aside.
  - Add the carrot, cucumber, pepper, edamame and coriander and toss together.
  - Make the peanut dressing. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  - Serve with a garnish of roasted peanuts and sesame seeds.
