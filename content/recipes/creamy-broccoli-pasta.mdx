title: Creamy broccoli pasta
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.260Z'
categories:

  - pasta
featured_image: /images/recipes/creamy-broccoli-pasta.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - '½ broccoli, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 150g frozen peas
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - '## Sauce'
  - 150ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp white miso paste
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 1 small garlic clove
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the broccoli, brush with olive oil and roast for 10 minutes.
  - Cook the pasta and peas, reserving some pasta water.
  - >-
    In a measuring jug, whisk together the soya milk, miso paste, olive oil,
    nutritional yeast, garlic and seasoning. Add more milk as
    needed.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the sauce on a medium heat and stir frequently until
    slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp of pasta water as needed. Stir in the lemon juice.
  - >-
    Add the pasta and stir until coated and heated through. Season to
    taste. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
