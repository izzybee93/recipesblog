---
title: "Blueberry compote"
date: "2026-02-26"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/blueberry-compote.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 small jar"
ingredients:
  - 250g blueberries
  - 30g caster sugar
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - ½ tsp lemon zest
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - 'In a pan, combine the blueberries, sugar, salt, and lemon juice. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.'
  - 'Reduce the heat and leave to simmer for 5 more minutes, until the blueberries are soft and the sauce has thickened. Remove from the heat and stir in the lemon zest.'
  - 'Serve warm with pancakes, or leave to cool and thicken. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 2 months.'
---
