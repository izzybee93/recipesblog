---
title: "Green pasta with mushroom, tomato and crispy balsamic tofu"
date: "2026-07-24"
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/green-pasta-with-mushroom-tomato-and-crispy-balsamic-tofu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 160g pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '200g mushrooms, sliced'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 150g cherry tomatoes
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - '## Sauce'
  - 100g rocket or spinach
  - 50g silken tofu
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 15g fresh basil
  - 2 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 4 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 1 tbsp miso paste
  - 1 tsp garlic powder
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - '## Topping'
  - 120g crispy balsamic tofu bites (see recipe)
directions:
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, until lightly browned. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Stir in half the crispy balsamic tofu bites.
  - Add all the sauce ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  - Add the sauce to the pan and stir over medium-low heat until smooth and slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp pasta water as needed. Stir in the lemon juice.
  - Add the pasta and toss to combine. Stir in the tomatoes until heated through. Serve topped with the remaining crispy balsamic tofu bites.
