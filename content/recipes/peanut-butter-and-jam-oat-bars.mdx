---
title: "Peanut butter and jam oat bars"
date: "2025-09-21"
categories: 
  - treats
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/peanut-butter-and-jam-oat-bars.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 215g peanut butter
  - 35g maple syrup
  - 25g vegetable oil
  - 125g rolled oats
  - 100g oat flour
  - ¼ tsp baking powder
  - 2-3 tbsp water 
  - 140g raspberry jam
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 8x8 baking tin with baking paper.
  - Set 60g of the peanut butter aside. In a large bowl, combine all the wet ingredients.
  - Add the dry ingredients and mix well to form a dough. Add the water, 1 tbsp at a time, until the mixture comes together.
  - Place ¾ of the dough into the baking tin and press down firmly with the back of a spoon. Bake the layer for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and leave to cool for 10 minutes. 
  - Spread the remaining peanut butter evenly on top of the baked layer. Spread a layer of jam on top.
  - Crumble up the remaining dough and spread evenly on top. Add a handful of frozen raspberries if desired. Bake for 25 minutes.
  - Leave to cool completely, then cut into squares.
