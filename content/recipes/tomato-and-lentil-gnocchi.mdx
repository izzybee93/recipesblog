---
title: Tomato and lentil gnocchi
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.314Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/tomato-and-lentil-gnocchi.jpeg
servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 onion, finely chopped
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 400ml vegetable stock
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - '1 400g can green lentils, drained and rinsed'
  - 400g gnocchi
  - 200g spinach
  - Handful basil leaves
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  - >-
    Heat the oil in a large oven safe pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and
    cook for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another
    minute.
  - >-
    Stir in the tomato paste, tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, salt and vegetable
    stock. Simmer for 5–10 minutes until slightly thickened.
  - >-
    Remove from the heat and stir in the lentils and gnocchi. Place the lid on
    the pan and bake for 25 minutes.
  - >-
    Over medium-low heat, stir in the spinach until wilted. Season with ground
    black pepper. Remove from the heat and stir in the torn basil leaves. Serve
    topped with ground black pepper.
