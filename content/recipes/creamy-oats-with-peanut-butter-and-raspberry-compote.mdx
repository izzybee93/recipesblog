---
title: "Creamy oats with peanut butter and raspberry compote"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["breakfast", "treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-oats-with-peanut-butter-and-raspberry-compote.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 4 tsp peanut butter
  - 2 tsp raspberry compote (see recipe)
  - 3 tbsp oats
  - 50ml soya milk
directions:
  - In a small glass, add 2 tsp of peanut butter. Layer 1 tsp of raspberry compote on top. Add the oats and soya milk.
  - Spoon on the remaining layers of raspberry compote and peanut butter on top.
  - Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.
