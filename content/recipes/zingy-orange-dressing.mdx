---
title: "Zingy orange dressing"
date: "2026-04-22"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/zingy-orange-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp fresh orange juice
  - 1 tsp orange zest
  - ½ tsp maple syrup
  - ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  - ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  - 'Pinch sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Whisk all the ingredients together in a small measuring jug.
