---
title: "Crispy tofu"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crispy-tofu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '280g extra-firm tofu, cubed'
  - ½ tbsp sesame oil
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp maple syrup
  - 1 tbsp rice vinegar or lime juice
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - Place the tofu in a shallow dish.
  - 'In a small bowl, whisk together all the marinade ingredients except the sesame seeds. Pour over the tofu, cover and transfer to the fridge for 2-12 hours.'
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the tofu and bake for 30-35 minutes, flipping halfway and brushing leftover marinade. Bake until golden and crispy. Alternatively, cook in an air fryer for 12 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.'
  - 'Serve with stir-fried veggies, rice, noodles, salad or your favourite sauce.'
