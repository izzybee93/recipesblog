title: Mediterranean roasted vegetable salad

categories:

  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/mediterraneanroastedvegetablesalad.jpeg
servings: serves 3
ingredients:
  - 90g rocket
  - '2 large tomatoes, sliced'
  - 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  - '1 red pepper, sliced'
  - 1 courgette sliced
  - '½ red onion, sliced'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp balsamic ginger
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Place the red pepper, courgette and red onion on
    a baking tray and drizzle with the vegetable oil. Bake for 15-20 mins.
  - >-
    Place the rocket, tomatoes and chickpeas in a large bowl. Add the roasted
    vegetables and gently mix.
  - >-
    Sprinkle over the salt, then add the lemon juice, balsamic vinegar and olive
    oil. Gently mix until coated in the dressing.
