---
title: "Broccoli salad"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories: 
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/broccoli-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 1 broccoli head, chopped into small florets
  - 250g cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - 25g Medjool dates or dried cranberries, chopped
  - 50g pecans or walnuts, chopped
  - 25g pumpkin seeds
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped
  - 4 mint leaves, chopped
  - 1 400g chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '## Dressing'
  - 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil 
  - Juice 1 lime
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the broccoli, tomatoes, dried fruit and herbs.
  - Heat a large pan over medium heat and lightly toast the pecans or walnuts for a two minutes. Add the pumpkin seeds and toast for one more minute, then transfer to the large bowl.
  - Add the chickpeas to the pan over a medium heat. Cook for 5 minutes, then add the spices. Cook for 5 more minutes or until slightly crispy. Transfer to the large bowl and toss together.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss to combine. Serve with an extra sprinkle of pumpkin seeds.
