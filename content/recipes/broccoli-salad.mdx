---
title: "Broccoli salad"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/broccoli-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-3"
ingredients:
  - '½ broccoli head, finely chopped'
  - '125g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - '15g dried cranberries or Medjool dates, finely chopped'
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, finely chopped'
  - 'Small handful mint leaves, finely chopped'
  - '25g walnuts, chopped'
  - 15g pumpkin seeds
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the broccoli, tomatoes, dried fruit and herbs.
  - Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly toast the walnuts for 2 minutes. Add the pumpkin seeds and toast for 1 more minute, then transfer to the large bowl.
  - Add the chickpeas to the pan over a medium heat. Cook for 5 minutes, then add the spices. Cook for 5 more minutes or until slightly crispy. Transfer to the large bowl and toss together.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss to combine. Serve with an extra sprinkle of pumpkin seeds.
