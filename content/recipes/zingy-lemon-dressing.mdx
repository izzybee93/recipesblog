---
title: "Zingy lemon dressing"
date: "2025-10-16"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/zingy-lemon-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1-2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped 
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Freshly ground black pepper
directions:
  - Whisk the ingredients together in a small measuring jug.
---
