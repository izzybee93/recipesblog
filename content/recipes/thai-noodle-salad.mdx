servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 50g rice noodles
  - 'Mix of red cabbage, lettuce, carrots or cucumber, grated'
  - '1 bell pepper, finely sliced'
  - 250g edamame, cooked
  - 'Large handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - '## Sauce'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, peeled'
  - '1 large clove garlic, peeled'
  - 30g peanut butter
  - Juice of ½ orange
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 1 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  - 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  - ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  - '## Garnish'
  - '4 tbsp roasted, crushed peanuts'
  - 1 tbsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - 'Cook the rice noodles. Drain and rinse, then set aside.'
  - >-
    Add all the sauce ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  - >-
    Place the veggies, edamame and coriander into a serving
    bowl and toss together. Add the noodles and toss again. Pour the peanut
    sauce over top and toss well to combine.
  - 'Garnish with roasted peanuts, sesame seeds, coriander and a lime wedge.'
