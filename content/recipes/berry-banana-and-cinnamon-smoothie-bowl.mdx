title: "Berry, banana and cinnamon smoothie bowl"
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.260Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/berry-banana-and-cinnamon-smoothie-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, chopped'
  - 30g blueberries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - 50ml plant milk
  - 25g strawberries, sliced
  - 25g raspberries
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, blueberries (reserving a few for topping), nut butter, chia seeds, oats, cinnamon and plant milk
    until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with strawberries, raspberries, blueberries and
    granola.
