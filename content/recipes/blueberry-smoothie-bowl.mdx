title: Blueberry smoothie bowl

categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/blueberrysmoothiebowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, chopped'
  - 25g blueberries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - 50ml almond milk
  - '1 kiwi, sliced'
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, blueberries, peanut butter, oats, cinnamon and almond milk
    until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with kiwi and granola.
