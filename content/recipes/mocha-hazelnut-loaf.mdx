---
title: "Mocha hazelnut loaf"
date: "2026-06-27"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mocha-hazelnut-loaf.jpeg"
servings: "serves 10"
ingredients:
  - 250g self-raising flour
  - 15g cornflour
  - ¾ tsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - 60ml strong espresso or 4 tsp instant coffee
  - 60ml vegetable oil
  - 120ml soya milk
  - 115g caster sugar
  - '50g hazelnuts, roughly chopped and toasted'
  - '75g dark chocolate, chopped'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a loaf tin with baking paper.
  - In a large bowl, mix together the flour, cornflour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt.
  - In a measuring jug, combine the oil, soya milk, sugar and espresso. Stir until the sugar has mostly dissolved.
  - Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in the hazelnuts and 50g of the dark chocolate.
  - Bake for 45-50 minutes, until lightly browned and a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  - Leave to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before decorating.
  - Melt the remaining 25g chocolate in a bowl over a pan of boiling water. Drizzle the chocolate over the loaf and leave to cool completely before slicing.
---
