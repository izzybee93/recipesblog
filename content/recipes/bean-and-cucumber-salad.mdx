---
title: "Bean and cucumber salad"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["salad"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/bean-and-cucumber-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 400g cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  - ½ cucumber, chopped
  - 8 radishes, chopped
  - 2 tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 ½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the beans, cucumber, radishes and dill.
  - To make the dressing, whisk together the mustard, olive oil, lemon juice, sea salt and black pepper in a small measuring jug.
  - Pour the dressing over the salad and gently mix. Serve chilled, topped with fresh dill for garnish.
