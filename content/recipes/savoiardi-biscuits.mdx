---
title: "Savoiardi biscuits"
date: "2026-07-02"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/savoiardi-biscuits.jpeg"
servings: "makes 20"
ingredients:
  - 240g plain flour
  - 2 tbsp cornflour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 120ml aquafaba
  - 60g caster sugar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 30g vegetable oil
  - 1 tbsp icing sugar
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 150°C and line two baking trays with baking paper.
  - In a medium bowl, mix together the plain flour, cornflour, baking powder and sea salt, then set aside.
  - In a large bowl, add the aquafaba and whisk with a hand mixer on high speed until stiff peaks form. Add the caster sugar and whisk again until the mixture is glossy.
  - Gradually add the vanilla extract and oil and continue to whisk. Fold in the flour mixture until just combined and no lumps remain. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  - Transfer the batter to a piping bag fitted with a round nozzle and pipe in lines around 6cm long on the baking trays.
  - Sift the icing sugar over the biscuits and bake for 20 minutes, until golden. Leave to cool for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool and crisp up fully.
