---
title: "Carrot and quinoa salad"
date: "2025-11-12"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/carrot-and-quinoa-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 70g quinoa
  - 2 large carrots, grated
  - 2 handfuls cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - ¼ cucumber, chopped
  - 80g chickpeas
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - ¼ tsp oregano
  - Small handful fresh parsley, chopped
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - In a medium bowl, add the carrots followed by the salt, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar and 1 tbsp olive oil. Mix well.
  - Mix in the quinoa, cucumber, tomatoes and chickpeas. Add the remaining 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar and 1 tbsp olive oil, oregano and parsley. Mix well.
---
