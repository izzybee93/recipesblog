servings: makes 8
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 80g walnuts
  - 25g oats
  - '1 garlic clove'
  - '½ small red onion'
  - 1 tsp oregano
  - ½ tsp sage
  - ½ tsp thyme
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar,
  - '## Sauce'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, halved'
  - 250g passata
  - 2 handfuls spinach
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - A few fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - Place all the meatball ingredients in a food processor and pulse for a few seconds until combined, but not completely smooth. Add a tbsp of water if needed and pulse again.
  - Roll heaped tbsp of the mixture into balls and place on the baking tray. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  - >-
    Meanwhile, make the tomato sauce. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan.
    Cook the garlic clove for a few minutes. Add the passata continue to cook on
    a medium heat, stirring frequently. Stir through the basil and spinach until
    wilted. Season with salt and black pepper.
  - Serve the meatballs with tomato sauce and pasta if desired.
