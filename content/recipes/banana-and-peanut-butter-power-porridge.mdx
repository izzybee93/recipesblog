---
title: "Banana and peanut butter power porridge"
date: "2026-04-12"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/banana-and-peanut-butter-power-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml non-dairy milk or water
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds
  - '1 banana, sliced'
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
directions:
  - Cover the chia seeds with water and set aside for at least 20 minutes.
  - 'In a small pan, add the oats, chia seeds, non-dairy milk and half the banana slices. Cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently. Serve topped with hemp seeds, the remaining banana slices and a drizzle of peanut butter.'
