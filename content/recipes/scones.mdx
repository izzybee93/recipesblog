title: Scones

servings: makes 18
ingredients:
  - 180g sultanas or raisins
  - 250ml boiling water
  - 550g self-raising flour
  - 75g caster sugar
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - 115g vegan butter
  - 300ml oat milk
directions:
  - >-
    In a small bowl, add in the dried fruit then the boiling water and set
    aside.
  - Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, combine the flour, caster sugar and baking powder. Add the
    butter and rub together with your fingertips until it starts to look like
    breadcrumbs.
  - >-
    Drain the dried fruit, then stir into the flour mixture. Add the milk a
    little at a time until the mixture comes together. Add some more flour if
    the dough becomes too wet.
  - >-
    Place the dough on a floured surface and roll out to about 2cm thick. Using
    a round cutter, cut out the scones. Place on the prepared baking tray, then
    brush with milk. Bake for 20 minutes or until risen and golden.
