---
title: "Cauliflower and chickpea salad"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/cauliflower-and-chickpea-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - ½ cauliflower, chopped into small florets
  - 2 carrots, chopped
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground ginger
  - ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 120g baby spinach
  - 1 avocado, sliced
  - Small handful fresh mint, finely chopped
  - Small handful chives, finely chopped
  - 4 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  - 10 roasted almonds, chopped
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp fresh orange juice
  - 2 tsp orange zest
  - 2 tsp maple syrup
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  - In a large baking tray, add the chickpeas, cauliflower and carrots. Add the olive oil and spices and mix until coated. Bake for 25 minutes.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all the ingredients together in a small measuring jug.
  - In a large bowl, add the spinach, roasted vegetables, pomegranate seeds, herbs, and almonds. Add the dressing and gently toss to combine. Add the avocado and gently mix. Serve with extra mint leaves for garnish.
