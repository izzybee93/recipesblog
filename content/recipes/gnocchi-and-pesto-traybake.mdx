title: "Gnocchi and pesto traybake"
date: "2026-04-12"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/gnocchi-and-pesto-traybake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 250g gnocchi
  - '150g tomatoes, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp capers
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 150g tenderstem broccoli
  - 2 tbsp basil pesto (see recipe)
  - Juice of ¼ lemon
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the gnocchi, tomatoes, garlic and capers. Coat with 1½ tbsp olive oil and sprinkle with the salt. Roast for 15 minutes.'
  - Add the broccoli to the tray and coat with the remaining olive oil. Roast for 10 minutes.
  - 'Stir in the pesto, and a few tbsp water as needed, and roast for 2 more minutes.'
  - Stir in the lemon juice then serve topped with ground black pepper.
