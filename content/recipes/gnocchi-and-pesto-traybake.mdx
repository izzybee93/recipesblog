title: "Gnocchi and pesto traybake"
date: "2026-04-12"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/gnocchi-and-pesto-traybake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 280g gnocchi
  - '180g tomatoes, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp capers
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 150g tenderstem broccoli
  - ½ basil pesto (see recipe)
  - Juice of ¼ lemon
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the gnocchi, tomatoes and capers, and coat with the olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes.'
  - Add the broccoli to the tray and roast for 10 minutes.
  - Stir in the pesto and roast for 2 more minutes.
  - Stir in the lemon juice then serve topped with ground black pepper.
