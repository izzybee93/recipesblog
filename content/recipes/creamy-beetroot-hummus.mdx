---
title: "Creamy beetroot hummus"
date: "2026-04-25"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-beetroot-hummus.jpeg"
servings: "serves 6"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '150g cooked beetroot, cubed'
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - ¾ tsp ground cumin
  - 1 tsp sea salt
  - 2 tbsp water
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Combine the tahini and lemon juice in a food processor and mix for 1 minute. Scrape the sides of the bowl, then process for 30 more seconds.
  - Add the olive oil, garlic, cumin, and salt. Process for 30 seconds, scrape down the sides, then process another 30 seconds or until well blended.
  - Add the beetroot, leftover beetroot juice, and half of the beans, and process for 1 minute. Scrape the sides of the bowl, then add the remaining beans and process for another minute. Gradually add water, 1 tbsp at a time, and blend until it reaches the desired consistency.
  - Add the ground black pepper, taste for salt, and adjust as needed. Store in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 1 week.
