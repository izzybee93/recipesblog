title: Chocolate peanut butter pots
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.277Z'
categories:

  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolate-peanut-butter-pots.jpeg
servings: makes 6
ingredients:
  - 100g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids)
  - 300g silken tofu
  - 50g maple syrup
  - 1 tbsp vanilla bean paste
  - Pinch sea salt
  - '5 medjool dates, stones removed'
  - 40g smooth peanut butter
directions:
  - 'Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pan of boiling water, then remove from the heat.'
  - >-
    Line a medium bowl with a clean tea towel. Add the tofu, bunch up the tea
    towel and squeeze out the excess moisture into the bowl.
  - >-
    In a food processor, add the tofu, 40g of the maple syrup, vanilla bean paste
    and salt. Process until smooth. Pulse in the melted chocolate until fully combined, then 
    transfer to a bowl.
  - >-
    Clean out the blender and add the dates, a splash of boiling water, peanut
    butter and remaining 10g of maple syrup. Blend until totally smooth. Add
    another splash of hot water if needed and blend until smooth.
  - >-
    Spoon the peanut caramel into the bottom of small glasses and top
    with the chocolate mousse. Finish with a sprinkle of flaky sea salt or dark
    chocolate shavings if desired. Chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour before serving.
