---
title: "Chocolate, almond and chia pudding"
date: "2026-01-18"
categories: ["desserts", "treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-almond-and-chia-pudding.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 30g chia seeds
  - 30g hemp seeds
  - 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  - 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  - 15g almond butter
  - ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 150ml soya milk
  - 2 tsp cacao nibs
  - 50g blueberries, raspberries or strawberries
directions:
  - Add all ingredients except the cacao nibs and berries to a blender and blend until smooth. 
  - Pour into 2 glasses and refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Top each pudding with cacao nibs and berries, then serve.
---
