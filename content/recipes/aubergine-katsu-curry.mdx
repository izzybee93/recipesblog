---
title: "Aubergine katsu curry"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/aubergine-katsu-curry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - '1 aubergine, sliced'
  - 4 tbsp plain flour
  - 90ml water
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 100g panko breadcrumbs
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 50g pickled radishes (see recipe)
  - 50g salad leaves
  - '## Sauce'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '½ onion, chopped'
  - '1 carrot, diced'
  - '1 medium sweet potato, diced'
  - '1 garlic clove, sliced'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, minced'
  - 1 tbsp curry powder
  - 1 tbsp plain flour
  - 250ml vegetable stock
  - Pinch sea salt, to taste
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - Make the pickled radishes.
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. In a shallow bowl, mix the flour, water and salt to make a thin paste. Put the breadcrumbs on a plate. Coat the aubergine slices in the paste, shake off excess, then press into the breadcrumbs. Place on the tray, drizzle with ½ tbsp oil and bake for 30 minutes, turning halfway. Increase heat to 220°C and bake for 10 more minutes, or until crispy.'
  - 'To make the sauce, heat the oil in a large pan. Add the onion, carrots and sweet potato, and cook for 5 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for 1 more minute. Reduce the heat and cover, leaving to cook for 5 more minutes.'
  - 'Stir in the curry powder and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the flour, then add the stock and bring to the boil. Add the salt, soy sauce and ketchup. Remove from the heat and blend until smooth.'
  - 'When the aubergine is almost ready, gently reheat the sauce for 5 minutes. Assemble the bowls with rice and aubergine slices. Pour the sauce over the aubergine. Serve with the pickled radishes and salad leaves.'
