---
title: "Smoky mushroom, black bean and quinoa bowl"
date: "2026-05-07"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/smoky-mushroom-black-bean-and-quinoa-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 80g quinoa
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '200g chestnut mushrooms, sliced'
  - 100g sweetcorn
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - ½ smoky red pepper sauce (see recipe)
  - 'Small handful coriander, chopped'
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, until starting to brown. Stir in the smoked paprika.
  - Push the mushrooms to one side of the pan and add the sweetcorn. Cook for 2-3 minutes without stirring, until lightly charred. Push to the side and add the black beans. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until heated through.
  - Prepare the smoky red pepper sauce.
  - Divide the quinoa between serving bowls and top with the mushroom, sweetcorn and black beans. Drizzle with the red pepper sauce and top with fresh coriander.
