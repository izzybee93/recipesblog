---
title: "Raspberry mousse"
date: "2025-11-13"
categories: ["treats", "desserts"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/raspberry-mousse.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 300g silken tofu
  - 100g raspberries, plus a few for decoration
  - 20g icing sugar
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 2 tsp vanilla extract
directions:
  - Drain the silken tofu. In a food processor, add the tofu, lemon juice, vanilla extract and salt. Blend until smooth.
  - Add the raspberries and icing sugar. Blend until smooth.
  - Spoon into 4 glasses and chill for at least 2 hours to set.
  - Top with raspberries, whipped cream, or your favourite toppings. Chill until ready to serve.
