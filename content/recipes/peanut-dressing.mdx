servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tsp honey
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp ground ginger
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - 4 tbsp soya milk
  - 'Handful coriander, finely chopped (optional)'
directions:
  - >-
    Whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug. Serve with
    a rainbow salad or heat through to make a stir fry sauce.
