title: Chickpea and roasted vegetable salad

categories:

featured_image: /images/recipes/chickpea-and-roasted-vegetable-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - '½ aubergine, cut into cubes'
  - '½ red onion, chopped'
  - '½ red pepper, chopped'
  - 150g cherry tomatoes
  - 80g spinach
  - '4 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped'
  - 2 ½ tbsp olive oil
  - Zest and juice of ½ lemon
  - '¼ tsp garlic powder'
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp honey
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Place the aubergine, pepper and onion 
    on the tray and drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast for 15 mins. Remove from the
    oven and add the cherry tomatoes, then return to cook for another 10 mins.
  - >-
    To make the dressing, whisk the remaining olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic,
    smoked paprika, honey, chilli, salt and pepper together.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the spinach, chickpeas, sun-dried tomatoes and roasted vegetables. 
    Pour over the dressing and toss to combine until well coated. Season to taste.
