---
title: "Lemon icing"
date: "2026-04-04"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-icing.jpeg"
servings: "1 cake or 12 muffins"
ingredients:
  - 150g icing sugar
  - 2-4 tbsp lemon juice
directions:
  - In a medium bowl, mix together the sugar and 2 tbsp lemon juice until smooth. Add the remaining lemon juice until the icing reaches the desired consistency.
  - Drizzle on top of the cake, and add your favourite toppings to decorate.
---
