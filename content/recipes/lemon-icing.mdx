---
title: "Lemon icing"
date: "2026-04-04"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-icing.jpeg"
servings: "1 cake or 12 muffins"
ingredients:
  - 110g icing sugar
  - 2-3 tbsp lemon juice
directions:
  - Sift the sugar in a medium bowl. Mix with the lemon juice, 1 tbsp at a time, until smooth and the icing reaches the desired consistency.
  - Drizzle on top of the cake, and add your favourite toppings to decorate.
---
