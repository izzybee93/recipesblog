---
title: "Sesame tofu and quinoa bowl"
date: "2026-05-30"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sesame-tofu-and-quinoa-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 60g quinoa
  - 140g extra firm tofu, cubed
  - ½ cucumber
  - 6 tbsp pickled red cabbage (see recipe)
  - ½ avocado, sliced 
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - 1½ tbsp rice vinegar
  - 1 tsp maple syrup 
  - 4 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Heat a small pan over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning to brown each side.
  - Combine the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug. Add 4 tbsp dressing to the tofu and cook for 5 more minutes. 
  - Cut the cucumber into ribbons using a vegetable peeler, then place in a bowl. Pour the remaining dressing over the cucumber and toss to combine.
  - Assemble the bowls with the quinoa, then top with tofu, cucumber, cabbage and avocado. Pour over any remaining dressing and serve.
