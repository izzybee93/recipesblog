---
title: "Crunchy chocolate and cinnamon chickpea bites"
date: "2025-12-29"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crunchy-chocolate-and-cinnamon-chickpea-bites.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp cinnamon
  - 120g dark chocolate
  - '2 tbsp dried cranberries or blueberries, chopped'
  - '2 tbsp hazelnuts or almonds, chopped'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Add the chickpeas and coat with the cinnamon. Bake for 30 minutes, mixing halfway. Set aside to cool on a large plate.
  - Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pan of boiling water. Pour the chocolate over the chickpeas and mix well.
  - 'Divide into 12 portions and spoon each portion onto the cooled tray. Sprinkle with the nuts, drizzle over any leftover chocolate, then top with the fruit. Transfer to the fridge for at least 2 hours to set.'
---
