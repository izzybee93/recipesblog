---
title: "Tempeh and black bean burgers"
date: "2026-03-19"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tempeh-and-black-bean-burgers.jpeg"
servings: "makes 6"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 75g sunflower seeds or pumpkin seeds
  - '1 small onion, roughly chopped'
  - 2 garlic cloves
  - 300g tempeh
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground cumin
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - In a food processor, add the oats and seeds. Process until a powder forms.
  - Add the remaining ingredients and process until the mixture just comes together. Do not over-process. Set aside for 5 minutes to firm up slightly.
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare a baking tray. Divide the mixture into 6 portions, shape into patties and place on the tray. Bake for 25 minutes, flipping halfway.
---
