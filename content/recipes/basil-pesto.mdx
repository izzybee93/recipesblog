---
title: "Basil pesto"
date: "2025-11-08"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/basil-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8-12"
ingredients:
  - 25g fresh basil
  - 30g spinach
  - 2 tbsp mixed seeds (sunflower, pumpkin, hemp, flax)
  - 3 tbsp pine nuts, cashews or walnuts 
  - 2 garlic cloves
  - Zest of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - 1 tbsp nutritional yeast, optional
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
directions:
  - Add basil, spinach, seeds, nuts, garlic, salt, lemon zest and nutritional yeast to a food processor. Process into a coarse mixture.
  - Add the olive oil and a tbsp of water and process again. Add more water as needed, a tbsp at a time until the pesto reaches the desired consistency.
  - Taste and adjust the salt, lemon and nutritional yeast as needed. Store refrigerated for up to 5 days. Freeze small portions in ice cube trays.
---
