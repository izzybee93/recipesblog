---
title: Miso veggie burgers
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.262Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/black-bean-burgers.jpeg
servings: makes 4
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can black beans or kidney beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 large garlic clove, crushed'
  - '½ red onion, chopped'
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - 1 tsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 40g rolled oats
  - 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Place the beans in a medium bowl and mash
    well with a fork.
  - >-
    In a medium pan, heat ½ tbsp olive oil and sauté the onions for 3 minutes.
    Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - >-
    Add the sautéd onion and garlic to the mashed beans, together with the
    spices, ½ tbsp olive oil, soy sauce, miso paste, apple cider vinegar, oats and pumpkin seeds. Combine well,
    adding a splash of water if needed. Shape into 4 patties. Bake for 30 minutes, until crispy.
