---
title: Berry and cinnamon smoothie bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.260Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/berry-and-cinnamon-smoothie-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g frozen blueberries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 3 tbsp oats
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - 50ml plant milk
  - '25g strawberries, sliced'
  - 25g raspberries
  - 1 tbsp granola
  - 1 hemp seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the berries, nut butter, chia seeds, oats, cinnamon and plant milk until smooth
    and creamy. Transfer to a bowl and top with strawberries, raspberries, granola and hemp
    seeds.
---
