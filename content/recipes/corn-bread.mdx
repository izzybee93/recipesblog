---
title: "Corn bread"
date: "2026-03-17"
categories:
  - bread
featured_image: "/images/recipes/corn-bread.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 loaf"
ingredients:
  - 120g plain flour
  - 150g cornmeal
  - 30g caster sugar
  - 1 tbsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - 2 tbsp ground flaxseed
  - 6 tbsp water
  - 65ml vegetable oil
  - 300ml oat milk
directions:
  - Whisk together the flaxseed and water in a small bowl and set aside for 15 minutes to thicken.
  - Preheat oven to 160°C and prepare a loaf tin.
  - 'In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder and salt.'
  - Add the flax eggs, oil and oat milk. Mix until just combined.
  - 'Pour into the tin and smooth the top. Bake for 25-35 minutes, until golden and a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean.'
  - Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
