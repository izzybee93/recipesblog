---
title: "Beetroot spread"
date: "2026-01-23"
categories: ["snacks"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-spread.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 150g cooked beetroot, chopped
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 2 ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 200g firm or silken tofu
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 3-4 tbsp water
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C and prepare a baking tray. Add the beetroot and garlic, drizzle with ½ tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes.
  - Add the tofu, beetroot, garlic, tahini, lemon juice, 2 tbsp olive oil, sea salt, and black pepper to a food processor. Add the water if using firm tofu. Process for 5 minutes until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed. Add another tbsp water if needed and process until smooth.
  - Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving. Spread on toasted sourdough or crackers.
