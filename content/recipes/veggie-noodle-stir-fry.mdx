servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 2 nests dry rice noodles
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '120g edamame, cooked'
  - 2 medium carrots
  - 1 large courgette
  - >-
    Veggies can be swapped for any such as pepper, broccoli, corn, cauliflower,
    beansprouts
  - 4 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - Juice of Â½ lime
  - 1 tsp honey
  - '1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, minced'
  - 125ml water
  - 1 tsp cornflour
  - 'Handful of fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - 'Cook the rice noodles. Drain and rinse, then set aside.'
  - 'Slice the carrot and courgette into long strips, using a peeler.'
  - >-
    In a small jug, add the soy sauce, lime, honey, ginger and water, and stir.
    Add the cornflour and mix well.
  - >-
    In a large wok, heat the sesame oil. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    Add the edamame and carrots, and stir frequently, followed by the courgette.
    Add the sauce and cook until slightly thickened. Stir in the coriander.
  - Add the noodles and toss until combined.
  - Serve topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
