title: Veggie noodle stir fry
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.318Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/veggie-noodle-stir-fry.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 100g rice noodles
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '120g edamame, cooked'
  - '## Any 2 veggies:'
  - 2 medium carrots, chopped
  - 1 large courgette, chopped
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 150g baby corn, chopped
  - 150g beansprouts
  - 150g frozen peas
  - ¼ broccoli, chopped into florets
  - ¼ cauliflower, chopped into florets
  - '## Sauce'
  - 4 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 1 tsp honey
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, minced'
  - 125ml water
  - 1 tsp cornflour
  - '## Garnish'
  - 'Handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - 'Cook the rice noodles. Drain and rinse, then set aside.'
  - >-
    In a small jug, add the soy sauce, lime, honey, ginger and water, and stir.
    Add the cornflour and mix well.
  - >-
    In a large wok, heat the sesame oil. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    Add the edamame and veggies, and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  - Add the sauce and cook until slightly thickened. Stir in the coriander.
  - Add the noodles and toss until combined. Serve topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
