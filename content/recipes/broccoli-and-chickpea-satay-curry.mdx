---
title: "Broccoli and chickpea satay curry"
date: "2026-03-22"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/broccoli-and-chickpea-satay-curry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, peeled and minced'
  - '15g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves separated'
  - ½ tsp curry powder
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 120ml vegetable stock
  - '½ broccoli, cut into florets'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
  - 120g spinach
  - 120g basmati rice
  - Juice of 1 lime
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - "In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, ginger and finely chopped coriander stalks and cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant."
  - "Stir in the curry powder, chilli flakes and salt. Cook for 1 minute, then add the tomato paste and cook for another minute to deepen the flavour. Pour in the vegetable stock and stir well."
  - "Add the broccoli and chickpeas. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes, until the broccoli is tender."
  - Stir in the peanut butter.
  - "Add the spinach and stir through until wilted. Remove from the heat and stir in the lime juice."
  - Sprinkle with the coriander leaves and serve with the rice.
