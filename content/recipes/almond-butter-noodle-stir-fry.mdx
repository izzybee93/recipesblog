title: Almond butter noodle stir fry

featured_image: /images/recipes/almond-butter-noodle-stir-fry.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 100g rice noodles
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '100g tenderstem broccoli, chopped'
  - '1 red pepper, chopped'
  - '100g chestnut mushrooms, sliced'
  - '## Sauce'
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1½ tbsp almond butter
  - 50ml plant milk
  - ½ tsp maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
directions:
  - >-
    Cook the rice noodles. Drain and rinse, then set aside.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the sesame oil over a medium heat. Add the garlic and
    cook for 1 minute. Add the peppers and broccoli and cook for 5 minutes.
    Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-10 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - >-
    To make the sauce, add all the ingredients to a jug and whisk to combine.
  - >-
    Add the noodles to the pan and pour over the sauce. Toss until evenly coated
    and the sauce has thickened.
