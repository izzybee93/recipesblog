title: Rocket and tomato couscous salad
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.302Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/rocket-and-tomato-couscous-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 2-3
ingredients:
  - 100g couscous
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 2 tbsp sundried tomato paste
  - '2 large tomatoes, chopped'
  - '¼ large cucumber, chopped'
  - '8 olives, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 large handfuls rocket
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the couscous and boiled water, pouring until the water
    is 0.5cm above the couscous. Set aside for 10 mins.
  - >-
    Using a fork, loosen the couscous. Add the garlic, turmeric, salt and pepper
    then mix well.
  - >-
    Mix in the tomatoes, cucumber and olives. Add the rocket and olive oil, then
    gently mix. Serve cold.
