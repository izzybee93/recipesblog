---
title: "Creamy spinach and basil butter beans"
date: "2026-04-27"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-spinach-and-basil-butter-beans.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 courgette, chopped'
  - '1 400g can butter beans, drained and rinsed'
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 60g baby spinach
  - '2 tbsp pine nuts, toasted'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - '## Sauce'
  - 150g silken tofu
  - 100ml water
  - 30g nutritional yeast
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Zest of ¼ lemon
  - 20g fresh basil
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and courgette, and cook for 5 more minutes.
  - To make the sauce, set aside a few basil leaves, then add all the sauce ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  - Add the chilli flakes, salt, and butter beans to the pan. Stir in the sauce and cook for 5-10 minutes, until heated through and thickened slightly. Stir in the spinach and remaining basil, and cook over low heat until wilted.
  - Serve topped with pine nuts and ground black pepper.
