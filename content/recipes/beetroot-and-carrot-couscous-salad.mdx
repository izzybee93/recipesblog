---
title: Beetroot and carrot couscous salad
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.257Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/beetroot-and-carrot-couscous-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 3
ingredients:
  - 150g couscous
  - '2 large beetroot, cooked and chopped'
  - '1 large carrot, grated'
  - 150g edamame, cooked
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ~300ml vegetable stock
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - Small handful parsley, chopped
  - Small handful dill, chopped
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the couscous then vegetable stock, pouring until the water is 0.5cm above the couscous. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  - Using a fork, loosen the couscous. Add the garlic, salt, lemon juice, leftover beetroot juice, herbs and oil and mix well. Add the carrot, beetroot, edamame and chickpeas, then mix together. Add a drizzle more olive oil, season with black pepper and stir again. Serve chilled.
