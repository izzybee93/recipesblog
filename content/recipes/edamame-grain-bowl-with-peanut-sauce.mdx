---
title: "Edamame grain bowl with peanut sauce"
date: "2026-07-21"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/edamame-grain-bowl-with-peanut-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice or quinoa
  - '150g edamame, cooked'
  - '½ cucumber, diced'
  - '½ red pepper, finely sliced'
  - 6 tbsp pickled red cabbage (see recipe)
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - Peanut dressing (see recipe)
  - '4 tbsp roasted peanuts, roughly chopped'
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the rice or quinoa.
  - Make the peanut dressing. Add to a small pan and heat over medium-low heat until slightly thickened.
  - Assemble the bowls with rice or quinoa and top with the edamame, cucumber, red pepper, red cabbage and coriander.
  - Drizzle over the peanut sauce and top with the roasted peanuts and sesame seeds.
