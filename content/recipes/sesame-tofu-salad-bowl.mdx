categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/sesame-tofu-salad-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '225g extra firm tofu, cubed'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - '2 large carrots, grated'
  - 80g sugar snap peas
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - Sesame salad dressing (see recipe)
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Fry the tofu on a medium-high heat
    for a few minutes. Add the soy sauce and continue to cook, turning
    occasionally to brown each side.
  - >-
    Make the dressing.
  - >-
    Assemble the salads, drizzle with the dressing, and sprinkle with sesame
    seeds.
