---
title: "Banana and chocolate chip cookies"
date: "2026-03-18"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/banana-and-chocolate-chip-cookies.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 70g plant butter
  - 160g plain flour
  - ½ tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp fine salt
  - 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  - ¼ tsp nutmeg
  - ½ tsp ground ginger
  - 1 overripe banana
  - 30g caster sugar
  - 50g soft brown sugar
  - 1½ tsp vanilla extract
  - '80g dark chocolate, chopped'
directions:
  - "Melt the butter in a pan over low heat, then set aside to cool."
  - "In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt and spices."
  - "In a large bowl, mash the banana. Add the sugar and mix well. Stir in the melted butter and vanilla extract and mix well."
  - "Gradually add the dry ingredients, mixing gently. Fold in the chocolate chips. Cover and refrigerate the dough overnight."
  - "Preheat oven to 180°C and prepare a large baking tray. Spoon the dough into 12 equal portions, place on the tray and flatten slightly. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden."
  - "Leave to cool on the tray for 10 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool completely."
