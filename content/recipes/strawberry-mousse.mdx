---
title: "Strawberry mousse"
date: "2026-05-30"
categories:
  - desserts
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/strawberry-mousse.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 300g silken tofu
  - 100g strawberries
  - 20g icing sugar
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 2 tsp vanilla extract
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Drain the silken tofu. In a food processor, add the tofu, lemon juice, vanilla extract and salt. Blend until smooth.
  - Add the strawberries and icing sugar, reserving a few strawberry slices for topping. Blend until smooth.
  - Spoon into 4 glasses and chill for at least 2 hours to set.
  - Top with strawberry slices, whipped cream, or your favourite toppings. Chill until ready to serve.
---
