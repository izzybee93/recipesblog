title: "Mediterranean vegetable, chickpea and hummus bowl"
date: "2026-04-09"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mediterranean-vegetable-chickpea-and-hummus-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '¼ cauliflower head, chopped into florets'
  - '1 pepper, chopped'
  - '½ red onion, quartered'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - '8 olives, chopped'
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - '100g baby plum tomatoes, chopped'
  - 4 tbsp hummus (see recipe)
  - '2 pitta breads, warmed'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the cauliflower, pepper, chickpeas, and onions. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle over the spices, and mix well until coated. Roast for 20-25 minutes.
  - Make the hummus.
  - To assemble the bowls, add the roasted veggies, cucumber, tomatoes and olives. Serve with a few tbsp hummus and pitta bread.
