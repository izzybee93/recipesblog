---
title: 'Pasta with tomato, courgette and artichoke'
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.295Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: /images/recipes/pasta-with-tomato-courgette-and-artichoke.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 180g pasta
  - '½ red onion, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 large courgette, chopped'
  - '60g artichoke hearts, cooked and chopped'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 'Sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - Small handful fresh basil
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the oil. Add the onion and cook for a few minutes,
    followed by the garlic and courgette. Cook for a few minutes until the onion
    starts to soften.
  - 'Meanwhile, cook the pasta.'
  - >-
    Add the tomato paste and chopped tomatoes and continue to cook on a medium
    heat for 5 minutes. Add the artichoke and cook for another 5 minutes, until
    heated through and the sauce has thickened. Season to taste.
  - >-
    Drain the pasta. Add to the sauce and toss together until coated and heated
    through. Remove from the heat, then stir in the basil. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
