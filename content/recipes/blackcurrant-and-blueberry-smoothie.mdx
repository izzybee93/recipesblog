---
title: "Blackcurrant and blueberry smoothie"
date: "2026-07-16"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/blackcurrant-and-blueberry-smoothie.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 75g blackcurrants
  - 75g blueberries
  - 4 tbsp oats
  - '2 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 200ml plant milk
  - ½ tsp lemon zest (optional)
directions:
  - Place all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
