---
title: "Strawberry, beetroot and almond butter smoothie"
date: "2026-06-29"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/strawberry-beetroot-and-almond-butter-smoothie.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 60g beetroot
  - 150g strawberries
  - 4 tbsp oats
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
  - '2 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 200ml soy milk
  - 2 drops vanilla extract
directions:
  - Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
---
