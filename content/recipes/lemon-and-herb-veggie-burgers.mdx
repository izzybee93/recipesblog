---
title: "Lemon and herb veggie burgers"
date: "2026-03-21"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-and-herb-veggie-burgers.jpeg"
servings: "makes 6"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 medium carrot
  - 1 small courgette
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - Zest of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 40g rolled oats
  - 2 tbsp sunflower seeds
  - '2 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped'
  - '1 tbsp fresh dill or mint, finely chopped'
  - ½ tsp dried oregano
  - ½ tsp sea salt
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 190°C. Prepare a baking tray with parchment paper."
  - "Grate the carrot and courgette, then squeeze out excess moisture using a tea towel."
  - "Place the beans in a large bowl and mash well with a fork. Add the grated vegetables, garlic, lemon juice and zest, olive oil, mustard, oats, seeds, herbs and salt. Combine well and shape into 6 patties. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until crispy, flipping halfway."
  - "If freezing, slightly underbake the burgers. Freeze flat and then transfer to an airtight container."
