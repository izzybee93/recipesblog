---
title: "Green herb hummus"
date: "2025-12-29"
categories: ["snacks"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/green-herb-hummus.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - 1 400g tin chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 4 tbsp tahini
  - 15g chives, finely chopped
  - 15g parsley, finely chopped
  - 25g basil, roughly chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 125ml water
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Combine the tahini and lemon juice in a food processor and mix for 1 minute. Scrape the sides of the bowl then process for 30 more seconds.
  - Add the remaining ingredients and process for a few minutes until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
  - Serve with a sprinkle of leftover chopped herbs.
