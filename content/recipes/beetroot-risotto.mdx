---
title: "Beetroot risotto"
date: "2026-01-23"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-risotto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 large beetroots, cooked and chopped
  - 1 onion, finely chopped
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 2 tbsp olive oil 
  - 140g risotto rice
  - 800ml vegetable stock
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast 
  - 2 tsp maple syrup 
  - 50ml soya milk 
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - Small handful fresh parsley, chopped
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - 2 tbsp hazelnuts or walnuts, chopped and toasted
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C and prepare a baking tray. Add half the beetroot, drizzle with ½ tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes.
  - In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Add the onions and cook for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - Add the rice and cook for 2 minutes. Gradually add the stock and cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring frequently.
  - In a small bowl, add the remaining beetroot, lemon juice, ½ tbsp olive oil, maple syrup, nutritional yeast, soya milk and sea salt. Blend until smooth.
  - Once the rice is almost cooked, stir in the beetroot mixture and cook for 5 minutes. Add the roasted beetroot, reserving a handful for topping, and cook for 1 more minute. Stir in the parsley and ground black pepper.
  - Serve topped with the remaining beetroot and toasted hazelnuts.
