---
title: "Beetroot risotto"
date: "2026-01-23"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-risotto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 large beetroots, cooked and chopped
  - ½ onion, finely chopped
  - 1 garlic, peeled & chopped 
  - 2 tbsp olive oil 
  - 100g risotto rice
  - 800ml vegetable stock
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast 
  - 1 tsp maple syrup 
  - 50ml soya milk 
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - Small handful of parsley, chopped
  - 2 tbsp hazelnuts or walnuts, chopped and toasted
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C and prepare a baking tray. Add half the beetroot, drizzle with ½ tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes.
  - In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Add the onions and cook for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - Add the rice cook for 2 minutes. Gradually add the stock and cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring frequently.
  - Add the remaining beetroot to a blender, along with the nutritional yeast, ½ tbsp olive oil, maple syrup, soya milk, lemon juice and sea salt. Blend until a smooth paste forms.
  - Once the rice is almost cooked, stir in the beetroot paste and cook for 5 minutes. Stir the roasted beetroot, reserving a small handful for topping and cook for 1 more minute. Stir in the parsley. 
  - Serve topped with the remaining beetroot, toasted hazelnuts and ground black pepper.
