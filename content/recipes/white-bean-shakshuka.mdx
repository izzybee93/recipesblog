---
title: "White bean shakshuka"
date: "2026-03-28"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/white-bean-shakshuka.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 small red onion, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 red bell pepper, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - '1 400g can butter beans or cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 50g spinach
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, chopped'
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Stir in the spices and tomato paste. Add the pepper and cook for 5 more minutes.
  - Stir in the beans and tomatoes. Simmer for 5 minutes, then stir in the spinach and cook until wilted. Stir in the coriander, reserving a few leaves for garnish. Cover with a lid and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  - Divide between serving bowls, top with coriander leaves and serve with toasted crusty bread.
