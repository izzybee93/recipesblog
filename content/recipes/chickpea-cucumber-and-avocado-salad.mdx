  - 1 avocado, chopped

  - 2 tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped 
  - 1 tbsp chives, finely chopped
  - Creamy Dijon dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the chickpeas, cucumber, radishes, walnuts, dill and chives.
  - Pour the dressing over the salad and gently mix. Add the avocado and gently mix again. Serve chilled, topped with fresh dill for garnish.
