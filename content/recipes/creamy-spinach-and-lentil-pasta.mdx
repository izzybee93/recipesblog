---
title: "Creamy spinach and lentil pasta"
date: "2026-05-06"
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-spinach-and-lentil-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 150g pasta
  - '1 400g can brown or green lentils, drained and rinsed'
  - Zest of ½ lemon
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - '## Sauce'
  - 160g spinach
  - 150g silken tofu
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 10g fresh basil
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  - Pour boiling water over the spinach until wilted. Add all the sauce ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth. Season to taste.
  - In a large pan, add the sauce and a few tbsp pasta water as needed. Stir over medium-low heat until smooth and slightly thickened. Add the lentils and stir until heated through. Add the pasta and toss to combine.
  - Serve topped with lemon zest and ground black pepper.
