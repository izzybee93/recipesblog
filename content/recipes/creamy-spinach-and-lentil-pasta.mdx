---
title: "Creamy spinach and lentil pasta"
date: "2026-05-06"
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-spinach-and-lentil-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 150g pasta
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 150g mushrooms, sliced
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 120g brown or green lentils
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tsp lemon zest
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - '## Sauce'
  - 100g spinach
  - 150g silken tofu
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 10g fresh basil
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, until lightly browned. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  - Pour boiling water over the spinach until wilted. Add all the sauce ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  - Add the sauce to the pan over medium-low heat, and stir until smooth and slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp pasta water as needed. Add the lentils and stir until heated through. Stir in the lemon juice.
  - Add the pasta and toss to combine. Serve topped with lemon zest and ground black pepper.
---
