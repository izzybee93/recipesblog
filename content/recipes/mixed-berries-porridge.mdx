---
title: "Mixed berries porridge"
date: "2026-02-22"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mixed-berries-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml non-dairy milk or water
  - 2 tbsp blueberries
  - 2 tbsp blackcurrants
  - 2 tbsp raspberries
  - 1 large strawberry, sliced
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
directions:
  - Add the oats and milk to the pan and cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently until the juices start to release. Add the blueberries and blackcurrants, and continue to cook until heated through.
  - Serve topped with the raspberries, strawberries and hemp seeds.
---
