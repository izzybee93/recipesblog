---
title: "Super green soup"
date: "2026-01-23"
categories: ["mains", "soup"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-soup.jpeg"
servings: "serves 5"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 onion, chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  - 1 tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 1L vegetable stock
  - 1 large broccoli head, chopped
  - 240g frozen peas
  - 150ml soya milk
  - 180g spinach
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - Handful fresh coriander
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger, spices and salt, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  - Add the broccoli and cook for 5 minutes, stiring occasionally. Add the vegetable stock, cover with a lid and cook for 10 minutes, until the broccoli starts to soften. Add the peas and cook for 5 more minutes. Stir in the soya milk.
  - Reduce to a low heat and add the spinach, then cover for a few minutes until wilted.
  - Stir in the lime juice and coriander and blend until smooth. Season to taste, and serve topped with ground black pepper.
