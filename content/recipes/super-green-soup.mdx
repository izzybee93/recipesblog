---
title: "Super green soup"
date: "2026-01-23"
categories: ["mains", "soup"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-soup.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 large white onion, chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 1 thumb sized piece of ginger, minced
  - 1 tsp ground turmeric
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 1 litre vegetable stock
  - 250ml soya milk
  - 1 large broccoli head, chopped
  - 240g frozen peas
  - 200g spinach
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - Handful fresh coriander
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 mins. Add the garlic, ginger, spices and salt, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  - Add the broccoli, vegetable stock and soya milk. Cook for 5 mins until the broccoli starts to soften. Add the peas and cook for 5 more minutes.
  - Turn off the heat and add the spinach, then place a lid on the pan for a few minutes to allow it to wilt.
  - Stir in the lime juice and coriander and blend until smooth. Season to taste, and serve topped with ground black pepper.
