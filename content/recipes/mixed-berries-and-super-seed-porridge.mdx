---
title: "Mixed berries and super seed porridge"
date: "2026-02-22"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mixed-berries-and-super-seed-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water'
  - 20g blueberries
  - 20g blackcurrants
  - 20g raspberries
  - 20g strawberries, sliced
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 1/2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  - 1/2 tbsp sunflower seeds
directions:
  - Add the oats, milk and chia seeds to the pan and cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently until the juices start to release. Add the blueberries and blackcurrants, and continue to cook until heated through.
  - Serve topped with the raspberries, strawberries and seeds.
