---
title: "Burmese tofu"
date: "2025-11-09"
categories: ["grains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/burmese-tofu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 6"
ingredients:
  - 120g chickpea flour or besan flour 
  - 1 tsp sea salt
  - Â¼ tsp turmeric,
  - 720ml water, divided
directions:
  - Lightly oil a 20x20cm baking dish.
  - In a medium bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour, salt and turmeric. Add half the water and whisk until smooth.
  - Add the other half of the water to a saucepan and bring to boil. Reduce the heat to medium-high and add the mixture. Whisk over the heat until the mixture becomes thick and glossy, for about 8 minutes.
  - Pour into the prepared baking dish and leave to cool at room temperature for 2 hours. It will get firmer if left for a longer time.
  - Cut into cubes or slices. Store in an airtight container and use within 3 days. Alternatively, freeze the cubes separated on a baking tray, then transfer to an airtight container and store for up to 3 months.
