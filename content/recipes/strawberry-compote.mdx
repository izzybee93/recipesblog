---
title: "Strawberry compote"
date: "2026-02-26"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/strawberry-compote.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 small jar"
ingredients:
  - '250g strawberries, chopped'
  - 30g caster sugar
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 tsp lemon zest
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - 'In a pan, combine the strawberries, sugar, salt, lemon juice and lemon zest. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.'
  - 'Reduce the heat and leave to simmer for 5-10 more minutes, until slightly thickened.'
  - 'Serve warm with pancakes, or leave to cool and thicken. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 2 months.'
---
