---
title: "Tomato risotto"
date: "2026-04-13"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tomato-risotto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '½ celery stick, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 200g tomatoes
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 150g risotto rice
  - 180g passata
  - 300ml vegetable stock
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - Small handful fresh basil
directions:
  - 'Heat the oil in a large pan over medium-low heat. Add the onion and celery and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, then stir in the tomatoes, chilli and salt.'
  - 'Add the rice and cook for 3 minutes, stirring until the grains are toasted and translucent.'
  - 'Stir in the passata and stock. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.'
  - 'Stir in the balsamic vinegar, half the basil and ground black pepper. Serve topped with a few fresh basil leaves.'
---
