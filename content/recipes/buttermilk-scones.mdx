---
title: Buttermilk scones
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.265Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/buttermilk-scones.jpeg
servings: makes 16
ingredients:
  - 550g self-raising flour
  - 80g caster sugar
  - 115g plant butter
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - 1½ tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 300ml plant milk
  - Soya milk for brushing
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line two large baking trays with paper.
  - >-
    Add the apple cider vinegar to a measuring jug, then the milk. Set aside
    to form the buttermilk.
  - >-
    Mix the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and sugar in a bowl. Add
    the butter and mix it in with your fingers until it starts to look a bit
    like breadcrumbs. 
  - Add the buttermilk a little at a time until the mixture comes together. Add a little more flour if
    the dough becomes too wet.
  - >-
    Place the dough on a floured surface and roll out to about 2cm thick. Using
    a round cutter, cut out the scones. Place on the tray and brush with soya milk.
    Bake for 20 minutes or until risen and golden.
