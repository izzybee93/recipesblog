---
title: "Balsamic butter beans with tofu cream"
date: "2026-06-17"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/balsamic-butter-beans-with-tofu-cream.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '150g cherry tomatoes, halved'
  - '8 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - '1 400g can butter beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 150ml vegetable stock
  - 'Small handful fresh parsley, chopped'
  - '## Tofu cream'
  - 150g firm tofu
  - 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 80ml water
directions:
  - Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5-8 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Add the tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Stir in the sun-dried tomatoes, tomato paste and balsamic vinegar and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the butter beans and vegetable stock, then simmer for 15 minutes over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  - To make the tofu cream, add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  - Assemble the bowls with the butter beans and drizzle over the tofu cream. Top with fresh parsley and serve with toasted sourdough.
