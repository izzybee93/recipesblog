title: Harissa roasted vegetable and quinoa bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.285Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/harissa-roasted-vegetable-and-quinoa-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 80g quinoa
  - 1 400g can chickpeas or green lentils, drained and rinsed
  - '2 bell peppers, chopped'
  - '½ broccoli, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tsp harissa paste
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Small handful parsley, chopped
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tbsp toasted almonds, chopped
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare large baking tray. Add the peppers, broccoli and chickpeas, and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and 1 tsp harissa paste. Roast for 15-20 minutes.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the quinoa. Add turmeric, paprika, salt, lemon juice, 1 tsp harissa paste. Mix well, then stir in the parsely, extra virgin olive oil and roasted vegetables. Serve topped with toasted almonds.
