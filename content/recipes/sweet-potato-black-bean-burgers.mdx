---
title: "Sweet potato black bean burgers"
date: "2026-01-01"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sweet-potato-black-bean-burgers.jpeg"
servings: "makes 4"
ingredients:
  - 1 medium sweet potato, chopped
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed
  - 20g oats
  - 30g walnuts
  - ½ onion, finely chopped
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 30g plain flour
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two large baking trays with paper. Bake the sweet potato for 20 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  - In a food processor, process the oats and walnuts to create a flour. Add all the remaining ingredients and process until a smooth paste forms. Add flour or water as needed to reach a sticky consistency.
  - Divide into 4 portions and shape each burger on the baking tray. Bake for 30 minutes, flipping halfway.
  - Serve in a bun with lettuce, tomatoes, and your favourite sauces.
