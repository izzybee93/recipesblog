---
title: "Peanut butter icing"
date: "2026-06-20"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/peanut-butter-icing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1 layer cake"
ingredients:
  - 75g smooth peanut butter
  - 15g maple syrup
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 4-8 tbsp plant milk
directions:
  - Add all the ingredients to a bowl and beat together until smooth. Add more milk as needed and mix until the icing reaches desired consistency.
