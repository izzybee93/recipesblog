title: Vegetable chickpea bowl with tahini sauce
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.316Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/vegetable-chickpea-buddha-bowl.jpeg
servings: 2
ingredients:
  - '100g rice or quinoa, rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 garlic clove, crushed'
  - >-
    2-3 veggies, chopped, from aubergine, baby corn, brocollini, carrot,
    courgette, mushrooms
  - 2 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - Tahini sauce (see recipe)
directions:
  - Cook the rice or quinoa.
  - >-
    In a frying pan, add the chickpeas and spices. Toss together and cook on a
    medium-high heat until slightly crispy.
  - >-
    In another frying pan, heat the sesame oil, then add the garlic and cook for
    1 minute. Add the veggies and cook for 5 minutes. Add the soy sauce and
    continue to cook until slightly softened.
  - >-
    Divide the rice or quinoa between 2 bowls. Add the veggies and chickpeas.
    Serve with a drizzle of tahini sauce.
