---
title: "Roasted aubergine and tomato gnocchi"
date: "2026-05-28"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-aubergine-and-tomato-gnocchi.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '2 medium aubergines, cubed'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 300g passata
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 'Handful fresh basil, chopped'
  - ½ tsp maple syrup
  - Pinch chilli flakes (optional)
  - 300g gnocchi
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Place the aubergine on the tray, brush with the olive oil, and roast for 30 minutes, until softened.'
  - Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
  - 'Add the passata and 150ml water. Stir in the chickpeas, maple syrup, chilli flakes and salt. Cover with a lid and simmer for 15-20 minutes.'
  - 'Add the gnocchi, put the lid back on and simmer for 5 minutes until al dente.'
  - Stir through the basil and aubergine. Season to taste and top with ground black pepper.
