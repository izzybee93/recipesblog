---
title: "Chickpea flour cookies"
date: "2026-02-05"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chickpea-flour-cookies.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 55g vegan butter
  - 85g light brown soft sugar
  - 4 tbsp aquafaba or 1 small banana, mashed
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 4 tbsp soya milk
  - 160g chickpea flour or 140g besan flour
  - ½ teaspoon baking powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 60g dark chocolate, chopped
directions:
  - In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until fluffy. Mix in the aquafaba, vanilla and milk.
  - In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and salt. Add the dry ingredients to the wet and mix in until well combined. Add the chocolate and stir through.
  - Place the dough in the fridge for at least 30 mins before shaping.
  - Preheat the oven to 180 °C. Line a large baking tray with parchment paper.
  - Divide the dough into 12 balls. Place each ball on the baking tray, 3cm apart. Press the top of each ball to flatten slightly.
  - Bake for 10-15 minutes until golden brown. Leave to cool on the tray for 10 minutes then transfer to a wire rack.
