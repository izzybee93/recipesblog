---
title: "Chickpea flour cookies"
date: "2026-02-05"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chickpea-flour-cookies.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 160g chickpea flour or 210g besan flour
  - ½ teaspoon baking powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 120g caster sugar
  - 120g vegan butter, melted
  - 4 tbsp apple sauce or 1 small banana, mashed
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 80g dark chocolate, chopped
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 350 180 °C. Line a large baking tray with parchment paper.
  - In a large bowl, add the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Whisk together until no lumps remain.
  - Stir in the butter, apple sauce or banana and vanilla extract. Fold in chocolate chips.
  - Divide the dough into 12 balls. Place each ball on the baking tray, 3cm apart. Press the top of each ball to flatten slightly.
  - Bake for 8-12 minutes until golden brown. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
