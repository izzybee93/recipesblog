---
title: "Sesame tofu dip"
date: "2026-04-13"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sesame-tofu-dip.jpeg"
servings: "serves 6"
ingredients:
  - '300g silken tofu, drained'
  - 1½ tbsp tahini
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tsp lemon juice
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - '1 tbsp chives, dill or parsley, finely chopped (optional)'
  - 1 tbsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - Heat a small pan over medium-high heat and toast the sesame seeds for 1 minute, until golden.
  - Add the tofu, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic and salt to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Stir in the herbs. Chill in the fridge for 1 hour before serving.
  - Top with sesame seeds. Serve with your favourite veggies, pitta or drizzle over falafel.
---
