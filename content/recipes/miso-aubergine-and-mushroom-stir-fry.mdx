---
title: "Miso aubergine and mushroom stir fry"
date: "2026-07-10"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/miso-aubergine-and-mushroom-stir-fry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - 2 tbsp sesame oil
  - '200g firm tofu, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 large aubergine, chopped'
  - 50ml water
  - '300g mushrooms, sliced'
  - 60g spinach
  - Miso and ginger stir fry sauce (see recipe)
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - In a medium pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add the tofu and soy sauce. Toss to coat the tofu and cook on a medium heat for 5 minutes. Turn the tofu over to brown each side and cook for another 5 minutes.
  - In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add the aubergine and cook for 5 minutes, until lightly browned. Add the water, cover and leave to steam for 5 minutes over medium-low heat, until softened.
  - Make the miso stir fry sauce.
  - Remove the lid, then add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 3-5 minutes until lightly browned. Add the sauce and cook for another 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Stir in the tofu until heated through. Add the spinach and stir until wilted.
  - Serve with the rice and top with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
---
