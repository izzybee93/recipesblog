categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/blueberry-crunch-smoothie-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, sliced'
  - 25g frozen blueberries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - 75ml plant milk
  - 2 tbsp spelt flakes
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, blueberries, peanut butter, chia seeds, oats and plant milk until
    smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with spelt flakes and hemp seeds.
