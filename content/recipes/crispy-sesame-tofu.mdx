---
title: "Crispy sesame tofu"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crispy-sesame-tofu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '280g extra firm tofu, cubed'
  - ½ tbsp sesame oil
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp maple syrup
  - 1 tbsp rice vinegar or lime juice
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 1 tbsp cornflour
  - 2 tsp toasted sesame seeds
directions:
  - Place the tofu in a shallow dish.
  - 'In a small bowl, whisk together all the marinade ingredients except the sesame seeds. Pour over the tofu, cover and transfer to the fridge for 2-12 hours.'
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Drain off any excess marinade, then toss the tofu with the cornflour until lightly coated. Add to the baking tray and bake for 30-35 minutes, until golden and crispy. Alternatively, cook in an air fryer for 14 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.'
  - 'Serve topped with the toasted sesame seeds, alongside stir-fried veggies, rice, noodles, salad or your favourite sauce.'
