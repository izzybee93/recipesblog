---
title: "Teriyaki tempeh bowl"
date: "2026-01-26"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/teriyaki-tempeh-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g tempeh, sliced 
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tsp lime juice
  - 50g quinoa or rice
  - 50g edamame, cooked
  - 1 small beetroot, chopped
  - Â¼ cucumber, chopped
  - 3 radishes, sliced
  - 2-4 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - In a shallow dish, add the tempeh, sesame oil, soy sauce and lime juice. Mix well and leave to marinate in the fridge for 2 hours.
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa or rice.
  - In a small pan over medium-high heat, cook the tempeh for 2 minutes on each side until browned.
  - Assemble the bowl with the quinoa or rice, topped with tempeh, edamame, cucumber, beetroot and radish. Serve with a drizzle of teriyaki sauce and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
