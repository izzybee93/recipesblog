---
title: "Tempeh rice bowl with hoisin sauce"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tempeh-rice-bowl-with-hoisin-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - ¼ small red cabbage, shredded
  - '2 carrots, chopped'
  - '2 large radishes, finely sliced'
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, finely chopped'
  - '200g tempeh, chopped'
  - '120g edamame, cooked'
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
  - '## Sauce'
  - 4 tsp hoisin sauce
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tsp rice vinegar
  - ½ tsp maple syrup
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - To make the sauce, whisk together all the ingredients in a small bowl.
  - Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add the tempeh and brush with ⅓ of the sauce. Cook for 3-4 minutes then flip the tempeh, brush with another ⅓ of the sauce and cook for a further 3-4 minutes.
  - To assemble the bowls, add the rice, followed by the tempeh, edamame, veggies and a drizzle of the remaining sauce. Serve topped with sesame seeds.
