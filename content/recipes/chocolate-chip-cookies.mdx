title: Chocolate chip cookies
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.275Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolate-chip-cookies.jpeg
servings: makes 12 cookies
ingredients:
  - 55g vegan block butter
  - 75g light brown soft sugar
  - 30g caster sugar
  - ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  - 3 tbsp soya milk
  - 150g plain flour
  - ½ tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  - '60g dark chocolate, chopped'
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the butter, brown sugar and caster sugar and cream
    together until fluffy. Mix in the vanilla bean paste and milk.
  - >-
    In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and salt. Add the dry
    ingredients to the wet and mix in until well combined. Add the chocolate and
    stir through.
  - Place the dough in the fridge for at least 30 mins before shaping.
  - >-
    Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Divide the dough into 12 balls. Place 
    each ball on the baking tray, 3cm apart. Press the top of each ball to flatten slightly. 
    Transfer the tray to the v for another 30 minutes or more before baking.
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Bake the cookies for 13-15 minutes until golden.
    Be careful not to overbake as they will firm up as they cool. Leave to cool
    on the tray for 10 minutes then transfer to a wire rack.
