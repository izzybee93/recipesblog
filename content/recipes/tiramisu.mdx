---
title: "Tiramisu"
date: "2026-04-16"
categories:
  - desserts
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tiramisu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - 200ml espresso
  - 500g silken tofu
  - 5 tbsp caster sugar
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 2 tsp vanilla extract
  - Pinch of salt
  - 120ml oat or soya cream
  - 12 savoiardi biscuits
  - 1 tbsp cocoa powder
directions:
  - 'In a blender, add the tofu, sugar, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and salt, and blend until smooth. Gradually blend in the cream until fully combined.'
  - 'Spoon ⅓ of the tofu mixture into the base of a dish. Dip the biscuits in the coffee until soaked but not soggy, then layer on top. Repeat with another layer, then top with the remaining ⅓ tofu mixture. Smooth the top and dust with sifted cocoa powder. Transfer to the fridge for 3 hours before serving.'
