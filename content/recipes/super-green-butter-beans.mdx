---
title: "Super green butter beans"
date: "2025-10-01"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-butter-beans.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ onion, finely chopped
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 courgette, chopped
  - 60g baby spinach
  - 25g fresh basil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder 
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 2 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 1 400g can butter beans, drained and rinsed 
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Heat the oil In a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Add the courgette, cover and cook for about 8 minutes, or until the zucchini has softened.
  - Stir in the spinach and basil until wilted.
  - Blend the vegetables along with the lemon juice, chili, salt, nutritional yeast, and hemp seeds. Blend until smooth.
  - Add the butter beans and sauce to the pan, and heat through for 5 minutes, stirring regularly.
  - Season to taste. Serve with crusty bread.
