---
title: 'Harissa roasted squash, carrot and chickpeas'
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.285Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/harissa-roasted-squash-carrot-and-chickpeas.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '200g carrots, peeled, and sliced into strips'
  - '½ butternut squash, peeled, deseeded and sliced'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tbsp harissa paste
  - '400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 25g pumpkin seeds
  - 'Handful fresh parsley, chopped'
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Place the squash and carrots on a large baking
    tray. Drizzle with olive oil and harissa paste, then mix. Cook for 25 mins.
  - >-
    Remove the tray from the oven and add the chickpeas and pumpkin seeds. Stir
    them through, then return to the oven for a further 10-15 mins, until the
    vegetables have softened. Scatter with fresh parsley and serve.
