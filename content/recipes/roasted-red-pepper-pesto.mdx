---
title: "Roasted red pepper pesto"
date: "2026-01-08"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-red-pepper-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  - 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 1 tbsp pine nuts
  - Small handful fresh basil
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - Add the pepper to the tray and drizzle with ½ tbsp olive oil. Roast for 20 minutes until softened and slightly browned.  
  - Heat a small pan and lightly toast the pine nuts for 1-2 minutes.
  - In a food processor, add the pepper, garlic, pine nuts, basil, sea salt, ground black pepper and remaining olive oil.
  - Process until a smooth, thick pesto forms. Season to taste. Store refrigerated for up to 5 days, or freeze in small portions.
