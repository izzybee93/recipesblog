---
title: "Harissa aubergine and chickpea salad"
date: "2026-02-28"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/harissa-aubergine-and-chickpea-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '1 aubergine, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 60g rocket
  - 6 sun-dried tomatoes
  - '3 fresh mint leaves, chopped (optional)'
  - Harissa dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the aubergine, brush with the olive oil and roast for 15 minutes. Add the chickpeas and roast for another 10 minutes.'
  - 'In a large bowl, add the rocket, sun-dried tomatoes, mint, aubergine and chickpeas.'
  - 'Make the dressing, pour over the salad and toss to combine.'
