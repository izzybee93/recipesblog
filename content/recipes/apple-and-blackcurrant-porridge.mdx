title: "Apple and blackcurrant porridge"
date: "2026-07-04"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/apple-and-blackcurrant-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 50g blackcurrants
  - '1 small apple, sliced'
  - ½ tsp cinnamon
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
directions:
  - Add the oats, milk and chia seeds to the pan and cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently. Add the blackcurrants and continue to cook until heated through. Stir in the cinnamon.
  - Serve topped with apple slices, extra cinnamon and a swirl of almond butter.
