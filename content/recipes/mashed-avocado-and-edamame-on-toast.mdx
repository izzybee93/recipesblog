---
title: "Mashed avocado and edamame on toast"
date: "2025-10-25"
categories: ["breakfast"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mashed-avocado-and-edamame-on-toast.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - ½ large avocado
  - 100g edamame, cooked
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - Small handful fresh coriander
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 3 tbsp water
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  - Ground black pepper 
  - 4 slices sourdough, toasted 
directions:
  - Add the avocado, edamame, lime, coriander, salt and water to a food processor. Blend until a chunky paste forms, scraping down the sides as needed.
  - Serve on the sourdough, topped with black pepper, hemp seeds and pumpkin seeds.
