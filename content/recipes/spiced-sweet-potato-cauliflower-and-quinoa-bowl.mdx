title: Spiced sweet potato, cauliflower and quinoa bowl
date: '2025-11-02'
categories:

  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/spiced-sweet-potato-cauliflower-and-quinoa-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 50g quinoa
  - '1 large sweet potato, chopped'
  - '¼ cauliflower, chopped into florets'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp ground coriander
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - '¼ cucumber, sliced'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 2 tsp chimichurri seasoning
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray and add the sweet potato. Drizzle with ½ tbsp olive
    oil and the smoked paprika. Add the cauliflower. Drizzle with ½ tbsp
    olive oil and coat with the turmeric, cumin and coriander. Roast for 20-25 minutes, until slightly browned.
  - >-
    Whisk together the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug.
  - >-
    To assemble the bowls, add quinoa and sweet potato. Top with edamame,
    cauliflower, and cucumber. Pour over the dressing and serve.
