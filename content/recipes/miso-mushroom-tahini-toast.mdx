---
title: "Miso mushroom and tahini on toast"
date: "2025-11-18"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/miso-mushroom-tahini-toast.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 100g chestnut mushrooms, chopped
  - 50g edamame beans
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - ½ tsp miso paste
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp ginger
  - ½ tsp maple syrup
  - ~50ml water
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 2 slices sourdough
directions:
  - In a small measuring jug, mix together the soy sauce, miso paste, garlic, ginger, maple syrup and water. 
  - Heat the sesame oil in a pan over a medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for a few minutes. Add the edamame beans and sauce. Cook until the sauce thickens, stirring frequently.
  - Serve on toasted sourdough. Top with a drizzle of tahini, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds and hemp seeds.
---
