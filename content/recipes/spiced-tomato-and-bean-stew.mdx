---
title: "Spiced tomato and white bean stew"
date: "2026-03-24"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/spiced-tomato-and-bean-stew.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, peeled and minced'
  - '15g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves separated'
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 120ml vegetable stock
  - '1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 150g spinach
  - 50g silken tofu cream (optional, see recipe)
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - "In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened."
  - "Finely chop the coriander stalks. Add the stalks to the pan along with the garlic and ginger and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the cumin, smoked paprika, ground coriander, chilli powder and salt. Cook for 1 minute, then add the tomato paste and cook for another minute."
  - Add the pepper and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  - "Pour in the chopped tomatoes and vegetable stock and stir well. Add the cannellini beans and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened."
  - Make the silken tofu cream.
  - Add the spinach and stir through until wilted.
  - "Remove from the heat and stir in the cream. Top with black pepper, sprinkle with the coriander leaves and serve with crusty bread."
