---
title: Chocolate icing
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.276Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolateicing.jpeg
servings: serves 2 layer cake
ingredients:
  - 120g vegan butter
  - 210g icing sugar
  - 40g cocoa powder
  - 1 tbsp soya milk
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
directions:
  - >-
    Sift the sugar and cocoa powder. Add the salt then mix.
  - >-
    Beat the butter until light and fluffy. Beat in the vanilla, then gradually add
    the dry mixture. Blend until whipped and creamy. Add the milk as needed, until the
    desired consistency is reached.
  - >-
    Spread or pipe on top of the cake, and add your favourite toppings to
    decorate.
