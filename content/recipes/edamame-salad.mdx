servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '150g edamame, cooked'
  - '½ cucumber, diced'
  - '1 red bell pepper, diced'
  - 'Large handful coriander, chopped'
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - Juice of ½ a lime
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - 1 tsp honey
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl, combine the edamame, cucumber, pepper, coriander and sesame
    seeds.
  - 'In a small measuring jug, whisk together the remaining ingredients.'
  - Pour the dressing over the salad and toss together. Serve cold.
