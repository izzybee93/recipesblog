---
title: "Sun-dried tomato pasta salad"
date: "2026-04-21"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sun-dried-tomato-pasta-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 120g pasta
  - 10 sun-dried tomatoes
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - '150g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - 80g sweetcorn
  - '1 400g can butter beans, chickpeas or lentils'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - '1 tsp dried mixed herbs, such as oregano, basil and parsley'
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Cook the pasta. Rinse with cold water.
  - 'In a large bowl, combine the pasta, vegetables and beans.'
  - 'In a small measuring jug, mix together the dressing ingredients. Pour over the salad and gently toss together until evenly coated.'
