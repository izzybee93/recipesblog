---
title: "Quinoa, chickpea and green hummus salad bowl"
date: "2026-02-11"
categories:
  - salad
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/quinoa-chickpea-and-green-hummus-salad-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 50g quinoa
  - 1 large sweet potato, chopped
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - ½ small cucumber, chopped
  - 2 beetroots, chopped
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Pinch sea salt, to taste
  - 2 tbsp green herb hummus (see recipe)
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare a large baking tray. Place the sweet potato on the tray and brush with the oil and smoked paprika. Roast for 10 minutes. Add the chickpeas and roast for another 10 minutes.
  - To make the dressing, whisk together the lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil and sea salt.
  - To assemble the bowls, add the quinoa and top with the sweet potato, chickpeas, cucumber and beetroot. Pour over the dressing, and top with a spoonful of hummus.
