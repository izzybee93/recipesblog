---
title: "Creamy Dijon dressing"
date: "2026-01-30"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-dijon-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 ½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon or 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Whisk together the mustard, olive oil, lemon juice (or apple cider vinegar + 2 tsp water), sea salt and black pepper in a small measuring jug.
---
