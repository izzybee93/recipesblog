---
title: "Gnocchi with basil pesto"
date: "2026-04-28"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/gnocchi-with-basil-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 400g gnocchi
  - ½ basil pesto (see recipe)
  - 200g cherry tomatoes
  - 60g spinach
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Cook the gnocchi.
  - Heat a large pan and add the pesto, spinach, and tomatoes. Stir in the gnocchi and a few splashes of leftover water. Stir until heated through.
  - Serve topped with ground black pepper.
---
