---
title: Bean and sweet onion dip
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.256Z'
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: /images/recipes/beansweetoniondip.jpeg
servings: serves 4-6
ingredients:
  - '1 large red onion, quartered'
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - '1 400g can kidney beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - '½ tsp salt, to taste'
  - 2 tbsp water
  - 'Small handful dill, chopped'
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Drizzle the onion with olive oil and roast for 15
    minutes. Leave to cool.
  - >-
    Add all the ingredients except the water and herbs to a food processor.
    Process until a chunky dip forms, scraping down the sides as needed.
  - >-
    Add the water and process again until smooth. Add the dill and process
    again. Season to taste. Serve chilled.
