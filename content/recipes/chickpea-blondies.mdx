---
title: "Chickpea blondies"
date: "2025-11-09"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chickpea-blondies.jpeg"
servings: "serves 16"
ingredients:
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 85g tahini or almond butter
  - 60ml maple syrup or honey 
  - ½ tsp vanilla extract
  - ¼ tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  - 40g oat flour
  - 60g dark chocolate chips
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 8x8 pan with parchment paper.
  - Add the chickpeas, tahini or almond butter, maple syrup, vanilla, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, and salt. Process for about 1 minute until smooth.
  - Add in oat flour and process for 30 seconds.
  - Stir in chocolate chips, reserving a few tbsp for the topping.
  - Spread evenly into the prepared baking pan and sprinkle with the remaining chocolate chips.
  - Bake 25-30 minutes, or until golden and crusty on top.
  - Leave to cool for 20 minutes in the pan to firm up. Transfer to a cooling rack and leave for at least 30 minutes before slicing. Transfer to the fridge if desired to make it easier to slice.
