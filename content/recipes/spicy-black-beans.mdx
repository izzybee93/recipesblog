---
title: "Spicy black beans"
date: "2026-02-21"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/spicy-black-beans.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ onion, finely chopped
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 tsp dried oregano 
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika 
  - ¼ tsp chili flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed
  - 150ml vegetable stock
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Stir in the spices.
  - Stir in the tomato paste, black beans and vegetable stock. Cook for 8-10 minutes, until thickened, stirring occasionally.
  - Stir in the coriander and season to taste. Serve as a filling for tacos, burritos or with rice.
---
