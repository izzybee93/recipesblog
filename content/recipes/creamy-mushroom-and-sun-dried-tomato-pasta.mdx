---
title: "Creamy mushroom and sun-dried tomato pasta"
date: "2026-01-08"
categories: ["mains", "pasta"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-mushroom-and-sun-dried-tomato-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 250g chestnut mushrooms, chopped
  - ¾ tsp dried oregano
  - 'Small handful fresh basil, chopped'
  - Large handful spinach
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - '## Sauce'
  - 200ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp white miso paste
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 8 sun-dried tomatoes
  - Juice of ½ lemon
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil and cook the garlic on a medium heat for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  - To make the sauce, add all ingredients to a blender, reserving half of the lemon juice, and blend until smooth.
  - Add the sauce to the pan and stir frequently until slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp of pasta water as needed. Stir in the herbs and spinach until wilted. Stir in the remaning lemon juice and season to taste.
  - Add the pasta and stir until coated and heated through. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
