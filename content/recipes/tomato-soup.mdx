---
title: "Tomato soup"
date: "2025-08-30"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tomato-soup.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4-5"
ingredients:
  - 8 medium tomatoes, quartered
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  - 1 onion, chopped
  - 1 small carrot, chopped
  - 1 tbsp tomato purée
  - ½ tsp sugar
  - ½ tsp thyme
  - 1 tsp oregano
  - 900ml vegetable stock
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray with baking paper.
  - Place the tomatoes and garlic on the tray drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil. Gently toss to coat. Bake for 30 minutes.
  - Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large pan. Add the onion and carrot. Cook over a low heat for 5-10 minutes, until softened.
  - Stir in the tomato purée. Add the tomatoes, sugar and herbs. Stir together, then cover and leave to cook on a low heat for 5 minutes. Add the stock and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Remove the pan from the heat. Blend until smooth. Reheat and season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve with crusty bread.
