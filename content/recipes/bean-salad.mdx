featured_image: /images/recipes/bean-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 2-3
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can mixed beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '⅓ cucumber, chopped'
  - '200g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - 100g sweetcorn
  - '60g roasted artichoke, chopped'
  - 12 olives
  - Small handful fresh parsely, chopped
  - '## Dressing'
  - ½ tsp dried oregano
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
directions:
  - Add all the vegetables to a large bowl and gently toss together.
  - Whisk together the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss until coated.
