servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, chopped'
  - 50g raspberries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - 50ml plant milk
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, half the raspberries, nut butter, chia seeds, oats, cinnamon and
    plant milk until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with remaining
    raspberries and granola.
