---
title: Black bean ragu
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.262Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/black-bean-ragu.jpeg
servings: serves 2-3
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp ground cumin
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 tsp miso paste
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the olive oil on a medium-high heat. Add the onion
    and cook for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another
    minute.
  - >-
    Stir the paprika, cumin and salt. Add the black beans, chopped tomatoes, and
    half a can of water.
  - >-
    Bring to a simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until darkened
    and reduced.
  - Stir through the miso paste and ground black pepper.
