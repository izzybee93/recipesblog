---
title: "Chocolate pudding"
date: "2026-01-18"
categories: ["treats", "desserts"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-pudding.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - 3 Medjool dates
  - 45g hemp seeds
  - 30g chia seeds
  - 10g cocoa powder
  - 20g almond butter
  - 230ml soya milk
  - 3 tsp cacao nibs
directions:
  - Add all ingredients except the cacao nibs to a blender and blend until smooth. 
  - Pour into 3 glasses and refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Top each pudding with 1 tsp cocoa nibs and serve. 
---
