---
title: "Chocolate, almond and chia pudding"
date: "2026-01-18"
categories: ["desserts", "treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-almond-and-chia-pudding.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - 3 Medjool dates
  - 45g hemp seeds
  - 30g chia seeds
  - 10g cocoa powder
  - 20g almond butter
  - ½ tsp vanilla extract
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 250ml soya milk
  - 2 tsp cacao nibs
  - Handful blueberries
directions:
  - Add all ingredients except the cacao nibs to a blender and blend until smooth. 
  - Pour into 3 glasses and refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Top each pudding with cacao nibs and blueberries, then serve.
---
