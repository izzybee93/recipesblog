---
title: Melanzane a funghetto
date: '2025-08-17'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/melanzane-a-funghetto.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 large aubergine, chopped
  - ½ onion, finely chopped
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 200g baby plum tomatoes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - 3 tbsp water
  - Small handful fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat 2 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the aubergine and cook for 5 minutes, until golden brown.
  - Add the onion, garlic and the remaining tbsp olive oil. Cook for 5 minutes until softened
  - Stir in the tomatoes and season with sea salt and black pepper. Add a few tbsp water, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  - Stir in a few fresh basil leaves and serve with extra basil for garnish.
---
