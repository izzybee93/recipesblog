---
title: Melanzane a funghetto
date: '2025-08-17'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/melanzane-a-funghetto.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 large aubergine, diced'
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic cloves, minced'
  - 200g baby plum tomatoes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste,'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - Small handful fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the aubergine and
    cook until golden brown.
  - 'Add the onion and garlic and cook for another 5 minutes until softened.'
  - >-
    Add the tomatoes and season with sea salt and black pepper. Simmer for 15-20
    minutes.
  - Serve with a garnish of fresh basil leaves.
---
