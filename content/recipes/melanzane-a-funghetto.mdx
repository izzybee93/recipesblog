---
title: "Melanzane a funghetto"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/melanzaneafunghetto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 large aubergines, diced
  - 1 onion, finely chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  - Â½ tsp sea salt, to taste,
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - Small handful fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the aubergines and cook until golden brown.
  - Add the onion and garlic and cook for another 5 minutes, until softened.
  - Add the tomatoes and season with sea salt and black pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  - Serve with a garnish of fresh basil leaves.
