---
title: "Melanzane a funghetto"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/melanzaneafunghetto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 2 large aubergines, diced
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 onion, finely chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 400g canned tomatoes
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add diced aubergines and cook until golden brown.
  - Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  - Add canned tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  - Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
