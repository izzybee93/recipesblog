---
title: "Mediterranean vegetable pasta salad"
date: "2026-04-21"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mediterranean-vegetable-pasta-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 120g pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 courgette, chopped'
  - '½ bell pepper, chopped'
  - '150g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - '1 400g can butter beans, chickpeas or lentils'
  - 12 olives, chopped
  - Small handful fresh basil, chopped
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the courgettes and pepper to the tray and brush with the olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes.
  - Cook the pasta.
  - 'In a large bowl, combine the beans, tomatoes, olives, pasta and roasted vegetables. Add the basil. Sprinkle over the salt, then add the balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Gently toss together until evenly coated.'
