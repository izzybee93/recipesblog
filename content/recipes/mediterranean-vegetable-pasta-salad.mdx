---
title: "Mediterranean vegetable pasta salad"
date: "2026-04-21"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mediterranean-vegetable-pasta-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 120g pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 courgette, chopped'
  - '½ bell pepper, chopped'
  - '150g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - '1 400g can butter beans, chickpeas or lentils'
  - 12 olives
  - Small handful fresh basil
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the courgettes and pepper to the tray and brush with the olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes.
  - Cook the pasta.
  - 'In a large bowl, combine the pasta, roasted vegetables, tomatoes, beans, olives and basil. Sprinkle over the salt, then add the balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Gently toss together until evenly coated.'
