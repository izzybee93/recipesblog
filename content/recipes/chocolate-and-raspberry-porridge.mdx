---
title: Chocolate and raspberry porridge
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.274Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolate-and-raspberry-porridge.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 75g raspberries
  - '1 square 100% dark chocolate, or 1 tsp cocoa powder'
  - 1 tsp almond butter
  - 1 tsp cacao nibs (optional)
directions:
  - >-
    Add the oats, milk, chia seeds and ¾ of the raspberries to the pan and cook for a few
    minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently.
  - Stir in the chocolate or cocoa powder and almond butter.
  - Serve topped with the remainaing raspberries and cacao nibs.
