title: Mexican style quinoa and black bean bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.299Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/mexican-style-quinoa-and-black-bean-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 80g quinoa
  - '1 pepper, chopped'
  - '150g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp coriander
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 'Handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - ½ avocado, sliced (optional)
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the pepper and cook
    for 5 minutes. Stir in spices, and continue to cook until the peppers start to soften. Add the black beans and tomatoes and cook for a few more minutes, until heated through.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the quinoa and veggies. Mix well, then stir in the lime juice, coriander and extra virgin olive oil. Serve topped with avocado slices.
