title: Tofu noodle stir fry

featured_image: /images/recipes/tofu-noodle-stir-fry.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 100g rice noodles
  - '200g firm tofu, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp sesame oil
  - 2 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - '1 bell pepper, chopped'
  - '1 large courgette, sliced'
  - '## Any 2 veggies:'
  - 2 medium carrots, chopped
  - 1 large courgette, chopped
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 150g baby corn, chopped
  - 150g beansprouts
  - 150g frozen peas
  - ¼ broccoli, chopped into florets
  - ¼ cauliflower, chopped into florets
  - '## Sauce'
  - 2 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 1 tsp honey
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, minced'
  - 125ml water
  - 1 tsp cornflour
  - '## Garnish'
  - 'Handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
  - 2 tbsp crispy onion
directions:
  - 'Cook the rice noodles. Drain and rinse, then set aside.'
  - >-
    In a medium pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add the tofu and soy sauce.
    Toss to coat the tofu and cook on a medium heat for 5 minutes. Turn the tofu
    over to brown another side and cook for another 5 minutes.
  - >-
    In a small jug, add the soy sauce, lime, honey, ginger and
    water, and stir. Add the cornflour and mix well.
  - >-
    In a large wok, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add the garlic and cook for 1
    minute. Add the edamame and veggies, and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Add tofu and
    sauce, and cook until slightly thickened. Stir in the coriander.
  - Add the noodles and toss until combined. Serve topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and crispy onion.
