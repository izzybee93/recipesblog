title: Chickpea stir fry

servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 100g rice or noodles
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '½ onion, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 red pepper, chopped'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 handfuls spinach
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - '## Sauce'
  - ½ tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - ½ tbsp rice vinegar
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
directions:
  - Cook the rice or noodles.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the sesame oil over a medium heat. Add the onion and
    cook for a few minutes until softened. Add the garlic and pepper and cook
    for 5 minutes, followed by the chickpeas and cook for another 5 minutes,
    stirring occasionally.
  - >-
    To make the sauce, whisk all the ingredients together in a small jug.
  - >-
    Pour the sauce into the pan and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring until
    coated. Add a splash of water if needed. Stir through the spinach until
    wilted. Serve with rice or noodles, and top with sesame seeds.
