---
title: "Hazelnut and almond butter cups"
date: "2026-01-25"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/hazelnut-and-almond-butter-cups.jpeg"
servings: "makes 10"
ingredients:
  - 80g hazelnuts, toasted and chopped
  - 80g almond butter
  - 90g Medjool dates
  - 10g cocoa powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 2 tbsp water
  - 50g dark chocolate
directions:
  - Prepare a tray with 10 cupcake cups. Set aside 20g of the hazelnuts and 30g of the almond butter. 
  - In a food processor, add the remaining 60g of hazelnuts, remaining 50g almond butter, dates, cocoa powder and sea salt. Process until smooth. Add 1-2 tbsp water if needed, and blend until a soft, sticky mixture forms. 
  - Roll the mixture into 10 balls. Roll each ball in remaining the hazelnuts, then place in the prepared cups. Press down and make a small indent in the centre of each cup.
  - Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pan of boiling water.
  - Spoon the remaining almond butter into to each cup. Top with a spoonful of melted chocolate. Refrigerate for a few hours to set.
