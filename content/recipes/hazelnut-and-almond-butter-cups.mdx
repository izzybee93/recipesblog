---
title: "Hazelnut and almond butter cups"
date: "2026-01-25"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/hazelnut-and-almond-butter-cups.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 180g hazelnuts, toasted and chopped
  - 170g almond butter
  - 200g Medjool dates
  - 20g cocoa powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 100g dark chocolate
directions:
  - Prepare a tray with 12 cupcake cups. Set aside 50g of the hazelnuts and 50g of almond the almond butter. 
  - In a food processor, add the remaining 130g of hazelnuts, remaining 120g almond butter, dates, cocoa powder and sea salt. Process for a few minutes until smooth. Add 1-2 tbsp water if needed, and blend until a soft, sticky mixture forms. 
  - Roll the mixture into 12 balls. Roll each ball in the hazelnuts, then place in each ball the prepared cups. Press down and make a small indent in the centre of each cup.
  - Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pan of boiling water.
  - Spoon the almond butter into to each cup. Top with a spoonful of melted chocolate. Refrigerate for a few hours to set.
