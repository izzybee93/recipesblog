servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '1 banana, sliced'
  - 30g frozen blueberries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - 50ml plant milk
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, blueberries, nut butter, chia seeds, oats, cinnamon and plant milk
    until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with banana and granola.
