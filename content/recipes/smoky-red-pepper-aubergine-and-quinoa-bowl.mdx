title: "Smoky red pepper, aubergine and quinoa bowl"
date: "2026-02-20"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/smoky-red-pepper-aubergine-and-quinoa-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 80g quinoa
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 1 Romano pepper, chopped
  - 1 ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed
  - ½ smoky red pepper sauce (see recipe)
  - Small handful fresh chives, finely chopped
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray with baking paper. 
  - Place the aubergine on the tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and the smoked paprika. Roast for 10-15 minutes. Add the pepper on a tray and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Roast for another 8-10 minutes. 
  - Prepare the red pepper sauce.
  - Assemble the bowls with quinoa, black beans, aubergine and pepper. Drizzle with red pepper sauce and top with a sprinkle of chives.
