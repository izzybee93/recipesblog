servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '¼ small red cabbage, chopped'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - '1 large carrot, grated'
  - '¼ cucumber, sliced'
  - 80g sweetcorn
  - '200g edamame, cooked'
  - 'Large handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 2 servings sesame salad dressing (see recipe)
  - '## Garnish'
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the red cabbage and lime juice. Massage briefly then set aside.
  - Add the carrot, cucumber, sweetcorn, edamame and coriander and toss together.
  - Make the sesame salad dressing. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  - Serve with a garnish of sesame seeds.
