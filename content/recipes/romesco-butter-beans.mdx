---
title: "Romesco butter beans"
date: "2026-01-20"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/romesco-butter-beans.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 large red bell pepper
  - Large handful broccolini
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 100g silken tofu
  - 6 sun-dried tomatoes
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch sea salt
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 10 roasted almonds, chopped
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2 tbsp water
  - 1 400g can butter beans, drained and rinsed
  - Small handful fresh parsley, finely chopped
  - 1 tbsp lemon zest
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Slice the pepper into a few chunks and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes then set aside.
  - Add the broccolini to the baking tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast for 10 minutes.
  - In a blender, add the roasted pepper, garlic, tofu, sun-dried tomatoes, smoked paprika, salt, chili flakes, half the roasted almonds, lemon juice and water. Blend until smooth.
  - In a large pan, heat over a medium-low heat and add the sauce and butter beans. Cook for 10 minutes until slightly thickened. Stir in the parsley and half the lemon zest.
  - Divide between serving bowls and top with the remaining chopped almonds and lemon zest. Serve with the broccolini and toasted sourdough.
