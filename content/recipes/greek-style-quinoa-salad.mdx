---
title: "Greek style quinoa salad"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/greek-style-quinoa-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 50g quinoa
  - 120g brown lentils
  - ½ red pepper, finely chopped
  - ¼ cucumber, chopped
  - 100g cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - 8 olives
  - 4 mint leaves, finely chopped (optional)
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 80g extra-firm tofu, cubed (optional)
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp tahini
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp dried oregano 
  - ½ tsp honey or maple syrup
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 2 tsp water
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa. 
  - Heat the olive oil a pan over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook for 10 minutes, turning to brown each side.
  - In a large bowl, add all the vegetables and quinoa.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all the ingredients together in a measuring jug. Add more water as needed. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  - Serve topped with the tofu.
