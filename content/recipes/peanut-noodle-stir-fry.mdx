---
title: "Peanut noodle stir fry"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/peanut-noodle-stir-fry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice noodles
  - ½ tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 pepper, chopped'
  - ½ broccoli, chopped into florets
  - '120g edamame, cooked'
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - '## Sauce'
  - 2 tbsp peanut butter
  - 1 tbsp rice vinegar or lime juice
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 100ml water
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - 1 tsp maple syrup or honey
directions:
  - Cook the rice noodles. Drain and rinse, then set aside.
  - Heat the sesame oil in a wok over medium heat. Add the garlic and pepper and cook for 5 minutes. Add the broccoli and cook for 5-10 more minutes. Stir in the edamame.
  - In a small bowl, whisk together all the sauce ingredients until smooth. Add half the sauce to the pan and stir until heated. Add a splash of water as needed.
  - Add the noodles to the pan and toss to combine. Add the remaining sauce and stir until coated and heated through. Add a splash more water as needed.
  - Serve topped with sesame seeds.
