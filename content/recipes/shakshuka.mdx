---
title: Shakshuka
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.304Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/shakshuka.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 small red onion, chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 red bell pepper, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 50g spinach
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped
  - 2 large eggs
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5
    minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Stir in 
    the spices and tomato paste. Add the pepper and cook for 5 more minutes.
  - >-
    Stir in the tomatoes and simmer for 5 minutes, then stir in the spinach and cook until wilted. 
    Stir in the coriander, reserving a few leaves for garnish.
  - >-
    Use the back of a spoon to make a well near the edge of the pan and crack the egg
    directly into it. Repeat with the remaining egg.
  - >-
    Cover with a lid and cook over a medium-low heat for about 5 minutes, until the egg whites are opaque.
  - >-
    Divide between serving bowls, top with coriander leaves and serve with toasted crusty bread.
