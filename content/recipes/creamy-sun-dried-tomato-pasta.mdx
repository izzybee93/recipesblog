---
title: "Creamy sun-dried tomato pasta"
date: "2026-01-08"
categories: ["mains", "pasta"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-sun-dried-tomato-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 200ml soya milk
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 tsp white miso paste
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 8 sun-dried tomatoes + 1 tbsp oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - Large handful spinach
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Roast the aubergine for 20 mins.
  - Cook the pasta.
  - In a blender, add the soya milk, olive oil, garlice, miso paste, nutritional yeast, paprika, sun-dried tomatoes, lemon juice and seasoning. Blend until smooth, adding more milk as needed.
  - In a large pan, heat the sauce on a medium heat and stir frequently until slightly thickened. Stir in the spinach.
  - Add the pasta and aubergine to the pan and stir until heated through. Season to taste.
