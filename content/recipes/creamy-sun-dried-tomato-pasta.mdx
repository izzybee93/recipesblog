---
title: "Creamy sun-dried tomato and mushroom pasta"
date: "2026-01-08"
categories: ["mains", "pasta"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-sun-dried-tomato-and-mushroom-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 200g chestnut mushrooms, chopped
  - ¾ tsp dried oregano
  - 'Small handful fresh basil, chopped'
  - Large handful spinach
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - '## Sauce'
  - 200ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 tsp white miso paste
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 8 sun-dried tomatoes
  - Juice of ½ lemon
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil on medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-8 minutes, until slightly browned.
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  - In a blender, add the soya milk, olive oil, garlic, miso paste, nutritional yeast, paprika, sun-dried tomatoes and lemon juice. Blend until smooth.
  - Add the sauce to the pan and stir frequently until slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp of pasta water as needed. Season to taste. Stir in the herbs and spinach.
  - Add the pasta and stir until coated and heated through. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
