---
title: "Lentil and quinoa salad"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lentil-and-quinoa-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3-4"
ingredients:
  - 120g quinoa
  - 1 400g can brown lentils, drained and rinsed
  - 2 bell peppers, finely chopped
  - ½ cucumber, chopped
  - 100g cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - 16 olives
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 50g extra-firm tofu, cubed (optional)
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - ⅓ tsp garlic powder
  - ⅓ tsp oregano 
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 2-3 tbsp water
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa. 
  - Heat the olive oil a pan over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook for 10 minutes, turning to brown each side.
  - In a large bowl, add all the vegetables and quinoa.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all the ingredients together in a measuring jug. Add more water as needed. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  - Serve topped with the tofu.
