title: "Broccoli pasta with creamy tofu sauce"
date: "2026-02-15"
categories: 
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/broccoli-pasta-with-creamy-tofu-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - ½ large broccoli, chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - Creamy tofu sauce (see recipe)
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Roast the broccoli for 10 mins.
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some of the pasta water.
  - Make the creamy tofu sauce. Add to a large pan and gently heat through. Stir in 2 tbsp of pasta water, adding more as needed.
  - Add the roasted broccoli and pasta. Stir until combined and heated through. Season to taste.
