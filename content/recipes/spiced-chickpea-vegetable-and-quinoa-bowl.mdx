---
title: "Spiced chickpea, vegetable and quinoa bowl"
date: "2025-09-20"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/spiced-chickpea-vegetable-and-quinoa-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g quinoa
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 1 large courgette, chopped 
  - 6 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped 
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp garam masala
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder 
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large pan. Cook the pepper and courgette for 10-15 minutes on a medium heat until slightly browned. Stir in the sun-dried tomatoes and cook for 2 more minutes.
  - Heat a separate pan, then add the chickpeas. Heat for a 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the spices and mix well. Cook until slightly crispy.
  - Transfer the quinoa to a bowl. Stir in the coriander, sea salt and 1 tbsp olive oil. 
  - Divide the quinoa between the serving bowls. Top with vegetables and chickpeas.
