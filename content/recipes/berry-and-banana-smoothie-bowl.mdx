featured_image: /images/recipes/berry-and-banana-smoothie-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, chopped'
  - 50g mixed berries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - 50ml plant milk
  - 1 drop vanilla extract (optional)
  - 1 tbsp granola
  - 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, berries, nut butter, chia seeds, oats, plant milk and vanilla until smooth.
    Transfer to a bowl and top with granola and pumpkin seeds.
