servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, chopped'
  - 50g mixed frozen berries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - 50ml plant milk
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, berries, nut butter, chia seeds, oats, and plant milk until smooth.
    Transfer to a bowl and top with granola.
