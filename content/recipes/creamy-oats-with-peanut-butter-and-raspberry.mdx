---
title: "Creamy oats with peanut butter and raspberry"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["breakfast", "treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-oats-with-peanut-butter-and-raspberry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 4 tsp peanut butter
  - 2 tsp raspberry jam
  - 3 tbsp oats
  - ~100ml soya milk
directions:
  - Spoon layers into a small glass - add 2 tsp of peanut butter in the bottom. Layer 1 tsp of raspberry jam on top.
  - Add the oats then add the soya milk so that the oats are just covered. 
  - Spoon on the remaining layers of raspberry jam then peanut butter.
  - Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.
