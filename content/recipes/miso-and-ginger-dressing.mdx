---
title: "Miso and ginger dressing"
date: "2026-07-10"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/miso-and-ginger-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - ¾ tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tsp fresh ginger
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 1-2 tbsp water
directions:
  - Place all of the ingredients in a small bowl and whisk to combine until smooth.
