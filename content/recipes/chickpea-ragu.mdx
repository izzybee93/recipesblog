---
title: "Chickpea ragu"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chickpearagu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 onion, finely chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp ground cumin
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 2 tsp miso paste
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil on a medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - Stir the paprika, cumin and salt. Add the chickpeas, chopped tomatoes, and half a can of water.
  - Bring to a simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until darkened and reduced. Use a fork to lightly mash ⅛ of the chickpeas against the side of the pot.
  - Stir through the miso paste, almond butter and ground black pepper.
