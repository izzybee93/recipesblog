---
title: "Harissa tempeh bowl"
date: "2026-06-30"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/harissa-tempeh-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - '50g tempeh, sliced'
  - ½ tsp harissa paste
  - 2 tsp soy sauce
  - 2 tsp lime juice
  - 50g quinoa
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - '½ aubergine, chopped'
  - '50g edamame, cooked'
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - ½ harissa dressing (see recipe)
  - 'Small handful fresh mint, chopped (optional)'
directions:
  - In a shallow dish, add the tempeh, harissa paste, soy sauce and lime juice. Mix well and leave to marinate in the fridge for 2 hours.
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Place the aubergine on the tray, brush with ½ tbsp olive oil, and roast for 20 minutes, until softened.
  - In a small pan over medium-high heat, add the tempeh and cook for 2 minutes on each side until browned.
  - Make the harissa dressing.
  - Assemble the bowl with the quinoa, topped with tempeh, aubergine, edamame and cucumber. Pour over the dressing. Serve with a garnish of mint leaves.
