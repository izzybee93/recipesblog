---
title: "Super green fried rice"
date: "2026-04-13"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-fried-rice.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 120g rice
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 100g frozen edamame
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 80g sugar snap peas
  - '80g broccoli, chopped into florets'
  - 50g beansprouts
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 60g spinach
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, chopped'
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - 'Heat the oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the garlic, broccoli and beansprouts, and cook for 5 minutes. Add the sugar snap peas, and cook for 5 more minutes.'
  - 'Stir in the rice and chilli flakes. Add the soy sauce and stir in the spinach. Cook for 2 more minutes, until wilted.'
  - Remove from the heat and stir in the coriander. Top with a sprinkle of crispy onions.
---
