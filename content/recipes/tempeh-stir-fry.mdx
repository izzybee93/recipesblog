title: "Tempeh stir fry"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tempeh-stir-fry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 200g tempeh, cut into strips
  - 100g rice
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 100g tenderstem broccoli, chopped
  - 1 red pepper, chopped
  - 100g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
  - '## Marinade'
  - 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tbsp almond butter
  - 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  - 2 tbsp pure maple syrup
  - 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, minced
  - Â¼ tsp chilli flakes
directions:
  - Marinade the tempeh in a shallow dish for at least 2 hours in the fridge.
  - Cook the rice.
  - In a large pan, heat the sesame oil over a medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add the peppers cook for 5 minutes, until the peppers start to soften. Add the broccoli and mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes.
  - Add the tempeh and marinade, and continue to cook for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Serve with the rice and top with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
