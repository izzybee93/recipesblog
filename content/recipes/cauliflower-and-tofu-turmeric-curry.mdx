---
title: "Cauliflower and tofu turmeric curry"
date: "2026-03-22"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/cauliflower-and-tofu-turmeric-curry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '½ large cauliflower, cut into florets'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - '200g firm tofu, cubed'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, peeled and minced'
  - '15g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves separated'
  - 1 tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 120ml vegetable stock
  - 225g spinach
  - 150g basmati rice
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 60g silken tofu cream (see recipe)
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the cauliflower, drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes until golden and slightly crisp."
  - Cook the rice.
  - "In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning to brown on all sides. Set aside."
  - "In the same pan, add the garlic, ginger and finely chopped coriander stalks and cook for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the turmeric, cumin, chilli flakes and salt. Cook for 1 minute, then add the tomato paste and cook for another minute. Pour in the vegetable stock and stir."
  - Add the roasted cauliflower and tofu. Simmer for 5 minutes to allow the flavours to combine.
  - Make the silken tofu cream.
  - "Add the spinach and stir through until wilted. Remove from the heat and stir in the lime juice and cream."
  - Sprinkle with the coriander leaves and serve with the rice.
