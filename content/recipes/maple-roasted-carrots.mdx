---
title: "Maple roasted carrots"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/maple-roasted-carrots.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 3 large carrots, sliced
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tbsp maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 'Pinch sea salt, to taste'
  - '## Topping'
  - 1 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  - '½ tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray with baking paper.
  - Place the carrots on the tray. Add the olive oil, maple syrup, garlic powder, smoked paprika, chilli flakes and sea salt, and toss together until coated.
  - Roast for 20-25 minutes, until caramelised and softened.
  - Serve topped with fresh dill and pomegranate seeds. Optionally serve on a bed of butter bean and lemon dip (see recipe).
