---
title: Harissa veggie burgers
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.317Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/veggie-burgers.jpeg
servings: makes 4
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can kidney beans or black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 large garlic clove, crushed'
  - '½ red onion, chopped'
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp harissa paste
  - 1 tsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  - 40g rolled oats
  - 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Place the beans in a medium bowl and mash
    well with a fork.
  - >-
    In a medium pan, heat ½ tbsp olive oil and sauté the onions for 3 minutes.
    Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - >-
    Add the sautéd onion and garlic to the mashed beans, together with the
    spices, ½ tbsp olive oil, tomato paste, harissa paste, soy sauce, vinegar, oats and pumpkin seeds.
    Combine well and shape into 4 patties. Bake for 30 minutes, until crispy.
