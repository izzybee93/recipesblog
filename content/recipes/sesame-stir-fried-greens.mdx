---
title: "Sesame stir fried greens"
date: "2026-07-01"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sesame-stir-fried-greens.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 tsp ginger, minced'
  - 60g spinach
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 250g leafy greens, stems and leaves separated and chopped
  - '## Any leafy greens:'
  - Pak choi
  - Beetroot greens
  - Chard
  - Broccolini
  - '## Sauce'
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - Pinch chilli flakes
directions:
  - Heat the sesame oil in a wok over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 3 minutes, until softened. Stir in the garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute. Add the stems and cook for 5 minutes.
  - Add the leafy greens, spinach and ~50ml water. Cover and leave to steam 2 minutes.
  - To make the sauce, whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl. Add the sauce and continue to stir until the leaves are coated. Remove from the heat and stir in the lime juice.
  - Serve topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
