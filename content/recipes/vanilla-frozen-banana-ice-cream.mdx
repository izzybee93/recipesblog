---
title: "Vanilla frozen banana ice cream"
date: "2026-06-04"
categories:
  - desserts
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/vanilla-frozen-banana-ice-cream.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '2 large ripe bananas, chopped and frozen'
  - ½ tsp vanilla extract
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Add all ingredients to a food processor and process for 5 minutes until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze for 1-2 hours before serving. Scoop into bowls and serve with your favourite toppings.
