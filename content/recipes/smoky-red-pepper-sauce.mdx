---
title: "Smoky red pepper sauce"
date: "2026-02-11"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/smoky-red-pepper-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 roasted red peppers
  - 120 g cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch child flakes (optional)
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2–4 tbsp water
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Add the peppers, beans, tomato paste, olive oil, garlic, smoked paprika, lemon juice, and water to a blender.Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more water as needed to reach a creamy consistency.
  - Transfer to a small pan and warm gently over medium-low heat for 3–5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Serve with roasted vegetables or drizzle over your favourite veggie grain bowl.
