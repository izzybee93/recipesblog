---
title: "Burrito bowl"
date: "2025-08-27"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/burrito-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 80g rice
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 onion, chopped
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 400g can kidney beans or black beans, drained and rinsed
  - 3 large tomatoes, chopped
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp chili powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - Small handful coriander, chopped
  - 80g sweetcorn
  - 1 avocado, sliced
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - In a large frying pan, heat the oil. Add the onion and cook for a few minutes until it starts to soften. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - Add the beans and one of the chopped tomatoes. Cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the spices, lime juice and salt, and continue to cook on a medium heat for 5-10 minutes. Add a few tbsp water if it becomes too dry. Stir in the fresh coriander.
  - Assemble the bowls with the rice, topped with the bean mix, tomatoes, sweetcorn and avocado.
---
