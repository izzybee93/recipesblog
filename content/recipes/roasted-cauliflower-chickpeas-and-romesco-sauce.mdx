servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '½ cauliflower, cut into florets'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 handfuls cherry tomatoes
  - 1 ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 'Small handful coriander, roughly chopped'
  - '## Sauce'
  - 1 large red pepper, chopped
  - 4 sun-dried tomatoes
  - 1 garlic clove, chopped
  - 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon or 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - Pinch sea salt
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 10 roasted almonds, chopped
  - 50ml water
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Slice the pepper into a few chunks and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes then set aside.
  - On a large baking tray, toss together the cauliflower, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes and olive oil. Roast for 20-25 minutes until browned and slightly crispy.
  - To make the sauce, add all of the ingredients to a food processor and process for a few minutes until smooth.
  - To serve, spread the sauce onto the serving plates and place the roasted vegetables on top. Top with the coriander.
