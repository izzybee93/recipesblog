---
title: "Cauliflower and spinach curry"
date: "2026-03-22"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/cauliflower-and-spinach-curry.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 thumb-sized piece ginger, peeled and minced'
  - '15g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves separated'
  - ½ tsp ground turmeric
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp garam masala
  - ½ tsp chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 100ml vegetable stock
  - '½ large cauliflower, cut into florets'
  - '120g chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 225g spinach
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 75g tofu cream (see recipe)
  - 120g basmati rice or quinoa
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the rice or quinoa.
  - "In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened."
  - "Stir in the garlic, ginger and finely chopped coriander stalks and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the turmeric, cumin, garam masala, chilli flakes, salt and tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes. Pour in the vegetable stock."
  - "Add the cauliflower and toss until coated. Stir in the chickpeas. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender."
  - 'Make the "bright and fresh" flavour tofu cream.'
  - "Add the spinach to the pan and stir through until wilted. Remove from the heat and stir in the lime juice and tofu cream."
  - Sprinkle with the coriander leaves and serve with the rice.
