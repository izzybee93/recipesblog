categories:

  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/mexican-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 romaine lettuce, chopped'
  - '2 tomatoes, chopped'
  - '½ cucmber, chopped'
  - '1 avocado, chopped into chunks'
  - 160g sweetcorn
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 'Large handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - ¾ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ¼ tsp dried garlic granules
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl combine all the veggies except the avocado. Add the beans
    and fresh coriander.
  - >-
    To make the dressing, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, salt, cumin,
    chilli and garlic.
  - >-
    Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly. Add the avocado
    and gently toss again.
