---
title: "Super green butter bean shakshuka"
date: "2026-03-28"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-shakshuka.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 120g frozen peas
  - 80g spinach
  - 15g fresh coriander
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 200ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp tahini
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 small onion, diced'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - '1 green bell pepper, chopped'
  - '1 400g butter beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Place the frozen peas and spinach in a bowl and cover with boiling water. Leave for 5 minutes to soften the peas and wilt the spinach. Drain and transfer to a blender.
  - Add the coriander (reserving a few pieces for garnish), half the lime juice, soya milk, tahini and salt. Blend until smooth.
  - Heat the olive oil in a large pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Stir in the cumin and chilli. Add the peppers and cook for 10 minutes. Stir in the butter beans.
  - Add the green sauce and cook for 5-10 minutes until heated through and thickened slightly.
  - Garnish with coriander and ground black pepper. Serve with toasted sourdough.
