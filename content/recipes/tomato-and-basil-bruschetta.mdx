---
title: Tomato bruschetta
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.314Z'
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: /images/recipes/tomatobruschetta.jpeg
servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - ½ large baguette
  - 200g cherry tomatoes
  - 1 garlic clove
  - Handful fresh basil leaves
  - ½ tsp dried oregano
  - 'Extra-virgin olive oil, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    Slice the baguette into 1cm thick slices. Add to large pan on a medium heat,
    and cook until lightly toasted. Turn over and toast the other side.
  - Chop the tomatoes into small chunks. Chop the garlic clove in half.
  - >-
    Rub the inside of the garlic onto each slice of toasted bread for a few
    seconds. Top each slice with tomatoes and herbs. Drizzle with olive oil and
    serve.
