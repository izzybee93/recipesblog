---
title: "Quinoa, greens and hummus salad bowl"
date: "2026-03-30"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/quinoa-greens-and-hummus-salad-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 60g quinoa
  - '¼ broccoli head, chopped'
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - '120g chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - '80g edamame, cooked'
  - '12 olives, chopped'
  - '10g fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 4 tbsp lemon and coriander hummus (see recipe)
  - '## Dressing'
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tsp chimichurri seasoning
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - In a small pan over medium heat, add the chickpeas and cook for 5 minutes. Add the smoked paprika and toss until coated. Cook for another 5-10 minutes, until slightly crispy.
  - To make the dressing, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil and herbs.
  - In a large bowl, add the broccoli, cucumber, edamame, chickpeas, olives, quinoa and coriander. Pour over the dressing and mix well. Serve topped with lemon and coriander hummus.
