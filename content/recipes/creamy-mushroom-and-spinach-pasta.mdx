servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '200g mushrooms, sliced'
  - 150ml soya milk
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp white miso paste
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - Juice of Â½ lemon
  - 'Black pepper, to taste'
  - 2 large handfuls spinach
directions:
  - Cook the pasta.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil and cook the garlic on a medium heat
    for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  - >-
    In a measuring jug, whisk together the soya milk, miso paste, olive oil,
    nutritional yeast and seasoning. Add more milk as needed.
  - >-
    Pour the sauce into the pan of mushrooms to heat through. Stir frequently
    until slightly thickened. Stir in the lemon juice and simmer for a few more
    minutes.
  - >-
    Add the pasta and spinach. Stir until heated through and the spinach has
    wilted. Season to taste.
