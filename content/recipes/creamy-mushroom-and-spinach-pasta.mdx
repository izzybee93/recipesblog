title: Creamy mushroom and spinach pasta
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.280Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: /images/recipes/creamy-mushroom-and-spinach-pasta.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '250g mushrooms, sliced'
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2 large handfuls spinach
  - '## Sauce'
  - 150ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp white miso paste
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the olive oil and cook the garlic on a medium heat
    for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  - >-
    In a measuring jug, whisk together the soya milk, miso paste, olive oil,
    nutritional yeast and seasoning. Add more milk as needed.
  - >-
    Pour the sauce into the pan of mushrooms and stir frequently
    until slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp of pasta water as needed. Stir in the lemon juice.
  - >-
    Add the pasta and spinach. Stir until heated through and the spinach has
    wilted. Season to taste. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
