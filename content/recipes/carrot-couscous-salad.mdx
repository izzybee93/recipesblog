---
title: Carrot couscous salad
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.268Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/carrot-couscous-salad.png
servings: serves 3-4
ingredients:
  - 200g couscous
  - '2 large carrots, grated'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 50g sultanas or dried cranberries (optional)
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
directions:
  - >-
    Place the couscous in a medium bowl, and add boiling water until covered,
    half a centimeter above the couscous. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  - >-
    Run a fork through the couscous, then mix in the spices, seasoning and olive
    oil.
  - Add the carrot, chickpeas and dried fruit and mix well. Serve chilled.
