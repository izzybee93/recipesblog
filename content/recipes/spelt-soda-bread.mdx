---
title: "Spelt soda bread"
date: "2026-03-19"
categories:
  - bread
featured_image: "/images/recipes/spelt-soda-bread.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 loaf"
ingredients:
  - 350g wholemeal spelt flour
  - 115g white spelt flour or plain flour
  - 1½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - ½ tsp fine salt
  - 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 430ml non-dairy milk
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  - Add the apple cider vinegar to a measuring jug, then add the milk and set aside for 2 minutes.
  - In a large bowl, mix together the flours, bicarbonate of soda and salt. Add the buttermilk and mix until a soft, slightly sticky dough forms.
  - Knead lightly in the bowl for 1 minute. Add a little extra flour if needed.
  - Form a ball of dough and place on the baking tray. Cut a deep cross in the centre, almost to the bottom. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a golden crust forms. The loaf should sound hollow when tapped underneath.
  - Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
