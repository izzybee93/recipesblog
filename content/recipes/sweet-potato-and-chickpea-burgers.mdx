---
title: "Sweet potato and chickpea burgers"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sweet-potato-and-chickpea-burgers.jpeg"
servings: "makes 6"
ingredients:
  - 1 medium sweet potato, chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 40g oats
  - Juice ½ lime
  - ¼ tsp miso paste
  - 1 ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 3 tbsp sunflower seeds
  - Small handful fresh coriander
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray with parchment paper. 
  - Place the sweet potato on the tray and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Bake for 20 minutes, until softened.
  - In a food processor, process the oats to create a flour. Add the sweet potato, chickpeas, ½ tbsp olive oil, lime juice, miso paste, spices and sunflower seeds. Process until almost smooth, then add the coriander and process for another few seconds.
  - Form 6 burgers and place on the baking tray. Bake for 30 min until browned, flipping the burgers after 20 minutes. 
  - If freezing, slightly underbake the burgers. Freeze flat and then transfer to an airtight container.
  - Serve with grilled veggies and a creamy sauce or plant based mayo.
