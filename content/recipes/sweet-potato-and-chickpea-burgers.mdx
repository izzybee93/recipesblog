---
title: "Sweet potato and chickpea burgers"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sweet-potato-and-chickpea-burgers.jpeg"
servings: "makes 6"
ingredients:
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 20g oats
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two baking trays with parchment paper. 
  - Bake the sweet potato for 20 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  - In a food processor, process the oats to create a flour. Add the sweet potato, chickpeas, olive oil and spices, and process until almost smooth.
  - Form 6 small burgers and place on the prepared baking tray. Bake for 25 min until browned, flipping the burgers after 15 minutes.
  - If freezing, slightly underbake the burgers. Freeze flat and then transfer to an airtight container.
