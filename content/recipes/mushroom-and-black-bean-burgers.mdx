---
title: "Mushroom and black bean burgers"
date: "2026-03-21"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mushroom-and-black-bean-burgers.jpeg"
servings: "makes 6"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '150g mushrooms, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp soy sauce
  - ½ tsp miso paste
  - 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground cumin
  - ½ tsp dried thyme
  - 40g rolled oats
  - '2 tbsp walnuts, finely chopped'
directions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray with parchment paper."
  - "Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for about 5 minutes until browned. Add the garlic and cook for another minute."
  - "Place the black beans in a large bowl and mash well with a fork. Add the mushrooms, tomato paste, soy sauce, miso paste, balsamic vinegar, spices, thyme, oats and walnuts. Combine well and shape into 6 patties. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until crispy."
  - "If freezing, slightly underbake the burgers. Freeze flat and then transfer to an airtight container."
