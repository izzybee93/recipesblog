---
title: "Pesto risotto with tomato, spinach and chickpeas"
date: "2025-11-08"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pesto-risotto-with-tomato-spinach-and-chickpeas.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 400g cherry tomatoes
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 1 tsp paprika
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 onion, chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 200g risotto rice
  - 1.8l vegetable stock 
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 15g nutritional yeast
  - 75g basil pesto (see recipe) 
  - 90g spinach
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two baking trays. 
  - Place the cherry tomatoes on the largest tray, toss in 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 25 minutes.
  - Place the chickpeas on the other tray and toss with the paprika and garlic powder. Roast for 20-30 minutes until crispy.
  - In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp of olive oil. Add the onion and cook for about 3 minutes until it softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  - Stir in the rice and cook for a minute. Add a quarter of the stock and cook until nearly absorbed, stirring frequently. Gradually add stock and cook for 20 minutes.
  - When almost all the stock is absorbed, stir in pesto, lemon juice and nutritional yeast. 
  - Add the spinach and stir until wilted. Season with salt and ground black pepper. Divide the risotto into 4 serving bowls. Top with the roasted tomatoes and spiced chickpeas.
