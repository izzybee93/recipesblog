---
title: Peanut butter coconut bites
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.297Z'
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: /images/recipes/peanut-butter-coconut-bites.jpeg
servings: makes 16-20
ingredients:
  - 125g crunchy peanut butter
  - 85g oats
  - 40g shredded coconut
  - 30g hemp seeds (optional)
  - 2 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - 2-3 tbsp water
directions:
  - >-
    Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix. Gradually add the water and mix
    until a cookie dough consistency forms.
  - >-
    Roll into small balls and place on a lined baking tray or container. Place
    in the freezer for 10 minutes or in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before
    serving. Store for up to 5 days in the fridge or freeze for up to 3 months.
---
