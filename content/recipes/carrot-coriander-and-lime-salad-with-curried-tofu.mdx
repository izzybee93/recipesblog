---
title: "Carrot, coriander and lime salad with curried tofu"
date: "2026-06-26"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/carrot-coriander-and-lime-salad-with-curried-tofu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '1 small lettuce, chopped'
  - '2 medium carrots, peeled into ribbons'
  - '¼ cucumber, sliced'
  - 120g green lentils
  - '15g fresh coriander, finely chopped'
  - Crispy curried tofu (see recipe)
  - '1 tbsp pumpkin seeds, peanuts, or cashews, toasted'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Make the crispy curried tofu.
  - In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, carrot, cucumber, lentils and coriander.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  - Serve topped with the tofu and toasted pumpkin seeds.
