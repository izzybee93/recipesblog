  - sauces
featured_image: /images/recipes/tahinisauce.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp tahini
  - Juice of ¼ lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 1 tbsp water
directions:
  - >-
    Mix together the tahini, lemon juice, garlic and salt. Add the water and mix
    again. Continue to add another tbsp water and mix until desired consistency
    is reached.
