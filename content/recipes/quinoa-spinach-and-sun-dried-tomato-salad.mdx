---
title: 'Quinoa, spinach and sun-dried tomato salad'
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.300Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/quinoa-spinach-and-sun-dried-tomato-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - 120g quinoa
  - '1 red pepper, chopped'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 250g spinach
  - '8 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped'
  - '12 olives, chopped'
  - 'Small handful fresh basil, chopped'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ large lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Cook and rinse the quinoa.
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare a large baking tray. Add the pepper and chickpeas, brush with olive oil and roast for 10-15 minutes.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the quinoa, roasted veggies, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, spinach and basil.
  - >-
    Whisk together all the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug. Pour
    over the salad and mix well.
