title: 'Quinoa, spinach and sun-dried tomato salad'
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.300Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/qunioaspinachsalad.jpeg
servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - 120g quinoa
  - '250g baby spinach, torn or shredded'
  - 'Handful basil leaves, torn or shredded'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - '1 red pepper, chopped'
  - '8 sundries tomatoes, chopped'
  - '10 large olives, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice ½ large lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp turmeric
  - Ground black pepper
  - Salt to taste
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare a baking tray. Roast the pepper for
    10-15 minutes.
  - >-
    Meanwhile, cook the quinoa for 15-20 minutes, until softened with a slight
    crunch.
  - >-
    Transfer the quinoa to a large bowl and combine with all the veggies and
    chickpeas.
  - >-
    To make the dressing, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic,
    turmeric, salt and pepper. Pour over the salad and mix well.
