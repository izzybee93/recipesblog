---
title: "Raspberry and lemon muffins"
date: "2026-04-04"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/raspberry-and-lemon-muffins.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 325g self-raising flour
  - ¾ tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 110g caster sugar
  - 275ml soya milk
  - 100g vegetable oil
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - Zest of 1 lemon
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 220g fresh or frozen raspberries
  - Lemon icing (optional, see recipe)
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a muffin tin with liners.
  - In a large bowl, add the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, salt and sugar. Mix until well combined.
  - In a measuring jug, whisk together the milk, oil, apple cider vinegar, vanilla extract and lemon zest. Add to the flour mixture and mix until just combined.
  - Set aside 12 raspberries for decoration. Fold in the remaining raspberries until just combined.
  - Divide the batter between muffin cases. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean.
  - Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then place on a cooling rack to cool completely.
  - Make the icing. Drizzle over the muffins and top each muffin with a raspberry.
---
