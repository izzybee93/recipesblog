---
title: "Pomegranate compote"
date: "2026-02-26"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pomegranate-compote.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 small jar"
ingredients:
  - 170g pomegranate seeds
  - 20g caster sugar
  - 1 tbsp orange juice
  - 1½ tsp lemon zest
  - pinch sea salt
directions:
  - In a pan, combine the pomegranate seeds, sugar, salt, and orange juice. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  - Reduce the heat and leave to simmer for 5 more minutes to thicken slightly. Remove from the heat and stir in the lemon zest.
  - Serve warm with pancakes, or leave to cool and thicken. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 2 months.
---
