---
title: "Pomegranate Compote"
date: "2026-02-26"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pomegranate-compote.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 small jar"
ingredients:
  - 170g pomegranate seeds
  - 3 tbsp sugar
  - 1 tbsp orange juice
  - 1½ tsp lemon zest
  - pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Add the pomegranate seeds, sugar and orange juice to a pot, stir and bring to the boil. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  - Reduce the heat and leave to simmer for 5 more minutes to thicken slightly.
  - Serve warm with pancakes, or leave to cool and thicken. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 2 months.
