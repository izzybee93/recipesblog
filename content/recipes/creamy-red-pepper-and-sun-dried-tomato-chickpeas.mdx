---
title: "Creamy red pepper and sun-dried tomato chickpeas"
date: "2026-06-10"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-red-pepper-and-sun-dried-tomato-chickpeas.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '1 red bell pepper, finely chopped'
  - '8 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - ½ tsp dried oregano
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 50g silken tofu
  - 300ml soya milk
  - 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 60g spinach
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Small handful fresh basil
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add the red pepper and sun-dried tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  - Stir in the tomato paste, paprika, chilli flakes, oregano and salt. Cook for a few minutes, then add the chickpeas.
  - In a blender, add the silken tofu, soya milk and nutritional yeast and blend until smooth. Add to the pan and simmer for 5-10 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  - Stir in the spinach until wilted. Remove from the heat and stir in the lemon juice and basil. Season to taste. Serve topped with ground black pepper and a slice of crusty bread.
