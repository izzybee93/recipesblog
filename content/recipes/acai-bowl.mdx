---
title: "Açai bowl"
date: "2025-10-15"
categories: ["breakfast"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/acai-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 100g frozen açai berries
  - 1 banana
  - 50ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water
  - 25g strawberries, sliced
  - 1 tbsp goji berries
  - 2 tbsp granola
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
directions:
  - Add the açai berries, half the banana, soya milk and chia seeds to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  - Transfer to a bowl and top with the remaining sliced banana, berries granola and hemp seeds.
---
