---
title: "Super green shakshuka"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/super-green-shakshuka.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g frozen peas
  - 150g spinach
  - Large handful fresh coriander
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - 150ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp tahini
  - 1 garlic clove, chopped
  - ½ onion, finely chopped
  - 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  - 1 400g butter beans, drained and rinsed
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - Pinch sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Place the peas and spinach in a bowl and cover with boiling water. Leave for 5 minutes to soften the peas and wilt the spinach. Drain and transfer to a blender. 
  - Add the coriander (reserving a few pieces for garnish), lime juice, soya milk, tahini, garlic and salt. Blend until smooth, adding a splash more almond milk if needed.
  - Heat the olive oil in a large pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until soft. Add the peppers and cook for another 10 minutes. Stir in the butter beans.
  - Add the green sauce and combine well. Cook for a few more minutes until heated through, adding more milk as needed.
  - Garnish with coriander and ground black pepper. Serve with toasted sourdough.
---
