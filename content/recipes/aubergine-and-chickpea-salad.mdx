servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed 
  - 80g rocket
  - 6 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  - 4 tbsp olives or artichoke hearts, chopped
  - Small handful fresh basil, chopped
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon or 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar 
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare 2 baking trays. Place the aubergine on a tray, brush with olive oil and roast for 25 minutes. Add the chickpeas, smoked paprika and dried oregano to the other tray. Mix until coated, then roast for 10 minutes.
  - In a large bowl, add the rocket, basil, sun-dried tomatoes, olives or artichokes, aubergine and chickpeas.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
