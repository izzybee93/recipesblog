---
title: "Aubergine and chickpea salad"
date: "2026-02-08"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/aubergine-and-chickpea-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed 
  - 50g rocket
  - 12 olives, chopped
  - 6 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  - Small handful fresh basil, finely chopped (optional)
  - Juice of ½ lemon, or 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar 
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the aubergine, brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 15 minutes. Add the chickpeas and roast for another 10 minutes.
  - In a large bowl, add the rocket, basil, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, aubergine and chickpeas.
  - To make the dressing, whisk together the lemon juice, garlic and remaining 2 tbsp olive oil in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
