title: Chickpea grain bowl with tahini sauce
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.316Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/chickpea-grain-bowl-with-tahini-sauce.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '100g rice or quinoa, rinsed'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '## 1-2 roasted veggies:'
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 2 carrots, chopped
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 200g mushrooms, sliced
  - 150g baby corn, chopped
  - ¼ broccoli, chopped into florets
  - ¼ cauliflower, chopped into florets
  - '## 1-2 fresh veggies:'
  - 150g sugar snap peas
  - ¼ cucumber, chopped
  - 150g cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - '## Sauce'
  - Tahini sauce (see recipe)
directions:
  - Cook the rice or quinoa.
  - >-
    In a large pan, add the chickpeas and spices. Toss together and cook on a medium-high heat until crispy.
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the veggies to the tray and brush with the olive oil. Roast for 15-25 minutes, or 
    alternatively, cook in an air fryer for 10-15 minutes, until softened and slightly browned.
  - >-
    To assemble the bowls, add the rice or quinoa, then top with roasted veggies, fresh veggies and chickpeas.
    Serve with a drizzle of tahini sauce.
