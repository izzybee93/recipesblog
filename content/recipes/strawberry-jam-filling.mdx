title: Strawberry jam filling
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.310Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/strawberryjam.png
servings: serves 1 cake
ingredients:
  - '450g fresh strawberries, stems removed'
  - 150g granulated white sugar
  - 2 tbsp lemon juice
directions:
  - Add strawberries to a food processor and pulse until puréed.
  - >-
    In a saucepan, add the strawberry purée, sugar and lemon juice. Simmer on
    medium-low heat for 20-25 minutes until very thick.
  - Remove from the heat and allow the jam to cool completely.
