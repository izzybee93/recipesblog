---
title: Blackberry and banana smoothie bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.263Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/blackberry-and-banana-smoothie-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, sliced'
  - 50g blackberries
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - 50ml plant milk
  - ¼ tsp lemon zest (optional)
  - 1 tbsp granola
  - 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, ⅔ blackberries, nut butter, chia seeds, oats, 
    plant milk and lemon zest until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with remaining blackberries, granola and
    pumpkin seeds.
