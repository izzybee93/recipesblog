servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 large sweet potato, chopped'
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - '100g edamame or chickpeas, cooked'
  - '½ small cucumber, chopped'
  - '120g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp hummus (see recipe)
  - '## Dressing'
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - ½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare
    a large baking tray. Place the sweet potato on the tray and brush with the
    oil and smoked paprika. Roast for 20 minutes.
  - >-
    Whisk together all the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug.
  - >-
    Assemble the bowls, pour over the dressing, and top with a spoonful of
    hummus.
