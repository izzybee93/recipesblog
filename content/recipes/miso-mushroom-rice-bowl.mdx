---
title: "Miso mushroom rice bowl"
date: "2026-02-23"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/miso-mushroom-rice-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 portobello mushrooms, sliced
  - 100g edamame, cooked
  - ¼ small red cabbage, shredded
  - 1 large carrot, grated
  - ¼ cucumber, sliced
  - ½ avocado, sliced
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - Miso and ginger dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, browning each side.
  - Make the dressing.
  - Assemble the bowls with rice and add the veggies. Poir over the dressing and top with sesame seeds.
---
