---
title: "Miso mushroom rice bowl"
date: "2026-02-23"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/miso-mushroom-rice-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 portobello mushrooms, sliced
  - 100g edamame, cooked
  - 100g red cabbage, shredded
  - 2 medium carrots, grated
  - ¼ large cucumber, sliced
  - ½ avocado, sliced
  - 2 large handfuls baby spinach
  - 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp rice vinegar 
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - 1 ½ tsp miso paste
  - ¾ tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tsp fresh ginger
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - ¾ tbsp water
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, browning each side.
  - To make the dressing, place everything in a blender and blend until smooth.
  - Assemble the bowls with rice and spinach, followed by all the remaining veggies. Drizzle with the dressing and top with sesame seeds.
