---
title: "Herby chickpea salad"
date: "2025-10-16"
categories:
  - salad
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/herby-chickpea-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-3"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  - ¼ sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - 1 ½ 400g cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 200g red and yellow tomatoes, halved
  - ½ medium cucumber, diced
  - 15-20 kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  - Small handful fresh parsley, chopped
  - Small handful fresh dill, chopped
  - Small handful fresh mint, chopped
directions:
  - To make the dressing, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, mustard, salt, and black pepper.
  - In a large bowl, add the chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber and olives. Pour over the dressing and toss to coat. Add the herbs and toss again. Season to taste and serve.
---
