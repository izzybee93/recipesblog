---
title: "Moroccan style butternut squash and chickpea traybake"
date: "2025-10-21"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/moroccan-style-butternut-squash-and-chickpea-traybake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - ½  butternut squash, chopped
  - ½ head cauliflower, chopped into florets
  - 1 red pepper, chopped
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - ½ red onion, cut into wedges
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 3 tbsp water
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tsp ground coriander
  - 1½ tsp ground cumin
  - ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Place the butternut squash on the tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes.
  - Whisk the remaining olive oil, water and spices together in a small bowl.
  - Remove the tray from the oven and add the cauliflower, pepper, chickpeas and onion. Drizzle the spice mixture on top and brush until coated.
  - Roast for 15-20 minutes or until all the vegetables are tender and starting to brown.
