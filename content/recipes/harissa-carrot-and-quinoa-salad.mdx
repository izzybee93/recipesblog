---
title: "Harissa carrot and quinoa salad"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/harissa-carrot-and-quinoa-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 100g quinoa
  - 1 ½ 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - 50g kale
  - 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 50g baby spinach
  - 2 large carrots, grated
  - 1 Romano pepper, thinly sliced
  - 5 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  - 10 olives, sliced
  - Small handful fresh coriander, finely chopped
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tsp harissa paste
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tbsp fresh orange juice
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Heat a large pan over medium heat and add the chickpeas. Cook for 5 minutes, then add the spices. Cook for 5-10 minutes, until slightly crispy.
  - In a large bowl, add the kale and massage with 1 tsp olive oil to soften the leaves. Set aside for at least 5 minutes. Transfer to a chopping board and finely chop, then return the bowl.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all the ingredients together in a small measuring jug.
  - Add the spinach, carrots, pepper, pomegranate seeds, olives, chickpeas and quinoa to the kale and gently mix.
  - Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Divide between serving bowls and top fresh coriander.
