---
title: "Beetroot and butterbean couscous salad"
date: "2025-12-15"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-and-butterbean-couscous-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g dry couscous
  - 2 beetroots, chopped
  - Large handful cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - Large handful spinach 
  - 1 400g can butter beans, drained and rinsed
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Small handful parsley, chopped
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - In a large bowl, add the couscous and boiled water, pouring until the water is 0.5cm above the couscous. Set aside for 10 mins.
  - Using a fork, loosen the couscous. Add the garlic, sea salt, lemon juice, herbs, leftover beetroot juice and oil and mix well. Add the beetroot, tomatoes, spinach and butter beans, then mix together. Add a drizzle more olive oil, season with black pepper and stir again. Serve chilled.
