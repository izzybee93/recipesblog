---
title: Curried cauliflower
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.281Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/curriedcauliflower.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 small cauliflower, cut into quarters'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1½ tsp garam masala
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 125g unsweetened plant-based yoghurt
  - Juice of 1 small lemon
  - 2 tbsp toasted flakes almonds
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Fry the cauliflower on each side
    until browned. Add 150ml water and cover with a lid. Cook over medium heat
    for 10-15 minutes.
  - >-
    To make the sauce, combine the remaining tbsp of olive oil, tomato paste,
    spices, yoghurt and lemon juice. Mix until creamy, then warm through in a
    small pan over a medium-low heat for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    Serve the cauliflower on a bed of sauce and topped with a drizzle more of
    the sauce and toasted flaked almonds.
