---
title: Curried cauliflower
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.281Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/curried-cauliflower.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 small cauliflower, cut into quarters'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tbsp toasted flakes almonds
  - '## Sauce'
  - 120g cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp garam masala
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2-4 tbsp water
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Fry the cauliflower on each side
    until browned. Add 150ml water and cover with a lid. Cook over medium heat
    for 10-15 minutes.
  - >-
    To make the sauce, add all the ingrdients to a blender and blend until smooth. Add more water as needed to reach a creamy consistency. Transfer the sauce to a small pan 
    and warm gently over medium-low heat for 3–5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - >-
    Serve the cauliflower on a bed of sauce and topped with a drizzle more of
    the sauce and toasted flaked almonds.
