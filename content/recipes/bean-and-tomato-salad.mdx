title: "Bean and tomato salad"
date: "2026-06-17"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/bean-and-tomato-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '80g kidney beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '80g pinto beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '80g black eyed beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '200g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - 120g sweetcorn
  - '12 olives, chopped'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '2 tsp dried herbs, such as oregano, basil and parsley'
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
directions:
  - Combine all the beans and veggies in a large bowl.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all the ingredients together in a jug. Pour over the salad and toss until coated.
