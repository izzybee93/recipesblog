---
title: "Gnocchi with roasted aubergine and tomato"
date: "2026-05-28"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/gnocchi-with-roasted-aubergine-and-tomato.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '1 large aubergine, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 300g passata
  - '120g chickpeas'
  - 1 tsp capers
  - ½ tsp maple syrup
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 300g gnocchi
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 'Handful fresh basil, chopped'
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the aubergine, brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes, until softened.'
  - 'In a large pan over medium heat, heat the remaining tbsp olive oil. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add the passata and 250ml water. Stir in the chickpeas, maple syrup and salt. Cover with a lid and simmer for 10 minutes.'
  - 'Stir in the gnocchi, cover and simmer for 5 minutes.'
  - Stir in the aubergine. Remove from the heat and stir in the basil. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
