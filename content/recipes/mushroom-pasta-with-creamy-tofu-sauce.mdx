---
title: "Mushroom pasta with creamy tofu sauce"
date: "2026-02-15"
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mushroom-pasta-with-creamy-tofu-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 250g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  - Creamy tofu sauce (see recipe)
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Cook the pasta, reserving some of the pasta water.
  - In a large pan, heat the oil and cook the garlic on a medium heat for 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  - Make the creamy tofu sauce. Add to the pan and gently heat through. Stir in 2 tbsp of pasta water, adding more as needed.
  - Add the pasta. Stir until combined and heated through. Season to taste.
