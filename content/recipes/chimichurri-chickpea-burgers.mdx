title: Chimichurri chickpea burgers
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.273Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/chimichurrichickpeaburgers.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 garlic cloves
  - Large handful fresh coriander
  - '* 2 tsp chimichurri seasoning'
  - ½ tsp ground coriander
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 40g oats
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - >-
    Add all ingredients to a food processor and blend until a coarse mixture
    forms. Scrape down the side, add 1-2 tbsp water and pulse again.
  - >-
    Divide the mixture in 2 and place on the tray. Shape into 2 large burgers
    and smooth out using the back of a spoon.
  - 'Bake for 35-40 minutes, flipping the burgers halfway.'
  - >-
    Optionally serve in a bun with tomato, lettuce and tahini sauce (see
    recipe).
