title: Meringue drops
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.293Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/meringue-drops.jpeg
servings: makes ~50 drops
ingredients:
  - 60ml aquafaba
  - ¼ tsp apple cider vinegar
  - 75g caster sugar
  - 2 tsp beetroot juice
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl, beat chickpea water and vinegar with an electric whisk
    until stiff peaks form. Whip until the mixture does not slide down the bowl
    when turned upside down.
  - >-
    Add the sugar one tbsp at a time and whipping well after each addition. The
    mixture should become glossy and thick.
  - Pre-heat the oven to 100º C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  - "Take a clean ziplock bag and trickle 1 tsp\_of beetroot juice inside to coats the insides of the bag. Spoon meringue mixture into the bag, laid flat so the beetroot juice doesn’t run to the corner."
  - "Cut a small corner off of the bag.\_Hold perpendicular to the tray and squeeze small blobs of the mixture onto the tray and quickly pull away after each blob, allowing space in-between each meringue."
  - >-
    Bake for 75 minutes, then turn the oven off but leave the meringue drops
    inside for another 45-60 minutes for the meringues to dry out fully. Store
    in an airtight container.
