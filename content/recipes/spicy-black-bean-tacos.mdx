---
title: "Spicy black bean tacos"
date: "2026-03-05"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/spicy-black-bean-tacos.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - Spicy black beans (see recipe)
  - 80g sweetcorn
  - ½ avocado
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - '4-6 taco wraps, warmed'
directions:
  - Make the spicy black beans. Stir in the sweetcorn.
  - In a small bowl, mash the avocado with the lime juice and salt.
  - Assemble the tacos and top with guacamole.
