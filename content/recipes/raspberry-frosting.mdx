---
title: "Raspberry frosting"
date: "2025-11-18"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/raspberry-frosting.jpeg"
servings: "1 cake or 12 muffins"
ingredients:
  - 60g frozen raspberries
  - 1 tbsp caster sugar
  - 75g vegan butter
  - 200g icing sugar 
  - Â½ tsp vanilla extract
directions:
  - In a small saucepan, add the raspberries and sugar over medium heat. Stir frequently for a few minutes, until the raspberries have liquified and the sugar has dissolved.
  - Strain the mixture over a bowl to remove the seeds.
  - In medium bowl, beat the vegan butter until creamy. Gradually add the icing sugar and beat until light and fluffy. Beat in the vanilla. Add the raspberry one tbsp at a time until it reaches the desired consistency and flavour.
