title: "Teriyaki tofu bowl"
date: "2025-12-04"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/teriyaki-tofu-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g quinoa
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil 
  - 50g tofu, cubed
  - 50g edamame, cooked
  - 1 small beetroot, chopped 
  - Â¼ cucumber, chopped
  - 2-4 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - Heat the sesame oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook for a few minutes on each side until browned.
  - Assemble the bowl with the quinoa, topped with tofu, edamame, cucumber and beetroot. Serve with a drizzle of teriyaki sauce and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
