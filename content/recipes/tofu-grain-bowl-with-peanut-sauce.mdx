title: "Tofu grain bowl with peanut sauce"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tofu-grain-bowl-with-peanut-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice or quinoa
  - 280g firm tofu, cubed
  - ¼ cucumber, sliced
  - Peanut sauce (see recipe)
  - Small handful coriander, chopped 
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - '## Any 2 veggies:'
  - 100g edamame, cooked
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - ¼ broccoli, chopped
  - 100g mushroom, chopped
  - 1 small aubergine, chopped
  - '## Marinade'
  - 50ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  - ½ tsp harissa paste
  - ½ tbsp maple syrup
  - ½ tbsp soy sauce 
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the rice or quinoa.
  - Whisk the marinade ingredients together in a small bowl.
  - Place the tofu in a shallow dish and pour over the marinade. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - Place the tofu, veggies and any leftover marinade on the tray and bake for 20 minutes, until the tofu is golden brown and crispy.
  - Make the peanut sauce.
  - Assemble the bowls with rice or quinoa, tofu, veggies and cucumber slices. Drizzle over the peanut sauce, and garnish with coriander and sesame seeds.
