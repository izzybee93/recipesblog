---
title: "Shredded tofu tacos"
date: "2025-11-09"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/shredded-tofu-tacos.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 280g extra firm tofu
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  -  ½ tsp chilli powder
  -  ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder 
  - ½ red onion, sliced
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 2 large handfuls cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - Handful fresh coriander, chopped 
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 4-6 corn tortillas
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 190°C. Prepare a large baking tray.
  - Shred the tofu with a grater and transfer to a bowl. Drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil, the soy sauce, smoked paprika, chilli powder, cumin and garlic powder, and mix well.
  - Spread the tofu evenly on the tray bake for 15-20 minutes, until crispy.
  - Heat the remaining tbsp olive oil In a frying pan. Add the onion and cook for a few minutes, followed by the garlic. Cook for a few minutes until the onion starts to soften. Stir in tomatoes and cook for 2 more minutes. Stir in the lime juice, coriander and salt, to taste. 
  - Heat the tortillas. Assemble the tacos with the vegetables and tofu. Serve with guacamole or your favourite toppings.
