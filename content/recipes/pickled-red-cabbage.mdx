---
title: "Pickled red cabbage"
date: "2026-05-16"
categories:
  - salad
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pickled-red-cabbage.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 jar"
ingredients:
  - '½ small red cabbage, finely sliced'
  - 180ml apple cider vinegar or rice vinegar
  - 180ml water
  - 1 tbsp caster sugar
  - 1 tsp sea salt
  - '## Optional flavours'
  - 1 tsp mustard seeds
  - 1 tsp coriander seeds
  - 1 tsp caraway seeds
  - 1 tsp cumin seeds
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes 
directions:
  - Put the cabbage in a large mason jar, pushing down until lightly packed.
  - In a small pan, add the water and vinegar. Bring to a simmer, then stir in the sugar and salt until dissolved. Add the optional flavouring.
  - Pour into the jar, ensuring all the cabbage is covered. Loosely cover with the lid and set aside for 1 hour. Seal tightly and transfer to the fridge for up to 3 weeks.
  - Serve as a side with a veggie grain bowl, tacos or tossed in salad.
