title: Chickpea and vegetable teriyaki rice bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.271Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/chickpeavegetablericebowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 150g rice
  - '1 400g chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp turmeric
  - 6 baby corn
  - '½ broccoli, chopped'
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - '2 carrots, sliced'
  - Drizzle of teriyaki sauce
directions:
  - >-
    Cook the rice. Preheat the oven 180°C. Prepare a baking tray with baking
    paper.
  - >-
    Heat a pan and add the chickpeas. Cook on a medium heat for 5 minutes. Add
    the spices and toss to coat. Cook for another 5-10 minutes until crispy.
  - >-
    Place the broccoli on the baking tray and coat with the oil. Add the baby
    corn to the tray. Roast for 10-15 minutes.
  - >-
    Assemble the rice bowl and top with a drizzle of teriyaki or your favourite
    sauce.
