---
title: "Creamy tofu spread"
date: "2026-04-23"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-tofu-spread.jpeg"
servings: "serves 6"
ingredients:
  - '280g firm tofu, cut into large cubes'
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 1½ tsp miso paste
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Small handful fresh chives, finely chopped'
directions:
  - In a saucepan, bring water to the boil, then add the tofu and juice of ½ lemon. Simmer for 10 minutes. Drain and leave to cool for 2 minutes.
  - In a food processor, add the tofu, remaining lemon juice, miso paste and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy. Season to taste. Fold in the chives.
  - Transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate for 2 hours before serving. Spread on bagels, toasted sourdough or crackers. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days. 
---
