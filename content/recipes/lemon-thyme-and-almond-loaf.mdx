---
title: "Lemon, thyme and almond loaf"
date: "2026-06-27"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-thyme-and-almond-loaf.jpeg"
servings: "serves 10"
ingredients:
  - 250g self-raising flour
  - 15g cornflour
  - ¾ tsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  - Zest of 2 lemons
  - 60ml vegetable oil
  - 180ml soya milk
  - 120g caster sugar
  - 50g flaked almonds
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a loaf tin with baking paper.
  - In a large bowl, mix together the flour, cornflour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt.
  - In a measuring jug, combine the oil, soya milk and sugar. Stir in the thyme and lemon zest.
  - Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in 35g of the flaked almonds.
  - Pour the batter into the loaf tin and scatter over the remaining 15g flaked almonds.
  - Bake for 45-50 minutes, until lightly golden and a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  - Leave to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
---
