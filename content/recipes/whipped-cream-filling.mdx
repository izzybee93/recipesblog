title: Whipped cream filling
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.319Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/whipped-cream-filling.jpeg
servings: serves 16-20
ingredients:
  - 200g vegan double cream (Elmlea)
  - 1½ tbsp icing sugar
  - ¼ tsp vanilla extract
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the cream and whisk with a hand mixer on medium speed for 30 seconds. Mix in the icing sugar.
  - Continue to mix at a high speed until stiff peaks form.
  - Add the vanilla and mix on low for a few seconds to combine. Transfer to the fridge for at least 1 hour.
