---
title: "Beetroot, black bean and sweet potato bowl"
date: "2026-03-20"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/beetroot-black-bean-and-sweet-potato-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - '2 large beetroot, cooked and chopped'
  - '2 medium sweet potatoes, cut into wedges'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - 100g silken tofu
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 150ml vegetable stock
  - 60g spinach
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare 2 large baking trays.
  - Place the beetroot and garlic on a tray and coat with 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast for 20 minutes.
  - Place the sweet potato on the other tray and coat with 1 tbsp oil. Roast for 20-25 minutes.
  - Add the roasted beetroot, garlic, tahini, silken tofu, lemon juice and salt to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add a splash of water if needed.
  - In a large pan over medium heat, add the beetroot sauce. Stir in the black beans and vegetable stock, then simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through and slightly thickened. Stir in the spinach and cook for 2 more minutes, until just wilted.
  - Serve with the sweet potatoes and top with ground black pepper.
