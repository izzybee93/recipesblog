---
title: "Roasted vegetable and tomato pasta"
date: "2026-01-08"
categories: ["mains", "pasta"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-vegetable-and-tomato-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 160g pasta
  - 1 pepper, sliced
  - 1 courgette, sliced
  - 120g chickpeas (optional)
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 tbsp tomato purée
  - 1 tbsp mixed dried herbs
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - ½ tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tsp caster sugar
  - 1 tbsp capers
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
  - Small handful basil
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray and add the peppers, courgette and chickpeas. Drizzle over 1 tbsp olive oil and toss together. Roast for 20 minutes until softened.
  - In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp of olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the tomato purée and herbs and cook for another minute. Add the tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, sugar, capers, salt and black pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes until reduced. 
  - Cook the pasta.
  - Combine the pasta and vegetables with the sauce and heat through. Stir in the basil, and reserve a few leaves for garnish.
