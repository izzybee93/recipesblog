---
title: Berry and peanut butter porridge
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.259Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/berrypeanutbutterporridge.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 150ml non-dairy milk
  - 50g mixed berries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
directions:
  - >-
    In a small pan, add the oats, non-dairy milk and berries. Cook for a few
    minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently.
  - Serve topped with a drizzle of peanut butter.
