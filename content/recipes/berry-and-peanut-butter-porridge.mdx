title: Berry and peanut butter porridge

categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/berry-and-peanut-butter-porridge.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 75g mixed berries
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
directions:
  - >-
    In a small pan, add the oats, milk, chia seeds and berries. Cook for a few
    minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently.
  - Serve topped with a drizzle of peanut butter.
