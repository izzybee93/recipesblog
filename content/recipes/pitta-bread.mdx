---
title: "Pitta bread"
date: "2026-04-09"
categories:
  - bread
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pitta-bread.jpeg"
servings: "makes 8"
ingredients:
  - 400g plain flour
  - 8g sea salt
  - 7g instant yeast
  - 250ml warm water
  - 1 tsp granulated sugar
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
directions:
  - 'In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt and yeast.'
  - 'In a measuring jug, mix together the warm water and sugar. Stir in the oil. Pour into the flour mixture and mix until a rough dough forms.'
  - Turn the dough out onto a surface and knead for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  - 'Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and set aside in a warm place for about 1½ hours, or until doubled in size.'
  - Punch down the dough to release any air bubbles. Divide into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a ball. Cover with a damp cloth and set aside for 45 mins until roughly doubled in size.
  - 'Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Place a ball of dough on a floured surface and roll into a circle, roughly 20cm wide. Add to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on one side, until it starts to puff up and brown spots appear on the bottom. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes. Transfer to a plate and cover with kitchen towel to keep them soft and warm. Repeat with the remaining portions of dough.'
  - Store in an airtight container in the fridge or freezer.
---
