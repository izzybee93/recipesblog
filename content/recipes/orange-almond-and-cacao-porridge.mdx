---
title: "Orange, almond and cacao porridge"
date: "2026-02-15"
categories: ["breakfast"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/orange-almond-and-cacao-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 150ml non-dairy milk
  - 1 orange, peeled and chopped
  - 1/ tsp cinnamon 
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
  - 1 tsp cacao nibs
directions:
  - In a small pan, add the oats and non-dairy milk. Cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently. Stir in half the orange slices and cinnamon.
  - Serve topped with remaining orange slices, a drizzle of almond butter and a sprinkle of cacao nibs.
---
