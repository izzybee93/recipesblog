---
title: "Fruit buttermilk scones"
date: "2026-05-29"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/fruit-buttermilk-scones.jpeg"
servings: "makes 20"
ingredients:
  - '150g mixed dried fruit, such as cranberries, sultanas, raisins, cherries or blueberries'
  - 250ml boiling water
  - 550g self-raising flour
  - 60g caster sugar
  - 115g plant butter
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - 1½ tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 300ml plant milk
  - Soya milk for brushing
directions:
  - In a small bowl, add the dried fruit, then the boiling water and set aside.
  - 'Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line two large baking trays with paper.'
  - Add the apple cider vinegar to a measuring jug, then the milk. Set aside to form the buttermilk.
  - 'Mix the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and sugar in a bowl. Add the butter and mix it in with your fingers until it starts to look a bit like breadcrumbs.'
  - 'Drain the dried fruit, then stir into the flour mixture. Add the buttermilk a little at a time until the mixture comes together. Add a little more flour if the dough becomes too wet.'
  - 'Place the dough on a floured surface and roll out to about 2cm thick. Using a round cutter, cut out the scones. Place on the tray and brush with soya milk. Bake for 20 minutes or until risen and golden.'
