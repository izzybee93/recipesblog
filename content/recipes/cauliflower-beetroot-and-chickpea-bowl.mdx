---
title: "Cauliflower, beetroot and chickpea bowl"
date: "2026-02-01"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/cauliflower-beetroot-and-chickpea-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped
  - ½ cauliflower, chopped
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - 1 large beetroot, chopped
  - 2 tbsp lemon juice
  - 200g silken tofu
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 2 tsp fresh chives, finely chopped
  - 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare two trays with baking paper.
  - Place the sweet potato, cauliflower and half the chickpeas on a large tray and brush with olive oil. Add the spices and mix until coated. Bake for 25 minutes.
  - Place the beetroot on a small tray and bake for 20 minutes. Leave to cool for 10 minutes.
  - To make the sauce, add the remaining chickpeas, beetroot, silken tofu, lemon juice, maple syrup, garlic powder and salt to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  - Spoon the sauce into serving bowls. Top with the roasted veggies. Garnish with chives, pumpkin seeds and ground black pepper.
