---
title: "Miso and ginger stir fry sauce"
date: "2026-07-10"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/miso-and-ginger-stir-fry-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tbsp rice vinegar or lime juice
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - '1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced'
  - 125ml water
  - 1 tsp cornflour
directions:
  - In a small jug, mix all the ingredients together except the cornflour. Stir in the cornflour until dissolved.
  - Add to the stir fry and cook on a medium-high heat until the sauce thickens.
