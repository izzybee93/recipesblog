---
title: "Green cannellini bean pasta"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains", "pasta"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/green-cannellini-bean-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 160g pasta
  - 140g frozen peas
  - ½ white onion, chopped
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 100g kale or spinach
  - 1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  - 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 25g fresh basil leaves
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - Ground black pepper, to taste

directions:
  - Cook the pasta and peas. Reserve the water.
  - In a small pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  - To blanch the kale, place in a sieve and pour boiling water over it, then immediately rinse with cold water.
  - In a large blender, add the kale, beans, onion, 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, nutritional yeast, basil (reserving some for garnish) and 100ml of pasta water. Blend for 1 minute until smooth. Season to taste.
  - In the pan, combine the pasta and peas with sauce over low heat, adding a splash more pasta water if needed. 
  - Serve topped with black pepper and basil leaves.
