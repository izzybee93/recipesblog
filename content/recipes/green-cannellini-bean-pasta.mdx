title: "Green cannellini bean pasta"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/green-cannellini-bean-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g pasta
  - 150g peas
  - Â½ white onion, finely chopped
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 150g kale, roughly chopped
  - 1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  - 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 30g fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - Cook the pasta and peas. Reserve the water.
  - In a small frying pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  - To blanch the kale, place in a sieve and pour boiling water over it, then immediately rinse with cold water.
  - In a large blender, add the kale, beans, onion, garlic, lemon juice, nutritional yeast, basil (leaving a few for garnish) and ~100ml of pasta water. Blend for 2 minutes until smooth. Season to taste, adjusting the salt and lemon juice as needed.
  - In the pan, combine the pasta and peas with sauce over low heat, adding a splash more pasta water if needed. 
  - Serve topped with black pepper and a reserved basil leaves.
