---
title: "Mushroom and lentil tomato stew"
date: "2026-06-08"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mushroom-and-lentil-tomato-stew.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - '200g mushrooms, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tsp soy sauce
  - '1 400g can green lentils, drained and rinsed'
  - 350ml vegetable stock
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - Handful basil leaves
directions:
  - Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 8-10 minutes, until browned.
  - Stir in the tomato paste, tomatoes, lentils, balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, vegetable stock, salt and black pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, until thickened.
  - Remove from the heat and stir in the torn basil leaves. Serve with crusty bread.
