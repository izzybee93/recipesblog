title: "Tempeh grain bowl with peanut sauce"
date: "2026-06-02"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tempeh-grain-bowl-with-peanut-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 100g rice or quinoa
  - '120g tempeh, sliced'
  - '¼ cucumber, sliced'
  - '1 avocado, sliced'
  - 6 tbsp pickled red cabbage (see recipe)
  - Peanut sauce (see recipe)
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa or rice.
  - Heat a pan over medium heat. Add the tempeh and cook for 5-10 minutes, turning to brown each side.
  - Make the peanut sauce. Add 4 tbsp sauce to the tempeh and cook for 5 more minutes.
  - 'Assemble the bowls with the rice or quinoa, then top with tempeh, cucumber, cabbage and avocado. Drizzle over the remaining sauce and serve.'
