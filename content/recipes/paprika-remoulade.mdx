---
title: "Paprika remoulade"
date: "2026-07-04"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/paprika-remoulade.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-3"
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - '1 tbsp capers, finely chopped'
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Add all the ingredients to a small bowl and mix until smooth. Season to taste, then chill in the fridge for 15 minutes before serving.
  - Add to sandwiches, wraps, veggie burgers, or drizzle over crispy tofu, mushrooms, sweet potato or your favourite grain bowl.
