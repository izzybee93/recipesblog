servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g pasta
  - '1 aubergine, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 handfuls cherry tomatoes
  - 4 tbsp roasted red pepper pesto (see recipe)
  - A few fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the aubergine, brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 15 minutes. Add the tomatoes and roast for another 5 minutes.'
  - Make the roasted red pepper pesto.
  - Cook the pasta.
  - 'Toss together the pasta, pesto, aubergine, tomatoes and basil in the pan until coated. Stir until heated through. Serve with a garnish of basil leaves.'
