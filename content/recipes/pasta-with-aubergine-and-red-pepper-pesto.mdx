title: "Pasta with aubergine and red pepper pesto"
date: "2026-02-23"
categories:
  - "mains"
  - "pasta"
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pasta-with-aubergine-and-red-pepper-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g pasta
  - 1 aubergine, chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 handfuls cherry tomatoes
  - 4 tbsp roasted red pepper pesto (see recipe)
  - A few fresh basil leaves
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Add the aubergine, brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and roast for 20 minutes.
  - Make the roasted red pepper pesto.
  - Cook the pasta.
  - Toss together the pasta, pesto, aubergine and cherry tomatoes in the pan until coated. Stir until heated through. Serve with a garnish of fresh basil.
