---
title: "Smoky red pepper, sweet potato and chickpea bowl"
date: "2026-02-18"
categories: 
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/smoky-red-pepper-sweet-potato-and-chickpea-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 1 red Romano pepper, chopped
  - 1 ½ tbsp olive oil 
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 avocado, sliced
  - ½ smoky red pepper sauce (see recipe)
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two baking trays with baking paper. 
  - Place the sweet potato and chickpeas on a tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and the smoked paprika. Roast for 20-25 minutes.
  - Place the pepper on a tray and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Roast for 10-15 minutes. 
  - Make the red pepper sauce.
  - Assemble the bowls with sweet potato, chickpeas, pepper and avocado. Drizzle with red pepper sauce and top with a sprinkle of fresh coriander.
