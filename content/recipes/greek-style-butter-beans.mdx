---
title: "Greek style butter beans"
date: "2026-05-07"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/greek-style-butter-beans.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 carrot, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - ½ tsp paprika
  - 2 tsp dried oregano  
  - ¾ tsp sea salt
  - ⅛ tsp cinnamon
  - 2 400g cans butter beans, drained and rinsed
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 1 tsp sugar
  - 'Small handful fresh parsley, chopped'
  - '6 mint leaves, chopped'
directions:
  - Heat the oil in a large pan over medium-low heat. Add the onion and cook for 10 minutes. Add the carrot and garlic and cook for a few more minutes. Stir in the paprika, tomato paste, oregano, salt and cinnamon.
  - Add the butter beans, tomatoes and ½ can of water. Stir in the sugar. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove the lid and simmer for another 5-10 minutes until slightly thickened.
  - Remove from the heat and stir in the parsley and mint. Serve with crusty bread and salad.
