---
title: "Chocolate chickpea clusters"
date: "2025-12-29"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-chickpea-clusters.jpeg"
servings: "makes 12"
ingredients:
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 1 tsp cinnamon
  - 150g dark chocolate
  - 2 tbsp cranberries or goji berries (optional)
  - 2 tbsp chopped almonds or hazelnuts (optional)
directions:
  - Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Prepare a large baking tray with baking paper.
  - Dry the chickpeas with a paper towel and transfer to the tray. Coat with the cinnamon and bake for 35 minutes, mixing after about 20 minutes. Set aside to cool on a large plate.
  - Melt the chocolate using a bain-marie. Pour the chocolate over the chickpeas and mix well.
  - Scoop out 1 tbsp of this mixture at a time and add to the tray. Sprinkle over some chopped nuts and cranberries. Transfer to the fridge until set.
