---
title: "Sweet potato rainbow bowl"
date: "2026-03-04"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sweet-potato-rainbow-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped'
  - '1 aubergine, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - '120g edamame, cooked'
  - '150g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - Tahini dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare two baking trays with baking paper.
  - Place the sweet potato on a tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil and the smoked paprika. Roast for 20-25 minutes. Place the aubergine on a tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast for 20 minutes.
  - Prepare the tahini dressing.
  - Assemble the bowls with sweet potato, aubergine, edamame and tomatoes. Drizzle with tahini dressing.
