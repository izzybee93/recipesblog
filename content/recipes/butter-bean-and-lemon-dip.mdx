---
title: "Butter bean and lemon dip"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/butter-bean-and-lemon-dip.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can butter beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - Zest of ½ lemon
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 4 tbsp water
directions:
  - In a food processor, add the tahini and lemon juice and process until combined. Scrape down the sides as needed.
  - Add the olive oil, lemon zest, garlic and salt, and process for another 30 seconds.
  - Add the butter beans and process again. Add the water, a few tbsp at a time, and process until smooth and creamy.
  - Serve with veggies, crackers or toasted sourdough.
