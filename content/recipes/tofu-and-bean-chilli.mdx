---
title: "Tofu and bean chilli"
date: "2025-11-29"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/tofu-and-bean-chilli.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 280g extra-firm tofu, cubed
  - 1 large onion, finely chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp ground cumin
  - 1 tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp chilli powder
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - 1 large carrot, chopped
  - 1 pepper, chopped
  - 1 courgette, chopped
  - 1 400g can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  - 400ml vegetable stock
  - 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  - Handful of fresh coriander, finely chopped
directions:
  - In a large pan, heat the olive oil over a medium-high heat. Sauté the tofu for about 5 minutes, turning to brown each side.
  - Add the onion and cook over a medium hear for 3 minutes, then add the garlic and cook for another minute. Mix in the tomato paste, paprika, cumin, turmeric, chilli powder and sea salt.
  - Add the carrot, pepper and courgette and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the kidney beans, chopped tomatoes and vegetable stock and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  - Stir in half the coriander. Top with the remaining fresh coriander and serve with rice or crusty bread.
---
