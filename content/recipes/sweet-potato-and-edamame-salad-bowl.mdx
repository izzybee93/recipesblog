---
title: "Sweet potato and edamame salad bowl"
date: "2026-01-09"
categories: ["salad", "mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sweet-potato-and-edamame-salad-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 large sweet potato, chopped
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - 120g edamame, cooked
  - ½ small cucumber, chopped
  - Handful cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - Tahini dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Prepare the sweet potato in advance - preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare a large baking tray. Place the sweet potato on the tray and brush with the oil and smoked paprika. Roast for 20 minutes, leave to cool, then place in the fridge.
  - Assemble the bowls and pour over the dressing.
