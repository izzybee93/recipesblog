---
title: Crackers
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.279Z'
categories:
  - bread
  - snacks
featured_image: /images/recipes/crackers.jpeg
servings: makes ~50 crackers
ingredients:
  - 185g plain flour
  - ¼ tsp baking powder
  - 30g nutritional yeast (optional)
  - 2 tsps Italian herbs
  - ¾ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp garlic powder (optional)
  - ¼ tsp black pepper (optional)
  - 115ml water
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 190°C. Line two large baking trays with parchment paper.
  - >-
    In a medium bowl, add the flour, baking powder, nutritional yeast, herbs,
    salt, garlic powder and black pepper. Mix well to combine. Add the water and
    olive oil and mix until a dough forms.
  - >-
    Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface. Roll out as thin as
    possible (about 2mm thick).
  - >-
    Using a pizza cutter, cut the dough into a grid pattern. Transfer each
    cracker to the baking trays, leaving space intebween the crackers. Use a
    fork to prick each cracker 3 times.
  - >-
    Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the crackers are golden brown around the
    edges. Let cool completely in the tray. Store in an air-tight container for
    up to 1 week.
