title: Quinoa and black bean salad
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.299Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/quinoa-and-black-bean-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 2-3
ingredients:
  - 100g quinoa
  - '2 large tomatoes, chopped'
  - 160g sweetcorn
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 'Large handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - '1 avocado, chopped'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  - ¾ tsp cumin
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
directions:
  - >-
    Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - >-
    In a large bowl. Add the quinoa, tomatoes, sweetcorn, black
    beans and coriander, and mix well.
  - >-
    Whisk together all the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug. Pour
    over the salad and mix well. Add the avocado and gently mix.
