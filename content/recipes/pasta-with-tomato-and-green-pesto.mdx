---
title: "Pasta with tomato and green pesto"
date: "2026-02-28"
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pasta-with-tomato-and-green-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 180g pasta
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, finely chopped'
  - 200g cherry tomatoes
  - ½ green pesto (see recipe)
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Cook the pasta.
  - 'In a large pan, heat the oil over medium-low heat. Add the garlic and tomatoes and cook for a few minutes.'
  - Add the pesto and stir until heated and fragrant.
  - 'Add the pasta and a few tbsp of pasta water. Toss together until coated. Serve topped with ground black pepper.'
