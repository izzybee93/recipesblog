---
title: "Sweet potato and black bean salad"
date: "2026-02-20"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sweet-potato-and-black-bean-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-3"
ingredients:
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped
  - 1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed
  - 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  - ½ red onion, chopped
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 60g rocket
  - 15g fresh coriander, chopped
  - 1 medium avocado, sliced
  - "## Dressing"
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  - Add the sweet potato to a tray and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Bake for 25 minutes. 
  - Add the onion and pepper to the other tray and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Bake for 15 minutes. 
  - To make the dressing, whisk all the ingredients in a small measuring jug.
  - In a large bowl, add the black beans, rocket and coriander. Add the roasted veggies, pour over the dressing and gently mix. Top with the avocado slices and serve.
