---
title: "Sun-dried tomato hummus"
date: "2026-04-17"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sun-dried-tomato-hummus.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 3 tbsp tahini
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 clove garlic
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 40g sun-dried tomatoes
  - 2-4 tbsp water
directions:
  - Combine the tahini and lemon juice in a food processor and mix for 1 minute. Scrape the sides of the bowl then process for 30 more seconds.
  - 'Add the olive oil, garlic, paprika, salt. Process for 30 seconds, scrape down the sides, then add the sun-dried tomatoes and process until well blended.'
  - 'Add half of the chickpeas, and process for 30 seconds. Scrape the sides of the bowl, then add remaining chickpeas and process for another minute. Gradually add a tbsp water and blend until it reaches the desired consistency.'
  - Serve with a sprinkle of smoked paprika. Store in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to one week.
