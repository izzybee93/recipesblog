---
title: Chimichurri sweet potato and spiced cauliflower bowl
date: '2025-11-02'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/chimichurri-sweet-potato-and-spiced-cauliflower-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 50g quinoa
  - '1 large sweet potato, chopped'
  - '½ small cauliflower, chopped into florets'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp ground coriander
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 2 tsp chimichurri seasoning
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - >-
    Prepare a baking tray and add the sweet potato. Drizzle with ½ tbsp olive
    oil and the smoked paprika. Roast for 20-25 minutes.
  - >-
    Prepare a small baking tray and add the cauliflower. Drizzle with ½ tbsp
    olive oil and coat with the turmeric, cumin and coriander. Roast for 15
    minutes, until slightly browned.
  - >-
    To make the dressing, whisk together the lime, 1 tbsp olive oil, chimichurri
    seasoning, garlic powder, and salt.
  - >-
    To assemble the bowls, add the sweet potato and quinoa. Top with edamame,
    cauliflower, and cucumber. Pour over the dressing and serve.
---
