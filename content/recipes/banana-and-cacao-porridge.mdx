---
title: "Banana and cacao porridge"
date: "2026-04-12"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/banana-and-cacao-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 banana, sliced'
  - 1 tsp cacao nibs
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
directions:
  - 'Add the oats and milk to the pan and cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently. Add half the banana slices and continue to cook until heated through.'
  - 'Serve topped with the remaining banana, hemp seeds and a sprinkle of cacao nibs.'
---
