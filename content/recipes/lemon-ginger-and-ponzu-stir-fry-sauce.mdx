---
title: "Lemon, ginger and ponzu stir fry sauce"
date: "2026-05-19"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-ginger-and-ponzu-stir-fry-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tbsp ponzu sauce
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - '1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 125ml water
  - 1 tsp cornflour
directions:
  - 'In a small jug, mix all the ingredients together except the cornflour. Stir in the cornflour until dissolved.'
  - Add to the stir fry and cook on a medium-high heat until the sauce thickens.
---
