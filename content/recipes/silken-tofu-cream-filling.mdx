---
title: "Silken tofu cream filling"
date: "2026-02-26"
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/silken-tofu-cream-filling.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 cake"
ingredients:
  - 150g silken tofu
  - 1 tbsp maple syrup
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - ½ tsp vanilla
  - Pinch salt
directions:
  - 'Drain the tofu, then place on a tea towel and squeeze out the excess water.'
  - 'Add all the ingredients to a blender and blend for 1 minute, scraping down the sides as needed.'
  - Chill for 1 hour in the fridge to firm up slightly.
---
