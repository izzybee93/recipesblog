---
title: Baked falafel
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.253Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/baked-falafel.jpeg
servings: makes 12
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - '2 400g cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - '½ small red onion, roughly chopped'
  - '1 large handful fresh parsley, mostly leaves'
  - 1½ large handfuls fresh coriander
  - '4 cloves garlic, quartered'
  - 1 tsp fine sea salt
  - ½ tsp ground black pepper
  - ½ tsp ground cumin
  - ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  - >-
    In a food processor, combine all ingredients except the sesame oil and process until smooth,
    for about 1 minute.
  - >-
    Scoop out 2 tbsp of mixture at a time and form small patties. Place on the tray and brush with sesame oil.
  - >-
    Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden brown on both sides.
  - 'Serve with salad, tahini sauce and pitta.'
