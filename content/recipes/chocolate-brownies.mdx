servings: makes 16
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp ground flaxseed
  - 6 tbsp water
  - 115g brown sugar
  - 100g vegetable oil
  - "60ml water"
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract or vanilla bean paste
  - 60g cocoa powder
  - 110g almond flour
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - ¼ tsp baking powder
  - "50g dark chocolate chips"
directions:
  - >-
    Whisk together the flaxseed and water in a small bowl and set aside for 15 minutes to thicken.
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Line an 8x8 dish with parchment.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the flax egg, sugar and oil and mix until fully
    incorporated. Add the water and the vanilla and mix for another 2 minutes.
  - >-
    In a separate bowl, combine the cocoa powder, almond flour, baking powder
    and sea salt. Add to the wet mixture and stir until fully combined, with no
    lumps. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  - >-
    Pour into the pan and bake for 35-45 minutes. Allow to cool completely
    before slicing.
