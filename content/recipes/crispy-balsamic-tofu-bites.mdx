---
title: "Crispy balsamic tofu bites"
date: "2026-07-24"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crispy-balsamic-tofu-bites.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-4"
ingredients:
  - 225g extra-firm tofu
  - 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - '1 tbsp tomato paste, heaped'
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch sea salt
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a baking tray.
  - Crumble the tofu into small pieces and place in a shallow dish with a lid.
  - In a small bowl, mix the balsamic vinegar, maple syrup, tomato paste, garlic powder and salt. Pour over the tofu, cover with the lid and shake well to coat.
  - Bake for 20-25 minutes, mixing halfway, until golden and crispy. Alternatively, cook in an air fryer for 15 minutes.
  - Serve sprinkled on top of salad, pasta, or your favourite grain bowl.
