---
title: "Peanut butter and jam sandwich cake"
date: "2026-04-16"
categories:
  - cakes
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/peanut-butter-and-jam-sandwich-cake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 10"
ingredients:
  - 170g plain flour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - 80g caster sugar
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 200ml soya milk
  - ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 60g smooth peanut butter
  - '100g jam such as raspberry, strawberry, blueberry or blackcurrant'
  - 'Handful fresh berries, such as raspberry, strawberry, blueberry or blackcurrant'
  - '2 tbsp roasted peanuts, chopped'
  - '## Icing'
  - 100g smooth peanut butter
  - 25g maple syrup
  - 2 tbsp plant milk
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Prepare two 20cm cake tins.
  - 'In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate, sugar and salt.'
  - 'In a measuring jug, stir together the milk, vinegar, vanilla and peanut butter. Pour into the dry ingredients and beat to a smooth batter.'
  - 'Pour into the tins, cover loosely with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for a further 10-15 minutes until golden, and a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. Leave to cool for 10 minutes before removing from the tins. Leave to cool completely before assembling.'
  - 'To make the icing, mix the peanut butter, maple syrup and milk together until smooth.'
  - Spread the jam on one layer, then place the other cake on top. Spread the peanut icing over the top. Top with fresh berries and a sprinkle of peanuts.
