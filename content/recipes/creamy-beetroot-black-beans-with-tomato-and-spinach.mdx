---
title: "Creamy beetroot black beans with tomato and spinach"
date: "2026-03-20"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-beetroot-black-beans-with-tomato-and-spinach.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '2 beetroot, cooked and chopped'
  - 200g cherry tomatoes
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp tahini
  - 100g silken tofu
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - 3 tbsp water
  - '1 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - 200ml vegetable stock
  - 60g spinach
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped
  - Ground black pepper, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray.
  - Place the beetroot, tomatoes and garlic on a tray and coat with the olive oil. Roast for 20 minutes.
  - Add the roasted beetroot, garlic, tahini, silken tofu, juice of ½ lemon, cumin, salt and water to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - In a large pan over medium heat, add the beetroot sauce. Stir in the black beans and vegetable stock, then simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through and slightly thickened. Stir in the spinach and cook over low heat until wilted.
  - Remove from the heat and stir in the remaining lemon juice and coriander. 
  - Divide between bowls and top with the roasted tomatoes and ground black pepper. Serve with a side of crusty bread, quinoa or roasted sweet potato.
