---
title: Apple and cinnamon porridge
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.251Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/apple-and-cinnamon-porridge.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml plant milk or water
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 1 apple
  - ½ tsp cinnamon
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
directions:
  - >-
    Halve the apple. Cut one half into thin slices and set a side. Chop or grate the
    remaining half.
  - >-
    In a small pan, add the oats, milk, chia seeds and apple. Cook for a
    few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently.
  - >-
    Stir in the cinnamon. Serve topped with apple slices and a drizzle of almond
    butter.
