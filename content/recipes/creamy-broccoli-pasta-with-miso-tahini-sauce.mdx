servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 180g dry pasta
  - '½ broccoli head, chopped'
  - 150g frozen peas
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - '## Sauce'
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - 1 tsp miso paste
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 4 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 150ml water
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray. Roast the broccoli for 10
    mins.
  - Cook the pasta and peas, reserving some pasta water.
  - >-
    To make the sauce, add all the ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth. 
  - Heat the sauce in a large pan over medium heat, stirring frequently
    until slightly thickened. Add a few tbsp of pasta water as needed. Stir in the lemon juice.
  - >-
    Add the pasta and broccoli. Stir until combined and heated through. Season to taste. Serve topped with ground black pepper.
