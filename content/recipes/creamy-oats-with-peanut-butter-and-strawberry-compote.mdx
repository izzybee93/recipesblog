title: "Creamy oats with peanut butter and strawberry compote"
date: "2026-07-14"
categories:
  - breakfast
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/creamy-oats-with-peanut-butter-and-strawberry-compote.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 4 tsp peanut butter
  - 2 tsp strawberry compote (see recipe)
  - 3 tbsp oats
  - ~100ml soya milk
directions:
  - In a small glass, add 2 tsp of peanut butter. Layer 1 tsp of strawberry compote on top. Add the oats.
  - Add the soya milk so that the oats are just covered.
  - Spoon on the remaining layers of strawberry compote and peanut butter on top.
  - Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.
