---
title: "Crispy curried tofu"
date: "2026-05-17"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crispy-curried-tofu.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '280g extra firm tofu, cubed'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tsp maple syrup
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - 1 tsp curry powder
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ¼ tsp ground cumin
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tbsp cornflour
directions:
  - Place the tofu in a shallow dish.
  - 'In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, soy sauce, maple syrup, lime juice, curry powder, turmeric, cumin and garlic powder. Pour over the tofu, cover and transfer to the fridge for 2-12 hours.'
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Drain off any excess marinade, then toss the tofu with the cornflour until lightly coated. Add to the baking tray and bake for 30-35 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy. Alternatively, cook in an air fryer for 14 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.'
  - Serve with rice, salad, roasted veggies or your favourite sauce.
