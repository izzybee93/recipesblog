---
title: "Peanut sauce"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories: ["sauces"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/peanut-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 75ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
  - ½ tbsp harissa paste
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 thumb-size piece of ginger, minced
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - ½ tbsp maple syrup 
  - ½ tbsp rice vinegar, lemon juice or lime juice
  - 50ml water
directions:
  - Whisk together the sauce ingredients in a measuring jug. 
  - Gently heat the sauce in a small pan. Simmer on low heat for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently until the sauce thickens.
