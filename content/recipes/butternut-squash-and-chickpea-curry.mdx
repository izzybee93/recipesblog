title: Butternut squash and chickpea curry
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.265Z'

servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  - '1 large onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 thumb-size piece of ginger, minced'
  - '2 garlic cloves, minced'
  - '15g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves separated'
  - 2 tsp chilli powder
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1½ tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp ground coriander
  - 4 tsp curry powder
  - ½ tsp, sea salt, to taste
  - 400ml vegetable stock
  - 1 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  - '½ large butternut squash, peeled and cut into cubes'
  - '1 bell pepper, chopped'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 100g fresh spinach
  - 120g silken tofu cream (see recipe)
  - 240g basmati rice or quinoa
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the rice or quinoa.
  - Heat the oil in a large pan. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, until softened. 
  - 'Finely chop the coriander stalks. Add the stalks to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the garlic, ginger, chilli powder, turmeric, cumin, ground coriander, curry powder, salt and tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes. Pour in the vegetable stock.'
  - 'Add the butternut squash, peppers and chickpeas. Toss until coated, the simmer for 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the curry is reduced by a third and the butternut squash is cooked.'
  - 'Make the "bright and fresh" flavour silken tofu cream.'
  - Add the spinach and stir through until wilted. Reduce to a low heat and stir in the cream.
  - 'Serve with rice or quinoa, and topped with coriander leaves.'
