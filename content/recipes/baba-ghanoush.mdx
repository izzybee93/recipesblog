---
title: "Baba ghanoush"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: "/images/recipes/baba-ghanoush.jpeg"
servings: "serves 8"
ingredients:
  - '2 medium aubergines, halved'
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 3 tbsp tahini
  - 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  - ¼ tsp cumin
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - '2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped'
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
directions:
  - 'Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Place the aubergines on the tray and brush with 1 tbsp olive oil, then turn them face down. Roast until very soft, about 35 to 40 minutes. Set aside to cool for a few minutes. Scoop out the flesh into a mesh strainer over a mixing bowl. Leave to rest for a few minutes and shake to release the moisture.'
  - 'Discard the water and transfer the aubergine to the bowl. Add the garlic and lemon juice, then mash with a fork. Add the tahini and stir until well combined. Add the olive oil 1 tbsp at a time and continue to mix until creamy.'
  - 'Stir in the parsley, cumin, smoked paprika and salt. Season to taste.'
  - Serve with a sprinkle of smoked paprika on top.
