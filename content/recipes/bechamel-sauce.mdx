  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tbsp plain flour
  - 4 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - '400ml soya milk or oat milk, plus more if needed'
  - Â½ tsp sea salt
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    Heat the oil in a pan at medium to high heat. As the oil heats, add the
    flour and stir or whisk vigorously. Add the nutritional yeast and mix.
  - >-
    Add the soya milk and continue to whisk, and allow the sauce to gradually
    thicken. The sauce will usually reach the desired thickness after it has
    boiled then simmered for a few minutes. Keep in mind that the sauce will
    continue to thicken as it cools.
  - >-
    Remove from the heat and add the desired seasoning, such as salt, black
    pepper and garlic powder.
