title: Crunchy peanut butter cookies
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.281Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/crunchypeanutbuttercookies.jpeg
servings: makes 24
ingredients:
  - 240g natural peanut butter
  - 120g caster sugar
  - 90ml almond milk
  - 2 tsp vanilla extract
  - 120g plain flour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp sea salt (if peanut butter is unsalted)
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line two baking trays with baking paper.
  - 'In a large bowl, add the peanut butter and sugar and mix until creamy.'
  - Stir in the almond milk and vanilla extract.
  - >-
    In a medium bowl, add the flour, baking powder and salt (if needed), and
    stir together. Add the dry ingredients to the wet, and mix to combine, using
    your hands if needed.
  - >-
    Scoop out 1½ tbsp of the dough each time and roll into balls. Place the
    balls of dough about 5cm apart on a baking sheet. Using the back of a fork,
    flatten in a crisscross pattern.
  - >-
    Bake for 12 – 15 minutes (a longer bake will create a crunchier cookie). Let
    cool a few minutes, then transfer to wire rack.
