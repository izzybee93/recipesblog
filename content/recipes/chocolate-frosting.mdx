---
title: Chocolate frosting
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.276Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolate-frosting.jpeg
servings: 1 cake or 12 muffins
ingredients:
  - "150g plant butter"
  - '100g icing sugar, sieved'
  - "40g cocoa powder"
  - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
  - "1-2 tbsp soya milk"
directions:
  - >-
    Using a mixer, beat the butter until light and fluffy. Add the sugar, cocoa
    powder and vanilla. Blend until whipped and creamy. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons
    of milk, as needed, until the desired consistency is reached.
  - >-
    Spread or pipe on top of the cake, and add your favourite toppings to
    decorate.
