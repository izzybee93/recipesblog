---
title: "Carrot and cucumber salad"
date: "2026-04-09"
categories:
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/carrot-and-cucumber-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2-3"
ingredients:
  - 2 medium carrots
  - ½ cucumber
  - Small handful fresh coriander, chopped (optional)
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - ½ tbsp sesame oil
  - 1½ tbsp rice vinegar
  - 1 tsp maple syrup 
  - 2 tsp sesame seeds
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
directions:
  - Cut the carrots and cucumber into ribbons using a vegetable peeler, then place in a bowl. Add the coriander.
  - Combine the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug. Pour over the vegetables and toss to combine. Serve topped with extra sesame seeds.
