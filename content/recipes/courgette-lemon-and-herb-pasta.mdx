title: 'Courgette, lemon and herb pasta'

categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: /images/recipes/courgette-lemon-and-herb-pasta.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 180g pasta
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - '1 large courgette, sliced'
  - '80g sliced artichoke hearts, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp capers
  - Juice and zest of ½ lemon
  - 1½ tsp mixed herbs
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Cook the pasta. Reserve ~100ml of pasta water.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Cook the courgette for 8 minutes until
    slightly browned. Add the artichoke, capers, lemon juice, half the lemon zest, herbs, garlic and salt and cook for another
    few minutes.
  - >-
    Add the pasta and reserved water to the pan and toss to combine. Cook for one more minute. Serve topped with a sprinkle of lemon zest and ground black
    pepper.
