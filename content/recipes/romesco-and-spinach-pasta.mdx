---
title: "Romesco and spinach pasta"
date: "2026-01-20"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/romesco-and-spinach-pasta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 160g pasta
  - 1 large red bell pepper
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 100g silken tofu
  - 6 sun-dried tomatoes
  - 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - ½ tsp smoked paprika
  - Pinch sea salt
  - Pinch chilli flakes
  - 6 roasted almonds, chopped
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 2 tbsp water
  - Small handful fresh parsley, finely chopped
  - ½ tbsp lemon zest
  - 2 large handfuls spinach
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180. Prepare a baking tray. Slice the pepper into a few chunks and brush with ½ tbsp olive oil. Roast for 15 minutes then set aside.
  - In a blender, add the roasted pepper, garlic, tofu, sun-dried tomatoes, smoked paprika, salt, chili flakes, roasted almonds, lemon juice and water. Blend until smooth.
  - Cook the pasta.
  - In a large pan, heat over a medium-low heat and add the sauce. Cook for 10 minutes until slightly thickened. Stir in the parsley and lemon zest. Add the spinach and stir through until wilted.
  - Add the pasta and toss to combine.
