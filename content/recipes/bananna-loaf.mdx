---
title: Bananna loaf
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.256Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/bananaloaf.jpeg
servings: makes 1 cake
ingredients:
  - 2 overripe bananas
  - 80ml vegetable oil
  - 75g soft brown sugar
  - 100ml soya milk
  - 250g self-raising flour
  - 1½ tsp baking powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - "2 tsp\_cinnamon"
  - ½ tsp nutmeg
  - ½ tsp ground ginger
  - 80g chocolate chips (optional)
directions:
  - "Heat oven to 180°C.\_Prepare a loaf tin."
  - >-
    In a large bowl, mash the bananas, then add the oil, soya milk and sugar and
    mix well.
  - "In a separate bowl, combine the\_flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger. Add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients and mix well. Add a splash more soya milk if needed."
  - >-
    Pour the mixture into the loaf tin. Top with chocolate chips if desired.
    Bake for 35 minutes, until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out
    clean.
