---
title: "Quinoa and edamame salad with peanut dressing"
date: "2026-05-16"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/quinoa-and-edamame-salad-with-peanut-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 60g quinoa
  - '¼ small red cabbage, shredded'
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - '1 large carrot, grated'
  - '150g edamame, cooked'
  - 'Small handful coriander, chopped'
  - Peanut dressing (see recipe)
  - '2 tbsp peanuts, chopped and roasted'
directions:
  - Rinse and cook the quinoa.
  - In a large bowl, add the red cabbage and lime juice. Massage briefly, then set aside.
  - Add the quinoa, cucumber, carrot, edamame and coriander to the bowl and toss to combine.
  - Make the peanut dressing. Pour over the salad and toss to combine. Serve topped with roasted peanuts.
