---
title: "Chickpea crackers"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - bread
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chickpea-crackers.jpeg"
servings: "makes ~30"
ingredients:
  - 80g besan flour
  - 40g plain flour
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  - ⅛ tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  - ½ tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - ~60ml water
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  - In a bowl, whisk together the flours, salt, black pepper, garlic and sesame seeds.
  - Add the olive oil and 3 tbsp water. Mix until the dough starts to come together. Add more water, 1 tbsp at a time, until a dough forms.
  - Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface. Roll out as thin as possible, about 2mm thick.
  - Using a pizza cutter, cut the dough into a grid pattern. Transfer each cracker to the baking tray, leaving space between the crackers. Use a fork to prick each cracker 3 times.
  - Bake for 12-14 minutes, until the crackers are golden brown around the edges. Leave to cool completely on the tray. Store in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
