---
title: "Mashed chickpea and pesto sandwich"
date: "2026-03-16"
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/mashed-chickpea-and-pesto-sandwich.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - '80g chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ½ medium avocado
  - 2 tsp basil pesto (see recipe)
  - 2 tsp lemon juice
  - Small handful rocket
  - '1 medium tomato, sliced'
  - '2 slices sourdough, lightly toasted'
directions:
  - In a medium bowl, add the chickpeas, avocado and lemon juice. Mash together until a chunky spread forms. Add the pesto and stir through.
  - Assemble the sandwich with the chickpea filling, tomato and rocket.
