featured_image: "/images/recipes/quinoa-tempeh-salad-with-tahini-dressing.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g tempeh, sliced
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 2 tsp lemon juice
  - 30g quinoa
  - 50g edamame
  - Â¼ small cucumber, chopped
  - Handful cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - Tahini dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - In a shallow dish, add the tempeh, sesame oil, soy sauce and lemon juice. Mix well and leave to marinate in the fridge for 2 hours.
  - Cook the quinoa and edamame.
  - In a small pan over medium-high heat, cook the tempeh for 2 minutes on each side until browned. 
  - Assemble the bowls with quinoa, edamame, tempeh, cucumber and tomatoes. Drizzle with tahini dressing and serve.
