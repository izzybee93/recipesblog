---
title: Chocolate chip rice flour cookies
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.276Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolate-chip-rice-flour-cookies.jpeg
servings: makes 12-16
ingredients:
  - 200g rice flour
  - 120g caster sugar
  - ½ tsp baking powder
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 50ml vegetable oil
  - 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  - 6 tbsp aquafaba
  - 75g dark chocolate chips
directions:
  - Pre-heat the oven to 160°C. Line two baking trays with baking paper.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into a
    bowl and mix well.
  - >-
    In a measuring jug, whisk together the oil, vanilla extract and aquafaba.
    Add to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  - >-
    Using your hands, gather everything together into a dry dough. Add the
    chocolate chips and work them into the dough.
  - >-
    Spoon 6-8 balls of dough onto each prepared baking tray, leaving space
    between each.
  - >-
    Bake for 18-20 minutes until the cookies are light brown on the edges and
    still slightly soft in the middle.
  - Leave to cool for 5 minutes then transfer the cookies to a cooling rack.
