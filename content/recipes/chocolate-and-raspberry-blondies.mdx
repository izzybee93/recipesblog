---
title: "Chocolate and raspberry blondies"
date: "2026-01-05"
categories: ["treats"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-and-raspberry-blondies.jpeg"
servings: "serves 16"
ingredients:
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 50g oat flour 
  - 100g caster sugar
  - 135g almond butter
  - 2 tbsp maple syrup
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 150g raspberries
  - 50g hazelnuts, roughly chopped
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 50g dark chocolate
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Line a 8x8 dish with baking paper.
  - In a food processor, add the chickpeas, sugar, almond butter, maple syrup, vanilla extract and salt. Process until smooth.
  - Add the oat flour and process for another 30 seconds. Stir in the hazelnuts.
  - Spread evenly into the prepared dish and scatter over the raspberries. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden. Leave to cool completely before decorating. 
  - Melt the chocolate in a bain-marie. Drizzle the chocolate over the blondies and leave to set before slicing into squares.
