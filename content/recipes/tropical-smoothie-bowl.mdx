categories:

featured_image: /images/recipes/tropical-smoothie-bowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - '½ frozen banana, chopped'
  - 2 passion fruit
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
  - '1 tbsp chia seeds, soaked in water (optional)'
  - 2 tbsp oats
  - 50ml plant milk
  - '1 kiwi, sliced'
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Blend the banana, passion fruit, almond butter, chia seeds, oats and plant milk until
    smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with kiwi and granola.
