---
title: "Raspberry and peanut butter power porridge"
date: "2026-04-04"
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: "/images/recipes/raspberry-and-peanut-butter-power-porridge.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g oats
  - 250ml non-dairy milk or water
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds
  - 50g raspberries
  - 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter

directions:
  - Cover the chia seeds with water and set aside for at least 20 minutes.
  - In a small pan, add the oats, chia seeds, non-dairy milk and ¾ of the raspberries. Cook for a few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently. 
  - Serve topped with hemp seeds, the remaining raspberries and a drizzle of peanut butter.
