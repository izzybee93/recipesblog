---
title: "Roasted sweet potato, chickpeas and crispy kale"
date: "2025-12-07"
categories: ["mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/roasted-sweet-potato-chickpeas-and-crispy-kale.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 large or 2 medium sweet potatoes
  - 1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 2 tsp ground cumin
  - 1 tsp ground coriander 
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - 125g kale, finely chopped
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  - 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  - 2 tbsp soya or coconut yoghurt
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  - Slice the sweet potato in half lengthwise and place it cut-side down on a tray. Bake on the middle rack of the oven for 45 minutes.
  - After 20 minutes, toss the chickpeas with the cumin, coriander and chilli powder. Bake on the middle rack for the remaining 25 minutes.
  - Toss the kale with the olive oil and nutritional yeast. Spread it out on a separate tray and bake on the top rack for 8 minutes until crispy.
  - Place the sweet potato in serving bowls. Top with the roasted chickpeas, crispy kale, pumpkin seeds and a drizzle of yoghurt.
