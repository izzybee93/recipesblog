---
title: "Pasta e fagioli"
date: "2026-03-22"
categories:
  - mains
  - pasta
featured_image: "/images/recipes/pasta-e-fagioli.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '1 small onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 carrot, finely chopped'
  - '1 celery stick, finely chopped'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 700ml vegetable stock
  - '1 400g can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '1 sprig fresh rosemary, chopped'
  - '¼ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 80g small pasta
  - 'Small handful fresh parsley, finely chopped'
  - 2 large handfuls spinach
directions:
  - "In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, carrot and celery and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Stir in the tomato paste."
  - "Add the chopped tomatoes and vegetable stock and stir well. Add the beans, rosemary, salt and pepper. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 10 minutes. Add the pasta and cook for another 10 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked."
  - "Add the spinach and stir through until wilted. Stir in the parsley and serve topped with extra ground black pepper."
---
