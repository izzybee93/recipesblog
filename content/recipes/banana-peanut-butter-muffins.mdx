---
title: Banana peanut butter muffins
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.255Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/banana-peanut-butter-muffins.jpeg
servings: makes 12
ingredients:
  - 2 overripe bananas
  - 75ml vegetable oil
  - 55g soft brown sugar
  - 120ml non-dairy milk
  - 125g self-raising flour
  - 100g wholemeal flour
  - 2 tsp baking powder
  - "2 tsp\_cinnamon"
  - ¼ tsp nutmeg
  - 6 tsp smooth peanut butter
directions:
  - "Heat oven to 180°C.\_Line a muffin tin with cases."
  - >-
    In a large bowl, mash the bananas, then add the oil, milk and sugar and mix
    well.
  - "In a separate bowl, combine the\_flour, baking powder and cinnamon and nutmeg. Add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients and mix well."
  - >-
    Spoon into the muffin cases to fill each one halfway. Spoon ½ tsp peanut
    butter spread into the centre of each muffin. Cover with the remaining
    mixture. Bake for 20 minutes, until a skewer inserted into the centre comes
    out clean.
