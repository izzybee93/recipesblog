---
title: "Lemon and ginger stir fry sauce"
date: "2026-04-04"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-and-ginger-stir-fry-sauce.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - 3 tbsp ponzu sauce
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - '1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, minced'
  - '1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 125ml water
  - 1 tsp cornflour
directions:
  - In a small jug, mix all the ingredients together except the cornflour. Stir in the cornflour until dissolved.
  - Add to the stir fry and cook on a medium-high heat until the sauce thickens.
---
