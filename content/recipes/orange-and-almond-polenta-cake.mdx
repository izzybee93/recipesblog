---
title: "Orange and almond polenta cake"
date: "2026-04-15"
categories:
  - cakes
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/orange-and-almond-polenta-cake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 10"
ingredients:
  - 140g fine polenta
  - 100g plain flour
  - 120g ground almonds
  - 100g soft brown sugar
  - 90ml vegetable oil
  - 140ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - Zest of 2 oranges
  - Juice of 1 orange
  - 2 tsp baking powder
  - ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - '## Orange syrup'
  - Juice of 1 orange
  - 2 tbsp caster sugar
  - '## Garnish'
  - 1 tbsp icing sugar
  - 'Candied orange peel (optional, see recipe)'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Line a 23cm cake tin.
  - 'In a measuring jug, add the apple cider vinegar and soya milk. Set aside for 5 minutes.'
  - 'In a large bowl, combine the polenta, flour, ground almonds, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt.'
  - 'In a measuring jug, whisk together the oil, sugar, milk, orange zest, orange juice, and vanilla extract. Pour into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.'
  - Pour the batter into the tin and bake for 45-55 minutes until golden and a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  - 'To make the orange syrup, heat the orange juice and sugar in a small pan over medium heat until the sugar is dissolved. While the cake is still warm, prick all over and spoon over the syrup.'
  - Leave the cake to cool for 20 minutes before removing from the tin.
  - 'Make the candied orange peel. When the cake has cooled completely, dust with icing sugar and top with the candied orange peel.'
