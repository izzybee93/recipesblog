---
title: "Lemon and herb pasta salad"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains", "salad"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-and-herb-pasta-salad.jpeg" # TODO - enhance
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g pasta
  - 100g extra-firm tofu, sliced
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Handful cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - ½ small avocado, chopped 
  - Juice ½ lemon
  - 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
directions:
  - Cook the pasta.
  - Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan. Fry the tofu for 5 minutes on each side until browned.
  - To make the dressing, whisk together the remaining olive oil, lemon juice, herbs, chilli flakes and salt.
  - Combine the pasta, tomatoes, tofu and dressing in a bowl and mix well. Add the avocado. Refrigerate and serve chilled.
