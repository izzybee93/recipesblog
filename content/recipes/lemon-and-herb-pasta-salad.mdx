---
title: "Lemon and herb pasta salad"
date: "2026-01-17"
categories: ["mains", "pasta", "salad"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/lemon-and-herb-pasta-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 1"
ingredients:
  - 50g pasta
  - 100g extra-firm tofu, sliced
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 60g cherry tomatoes, chopped
  - ½ small avocado, chopped 
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 1 tsp dried mixed herbs, such as oregano, basil and parsley
  - ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
directions:
  - Cook the pasta.
  - Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan. Fry the tofu for 5 minutes on each side until browned.
  - Whisk together all the dressing ingredients in a small measuring jug.
  - Combine the pasta, tomatoes, tofu and dressing in a bowl and mix well. Add the avocado. Refrigerate and serve chilled.
