---
title: "Rye bread"
date: "2026-03-18"
categories:
  - bread
featured_image: "/images/recipes/rye-bread.jpeg"
servings: "makes 1 loaf"
ingredients:
  - 280g strong white bread flour
  - 180g rye flour
  - 7g instant yeast
  - 80g dark brown sugar
  - 9g sea salt
  - 330ml warm water
  - 30ml vegetable oil
  - 12g cocoa powder
  - 1 tbsp caraway seeds (optional)
directions:
  - In a large bowl, mix the bread flour, rye flour, yeast, salt, cocoa powder and seeds.
  - In a jug, mix the water and sugar until dissolved, then add the oil. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and mix until a sticky dough forms. Cover and leave for 1 hour. Transfer to the fridge overnight, for 8-16 hours.
  - When ready to bake, remove the bowl from the fridge for 30 minutes before shaping. Prepare a loaf tin. Gently fold and shape the dough into a loaf, then place in the tin. Cover with a damp tea towel and leave to rise for 1-2 hours. Preheat the oven to 220°C, for 30 minutes prior to baking.
  - "Bake for 45-55 minutes, until the loaf is well browned and sounds hollow when tapped. Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing."
