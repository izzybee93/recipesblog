servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '1 large aubergine, chopped'
  - '1 large sweet potato, chopped'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ½ tsp ground cumin
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - ¼ tsp ground coriander
  - ½ tsp sea salt
  - 50g baby spinach
  - 'Small handful mint, finely chopped'
  - 'Small handful parsley, finely chopped'
  - 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  - '10 roasted almonds, chopped'
  - Zingy orange dressing (see recipe)
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the aubergine, sweet potato and chickpeas. Drizzle with the olive oil, then add the spices and mix until coated. Bake for 25 minutes.
  - Make the dressing.
  - In a large bowl, add the spinach, herbs, roasted vegetables, pomegranate seeds and almonds. Add the dressing and gently toss to combine. Serve with extra mint leaves for garnish.
