---
title: "Crispy curried tofu salad"
date: "2026-06-26"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crispy-curried-tofu-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '1 lettuce, chopped'
  - '½ cucumber, sliced'
  - '2 medium carrots, peeled into ribbons'
  - 120g green lentils
  - 'Small handful fresh coriander, chopped'
  - Crispy curried tofu (see recipe)
  - '1 ½ tbsp pumpkin seeds, toasted'
  - '## Dressing'
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of ½ lime or lemon
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - Ground black pepper
directions:
  - Make the crispy curried tofu.
  - In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, cucumber, carrot, lentils and coriander.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  - Serve topped with the tofu and toasted pumpkin seeds.
