---
title: "Polenta"
date: "2025-11-19"
categories: ["grains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/polenta.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 950ml water
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 125g polenta
directions:
  - In a large pan, bring the water to a boil. Add the salt.
  - While whisking gently, pour the polenta into the boiling water in a steady stream.
  - Continue whisking over a low heat. Cook for 20-30 minutes until it thickens, whisking frequently.
  - Serve immediately for a creamy polenta, or transfer to a large dish to firm up. Slice the polenta and pan fry for a few minutes on each side, then serve.
---
