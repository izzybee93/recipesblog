---
title: "Chocolate orange bundt cake"
date: "2026-05-05"
categories:
  - cakes
  - treats
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chocolate-orange-bundt-cake.jpeg"
servings: "serves 16"
ingredients:
  - '## Orange cake'
  - 200g self-raising flour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - 60g caster sugar
  - 50ml vegetable oil
  - Zest of 1 orange
  - 100ml orange juice
  - 100ml water
  - '## Chocolate cake'
  - 170g self-raising flour
  - 30g cocoa powder
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - 60g caster sugar
  - 50ml vegetable oil
  - ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  - 100ml water
  - 100ml soya milk
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - '## Topping'
  - '20g dark chocolate, melted'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Grease a bundt tin.
  - 'In two medium bowls, mix together the flour and baking powder for each cake flavour.'
  - 'Set aside 1 tbsp orange zest for decorating. For the orange cake, whisk together the sugar, oil, remaining orange zest, orange juice and water in a small bowl. For the chocolate cake, whisk together the sugar, oil, vinegar, water, milk and vanilla in a small bowl.'
  - Spoon the batter into the bundt tin, alternating each flavour, then gently run a knife through to swirl them together. Bake for 40-45 minutes until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean.
  - Leave to cool in the tin for 20 minutes, then carefully turn out and leave to cool completely before decorating.
  - Drizzle melted chocolate over the top and sprinkle with remaining orange zest. Leave to set before serving.
