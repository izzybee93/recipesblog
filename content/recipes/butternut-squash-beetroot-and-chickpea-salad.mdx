date: "2026-02-21"

featured_image: "/images/recipes/butternut-squash-beetroot-and-chickpea-salad.jpeg"
servings: "serves 3"
ingredients:
  - ½ butternut squash, peeled and chopped
  - 2 medium beetroot, cooked and chopped
  - 1 400g can chickpeas
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp dried oregano
  - 90g rocket or spinach
  - 25g walnuts, chopped
  - 6 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  - '## Dressing'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar 
  - 2 tsp lemon juice 
  - ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a large baking tray. Add the squash, beetroot and chickpeas. Coat with the oil and oregano, and roast for 20-25 mins. Add the walnuts to the tray and roast for another 5 minutes.
  - To make the dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small measuring jug.
  - In a large bowl, add the rocket, roasted veg, chickpeas, walnuts, and pomegranate seeds. Add the dressing and gently toss to combine.
