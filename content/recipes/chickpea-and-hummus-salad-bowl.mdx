---
title: "Chickpea and hummus salad bowl"
date: "2026-03-24"
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: "/images/recipes/chickpea-and-hummus-salad-bowl.jpeg"
servings: "serves 2"
ingredients:
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - '2 carrots, grated'
  - '½ cucumber, chopped'
  - '100g cherry tomatoes, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - '1 tsp mixed herbs, such as oregano, basil and parsley'
  - Pinch sea salt
  - 4 tbsp hummus (see recipe)
directions:
  - "Heat a small pan over medium heat. Add the chickpeas and cook for 5 minutes. Add the smoked paprika and toss to coat. Cook on medium-low heat for 5 more minutes, until slightly crispy."
  - "To make the dressing, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, herbs and salt."
  - "To assemble the bowls, add the carrot, cucumber, tomatoes and chickpeas. Pour over the dressing and top with spoonfuls of hummus."
