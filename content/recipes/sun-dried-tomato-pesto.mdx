---
title: "Sun-dried tomato pesto"
date: "2026-04-06"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/sun-dried-tomato-pesto.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - 75g sun-dried tomatoes
  - 25g basil
  - 15g pine nuts
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 15g nutritional yeast
  - 2 tsp lemon juice
  - '¼ tsp sea salt'
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1-2 tbsp water
directions:
  - In a food processor, add the tomatoes, basil, pine nuts, garlic, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, salt and ground black pepper. Process until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
  - Add the olive oil and process until combined. Add water, 1 tbsp at a time, until the pesto reaches the desired consistency. Store refrigerated for up to 5 days, or freeze in small portions.
