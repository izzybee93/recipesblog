categories:

featured_image: /images/recipes/simple-black-bean-salad.jpeg
servings: serves 2-3
ingredients:
  - '1 400g black beans, drained and rinsed'
  - '1 bell pepper, chopped'
  - '2 large tomatoes, chopped'
  - '¼ cucumber, chopped'
  - '1 avocado, chopped'
  - 75g sweetcorn (optional)
  - Handful coriander chopped
  - Juice of ½ lemon or lime
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - ½ tsp honey or maple syrup
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
directions:
  - Combine the beans, pepper, tomatoes, cucumber, sweetcorn and coriander in medium bowl.
  - >-
    To make the dressing, whisk together the remaining ingredients. Pour over
    the salad and mix again.
  - >-
    Gently stir in the avocado. Serve topped with a garnish of coriander.
