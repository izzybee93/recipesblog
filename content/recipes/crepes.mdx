---
title: "Crêpes"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories:
  - breakfast
  - desserts
  - mains
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crepes.jpeg"
servings: "makes 10"
ingredients:
  - 125g plain flour
  - '260ml soya milk, room temperature'
  - 1 tbsp vegetable oil
directions:
  - In a medium bowl, add all the ingredients and whisk together until a smooth batter forms. Set aside for 5 minutes to thicken.
  - Heat the pan to medium-high heat, then add ~½ tsp of oil. Spoon a ladle of batter into the pan and spread evenly using the back of the ladle. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side. Flip the crêpe when the underside starts to become crispy and the sides loosen. Repeat for the remaining batter.
  - Add your favourite sweet or savoury fillings.
