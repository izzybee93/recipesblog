---
title: "Crêpes"
date: "2025-08-17"
categories: ["breakfast", "mains"]
featured_image: "/images/recipes/crepes.jpeg"
servings: "makes 10"
ingredients:
- 125g plain flour 
- 260ml of soya milk, room temperature 
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
directions:
- In a medium bowl, add all the ingredients and whisk together until a smooth batter forms. Set aside for 15 minutes to thicken.
- Heat the pan to medium-high heat, then add about ½ tsp of oil. Spoon a ladle of batter into the pan and tilt with a circular motion to thin out and spread evenly.
- Cook for around 1-3 minutes on each side. Flip the crêpe when the underside starts to become crispy the sides of the crêpe loosen. Repeat for the remaining batter.
- Add your favourite sweet or savoury toppings and enjoy warm.
