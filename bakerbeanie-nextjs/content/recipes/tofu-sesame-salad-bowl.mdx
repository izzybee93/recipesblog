title: Tofu sesame salad bowl

categories:
  - salad
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/tofusesamesaladbowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '225g extra firm tofu, cubed'
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - '2 large carrots, grated'
  - 100g sugar snap peas
  - '½ medium cucumber, chopped'
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - 2 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - ½ tsp ginger
  - Sesame seeds
directions:
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp sesame oil. Fry the tofu on a medium-high heat
    for a few minutes. Add 2 tbsp soy sauce and continue to cook, turning
    occasionally to brown each side.
  - >-
    Make the dressing by mixing together the remaining sesame oil, soy sauce,
    lime and ginger.
  - >-
    Assemble the salads, drizzle with the dressing, and sprinkle with sesame
    seeds.
