title: Beetroot couscous salad

categories:

  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/beetrootcouscoussalad.jpeg
servings: serves 3
ingredients:
  - 150g dry couscous
  - '2 large beetroots, chopped'
  - '½ cucumber, chopped'
  - '1 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - ~300ml vegetable stock
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - 'Black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the couscous then vegetable stock, pouring until the
    water is
  - 5cm above the couscous. Set aside for 10 mins.
  - >-
    Using a fork, loosen the couscous. Add the garlic, lemon juice, leftover
    beetroot juice and oil and mix well. Add the beetroot, cucumber and
    chickpeas, then mix together. Add a drizzle more olive oil, season with
    black pepper and stir again.
