title: Curried tofu salad

servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - '2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped'
  - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  - '1 pepper, chopped'
  - '2 courgettes, chopped'
  - '280g extra firm tofu, chopped'
  - ½ tbsp tomato paste
  - 2 heaped tsp curry powder
  - ½ tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 100g rocket
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 'Sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare 2 baking trays.
  - >-
    Place the sweet potatoes on a tray and brush with 1 tbsp oil. Roast for 20
    minutes until softened.
  - >-
    To make the curry paste, in a small bowl, add the tomato paste, curry
    powder, apple cider vinegar and vegetable oil and mix well. Toss the tofu in
    the curry paste until coated, then place on a baking tray.
  - >-
    Place the courgette and pepper on the tray and brush the remaining curry
    paste. Add more oil if needed. Roast for 15 minutes.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the rocket, roasted veggies and tofu. Drizzle with
    olive oil, season to taste, gently toss together and serve.
