---
title: "Fresh tomato salsa"
date: "2026-03-02"
categories:
  - sauces
featured_image: "/images/recipes/fresh-tomato-salsa.jpeg"
servings: "serves 4"
ingredients:
  - '¼ onion, rinsed'
  - 1 small garlic clove
  - '4 plum tomatoes, chopped'
  - Small handful fresh coriander
  - Juice and zest of 1 lime
  - '½ tsp chilli flakes, to taste'
  - ¼ tsp ground cumin
  - '½ tsp sea salt, to taste'
  - 1/8 tsp sugar
directions:
  - In a food processor, combine the onion and garlic. Pulse until well chopped.
  - Add the tomatoes, chilli, coriander, lime juice, lime zest, salt, cumin, and sugar. Pulse until a chunky mixture forms.
  - If the consistency is too watery, strain half to remove some liquid. Combine the strained mixture with the remaining salsa. Serve with tortillas, or as a topping for tacos and fajitas.
---
