---
title: Chocolate mousse
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.276Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/chocolatemousse.jpeg
servings: serves 4
ingredients:
  - Aquafaba from 1 400g can chickpeas
  - '85g dark chocolate, melted'
  - 2 tbsp caster sugar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
directions:
  - >-
    In a medium bowl, beat the aquafaba with a mixer until stiff peaks form
    (about 10 minutes). Gradually add the sugar and continue to beat. Allow the
    melted chocolate to cool a little.
  - >-
    Gently fold in the chocolate and vanilla.  Divide between 4 glasses and
    refridgerate overnight.
