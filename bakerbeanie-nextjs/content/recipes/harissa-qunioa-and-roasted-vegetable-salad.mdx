title: Harissa qunioa and roasted vegetable salad
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.285Z'
categories:
  - mains
  - salad
featured_image: /images/recipes/harissaquinoavegetablesalad.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 80g quinoa
  - 1 400g can black beans
  - '2 bell peppers, chopped'
  - '½ broccoli, chopped'
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  - 2 tsp harissa paste
  - 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
directions:
  - 'Cook the quinoa for 15-20 minutes, until softened with a slight crunch.'
  - >-
    Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 180°C. Roast the peppers and broccoli for
    10-15 mins.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, add the quinoa, turmeric, paprika, salt, harissa paste and
    olive oil, and mix well.
  - Stir in the black beans and roasted vegetables. Serve warm or cold.
