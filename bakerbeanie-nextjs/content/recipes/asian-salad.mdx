servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp sesame oil
  - 300g beansprouts
  - '2 large carrots, grated'
  - '½ cucumber, chopped'
  - '100g edamame, cooked'
  - 4 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - '¼ tsp garlic granules or 1 garlic clove, minced'
  - 1 tsp ground ginger
  - ½ tsp chilli powder
  - 'Handful of fresh coriander, chopped'
  - 1 tbsp sesame seeds
directions:
  - >-
    Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil in a medium pan. Cook the beansprouts on medium heat
    for 5-10 minutes.
  - >-
    To make the dressing, mix the sesame oil, soy sauce, lime juice, ginger,
    garlic, chilli and coriander.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, mix the beansprouts, carrot, cucumber and edamame. Pour
    over the dressing and mix well. Top with sesame seeds.
