ingredients:
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 1 onion finely chopped
  - '2 cloves garlic, minced'
  - 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 500ml vegetable stock
  - 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  - Large handful basil
  - 200g spinach
  - '1 400g can green or brown lentils, drained and rinsed'
  - 400g gnocchi
  - 'Ground black pepper, to taste'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  - >-
    Heat the oil in a large oven safe pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and
    cook  for 5 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another
    minute.
  - >-
    Stir in the tomato paste, tomatoes, balsamic vinegar and vegetable stock.
    Bring to the boil and then stir through the basil and spinach until wilted.
  - >-
    Mix in the lentils and gnocchi, season, then bake for 25 minutes. Serve
    topped with ground black pepper.
