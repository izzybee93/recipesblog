categories:

featured_image: /images/recipes/peanutsataydressing.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - ½ tbsp smooth peanut butter
  - Juice of ½ lime
  - ½ tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tsp honey
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - ¼ tsp chilli powder
  - 30ml soya milk
  - 'Small handful coriander, finely chopped (optional)'
directions:
  - >-
    Add all ingredients to a small measuring jug and whisk together. Serve with
    a rainbow salad or heat through to make a stir fry sauce.
