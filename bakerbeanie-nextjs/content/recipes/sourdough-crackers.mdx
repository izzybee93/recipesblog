---
title: Sourdough crackers
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.307Z'
categories:
  - snacks
featured_image: /images/recipes/sourdoughcrackers.jpeg
servings: makes 12
ingredients:
  - 200g discarded sourdough starter
  - 1½ tbsp olive oil
  - ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  - 2 tsp dried herbs (Italian style)
  - ¼ tsp sea salt for sprinkling
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 170°C and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  - >-
    In a medium bowl, add the sourdough discard, oil, dried herbs and salt and
    mix thoroughly until well combined. Use a spatula to spread the mixture in a
    thin, even layer onto the parchment paper. Sprinkle the top with salt.
  - >-
    Bake for 10 minutes. Remove from oven and score the crackers with a pizza
    cutter. Bake for an additional 15-30 minutes or until the crackers are
    golden brown. Let cool completely before breaking into squares.
