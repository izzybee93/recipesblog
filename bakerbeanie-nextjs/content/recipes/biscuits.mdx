---
title: Biscuits
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.261Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/biscuits.jpeg
servings: makes 20-30
ingredients:
  - 110g vegan butter
  - 150g caster sugar
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract or maple syrup
  - 250g plain flour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  - 2 tbsp soya milk
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line two large baking trays with baking paper.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together, then add the vanilla
    extract and mix.
  - >-
    In a medium bowl, add the flour, baking powder and salt and mix. Add the dry
    ingredients to the wet and mix until crumbly. Add the milk and mix in until
    a dough forms, adding more milk if needed.
  - >-
    Transfer the dough to a floured surface. Roll out the dough to around 1cm
    thick, and cut out the cookies using a cookie cutter, then place on the
    tray.
  - >-
    Bake in the oven for 10 minutes until golden. Allow to cool on a wire rack
    before decorating with icing or melted chocolate, or simply enjoy on their
    own.
