---
title: Peanut butter cups
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.297Z'
categories:
  - treats
featured_image: /images/recipes/peanutbuttercups.jpeg
servings: makes 12
ingredients:
  - 175g dark chocolate
  - 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  - 60g natural peanut butter
directions:
  - Line a tray with 12 cupcake liners.
  - >-
    Place the chocolate in a glass bowl, and melt over a pan of boiling water.
    Stir in the coconut oil.
  - >-
    Spoon a little bit of melted chocolate into each cup, spreading a thin layer
    up the edges of the liners. This will ensure the peanut butter is encased in
    the chocolate. Transfer to the fridge for 5-10 minutes to set slightly.
  - >-
    Remove from the fridge and divide the peanut butter equally, spooning it
    into the centre of each cup. Top with the remaining chocolate to cover.
  - Transfer to the fridge for 1 hour to set.
