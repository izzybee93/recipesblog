title: Chickpea and edamame rice bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.271Z'
categories:
  - mains
featured_image: /images/recipes/chickpeaedamamericebowl.jpeg
servings: serves 2
ingredients:
  - 100g rice
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - '½ onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 clove garlic, minced'
  - 'Small piece of ginger, minced'
  - 1 tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp cumin
  - ¼ tsp sea salt
  - '⅔ 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed'
  - 120g edamame beans
  - Juice of ½ lemon
  - 100g spinach
  - 1 tbsp tahini
  - 100ml soya milk
  - 'Handful fresh coriander, chopped'
directions:
  - Cook the rice.
  - >-
    In a large pan, heat the olive oil. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes
    until softened. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another 2 minutes.
    Add the turmeric, cumin and sea salt, then cook for another minute.
  - >-
    Stir in the chickpeas, edamame beans, lemon juice, spinach, tahini and soya
    milk. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring often, the spinach has wilted. Store
    in the coriander. Serve with the rice.
