title: Chocolate orange cookies

servings: makes 16
ingredients:
  - 205g self-raising flour
  - 55g cocoa powder
  - Zest of 1 large orange
  - 200g caster sugar
  - 150g dairy-free butter
  - 3 tbsp aquafaba
  - Splash of freshly squeezed orange juice
  - '150g dark chocolate, chopped into chunks'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and line two large baking trays.
  - >-
    In a medium bowl, sift the self-raising flour and cocoa powder, then set
    aside.
  - >-
    In a large bowl, rub the orange zest and sugar together using your fingers.
    Cream the orange sugar and the butter together until pale, then add the
    aquafaba.
  - >-
    Fold the dry ingredients into the mixture until combined. Add a splash of
    freshly squeezed orange juice to help the mixture come together.
  - >-
    Add the chocolate to the dough and mix until the chocolate is evenly
    dispersed.
  - >-
    Take a heaped tbsp of dough, shape into a ball, and place on a tray, then
    flatten. Make sure to space out the cookies, 8 per tray.
  - >-
    Bake for 15-18 minutes. They will look undercooked when they come out of the
    oven, but will harden as they cool.
