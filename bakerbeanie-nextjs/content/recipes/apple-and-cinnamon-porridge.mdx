directions:
  - >-
    Halve the apple. Cut one half into thin slices and set a side. Chop the
    remaining half into small chunks.
  - >-
    In a small pan, add the oats, non-dairy milk and chopped apple. Cook for a
    few minutes on a medium heat, stirring frequently.
  - >-
    Stir in the cinnamon. Serve topped with apple and a drizzle of almond
    butter.
