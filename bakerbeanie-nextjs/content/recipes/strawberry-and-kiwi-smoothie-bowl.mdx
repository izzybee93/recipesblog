---
title: Strawberry and kiwi smoothie bowl
date: '2025-08-16T15:27:07.310Z'
categories:
  - breakfast
featured_image: /images/recipes/strawberrykiwismoothiebowl.jpeg
servings: serves 1
ingredients:
  - 50g strawberries
  - 1 kiwi
  - 1 tbsp almond butter
  - 4 tbsp oats
  - Squeeze of lime juice
  - 75ml soya milk
  - 1 tbsp granola
directions:
  - >-
    Set aside a few slices of strawberry and kiwi for the topping. Blend the
    remaining strawberries, kiwi, almond butter, oats, lime juice and soya milk
    until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and top with strawberry, kiwi and granola.
