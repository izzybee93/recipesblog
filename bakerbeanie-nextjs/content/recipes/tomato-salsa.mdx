title: Tomato salsa

categories:

featured_image: /images/recipes/tomatosalsa.png
servings: serves 6
ingredients:
  - 6 plum tomatoes
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ½ red onion
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 1 tsp chilli powder (or 1 jalapeño)
  - '15 sprigs fresh coriander, rinsed'
  - 'Sea salt, to taste'
directions:
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C. Prepare a baking tray with baking paper.
  - >-
    Place the tomatoes on the baking tray and brush with olive oil. Roast for 20
    minutes or until they start to pop.
  - Roughly slice the onion and place in a bowl of cold water for 5 minutes.
  - >-
    In a food processor, add the onion, garlic, tomatoes, chilli, salt and
    coriander.
  - Blend until almost smooth. Season to taste.
  - 'Serve warm or cold, with tortillas, tacos, fajitas and more.'
